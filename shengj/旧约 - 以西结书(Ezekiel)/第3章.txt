</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你奉差遣不是往那说话深奥，言语难懂的民那里去，乃是往以色列家去。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou art not sent to a people of a strange speech and of an hard language, but to the house of Israel;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不是往那说话深奥，言语难懂的多国去，他们的话语是你不懂得的。我若差你往他们那里去，他们必听从你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Not to many people of a strange speech and of an hard language, whose words thou canst not understand. Surely, had I sent thee to them, they would have hearkened unto thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以色列家却不肯听从你，因为他们不肯听从我。原来以色列全家是额坚心硬的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But the house of Israel will not hearken unto thee; for they will not hearken unto me: for all the house of Israel are impudent and hardhearted.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看哪，我使你的脸硬过他们的脸，使你的额硬过他们的额。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, I have made thy face strong against their faces, and thy forehead strong against their foreheads.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我使你的额像金钢钻，比火石更硬。他们虽是悖逆之家，你不要怕他们，也不要因他们的脸色惊惶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As an adamant harder than flint have I made thy forehead: fear them not, neither be dismayed at their looks, though they be a rebellious house.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他又对我说，人子阿，我对你所说的一切话，要心里领会，耳中听闻。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Moreover he said unto me, Son of man, all my words that I shall speak unto thee receive in thine heart, and hear with thine ears.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And go, get thee to them of the captivity, unto the children of thy people, and speak unto them, and tell them, Thus saith the Lord GOD; whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I heard also the noise of the wings of the living creatures that touched one another, and the noise of the wheels over against them, and a noise of a great rushing.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	<tr>

</table>

</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Son of man, I have made thee a watchman unto the house of Israel: therefore hear the word at my mouth, and give them warning from me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我何时指着恶人说，他必要死。你若不警戒他，也不劝戒他，使他离开恶行，拯救他的性命，这恶人必死在罪孽之中。我却要向你讨他丧命的罪（原文作血）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When I say unto the wicked, Thou shalt surely die; and thou givest him not warning, nor speakest to warn the wicked from his wicked way, to save his life; the same wicked man shall die in his iniquity; but his blood will I require at thine hand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>倘若你警戒恶人，他仍不转离罪恶，也不离开恶行，他必死在罪孽之中，你却救自己脱离了罪。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yet if thou warn the wicked, and he turn not from his wickedness, nor from his wicked way, he shall die in his iniquity; but thou hast delivered thy soul.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Again, When a righteous man doth turn from his righteousness, and commit iniquity, and I lay a stumbling-block before him, he shall die: because thou hast not given him warning, he shall die in his sin, and his righteousness which he hath done shall not be remembered; but his blood will I require at thine hand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Nevertheless if thou warn the righteous man, that the righteous sin not, and he doth not sin, he shall surely live, because he is warned; also thou hast delivered thy soul.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
