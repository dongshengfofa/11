<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以西结书(Ezekiel) 第2章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他对我说，人子阿，你站起来，我要和你说话。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he said unto me, Son of man, stand upon thy feet, and I will speak unto thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他对我说话的时候，灵就进入我里面，使我站起来，我便听见那位对我说话的声音。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the spirit entered into me when he spake unto me, and set me upon my feet, that I heard him that spake unto me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他对我说，人子阿，我差你往悖逆的国民以色列人那里去。他们是悖逆我的，他们和他们的列祖违背我，直到今日。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he said unto me, Son of man, I send thee to the children of Israel, to a rebellious nation that hath rebelled against me: they and their fathers have transgressed against me, even unto this very day.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这众子面无羞耻，心里刚硬。我差你往他们那里去，你要对他们说，主耶和华如此说。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For they are impudent children and stiffhearted. I do send thee unto them; and thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith the Lord GOD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们或听，或不听，（他们是悖逆之家），必知道在他们中间有了先知。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they, whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear, (for they are a rebellious house,) yet shall know that there hath been a prophet among them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人子阿，虽有荆棘和蒺藜在你那里，你又住在蝎子中间，总不要怕他们，也不要怕他们的话。他们虽是悖逆之家，还不要怕他们的话，也不要因他们的脸色惊惶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And thou, son of man, be not afraid of them, neither be afraid of their words, though briers and thorns be with thee, and thou dost dwell among scorpions: be not afraid of their words, nor be dismayed at their looks, though they be a rebellious house.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们或听，或不听，你只管将我的话告诉他们。他们是极其悖逆的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And thou shalt speak my words unto them, whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear: for they are most rebellious.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人子阿，要听我对你所说的话，不要悖逆像那悖逆之家，你要开口吃我所赐给你的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But thou, son of man, hear what I say unto thee; Be not thou rebellious like that rebellious house: open thy mouth, and eat that I give thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我观看，见有一只手向我伸出来，手中有一书卷。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And when I looked, behold, an hand was sent unto me; and, lo, a roll of a book was therein;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他将书卷在我面前晒开，内外都写着字，其上所写的有哀号，叹息，悲痛的话。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he spread it before me; and it was written within and without: and there was written therein lamentations, and mourning, and woe.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
