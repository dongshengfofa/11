	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人子阿，葡萄树比别样树有什么强处。葡萄枝比众树枝有什么好处。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Son of man, What is the vine tree more than any tree, or than a branch which is among the trees of the forest?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>其上可以取木料作什么工用，可以取来作钉子挂什么器皿吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Shall wood be taken thereof to do any work? or will men take a pin of it to hang any vessel thereon?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看哪，已经抛在火中当作柴烧，火既烧了两头，中间也被烧了，还有益于工用吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, it is cast into the fire for fuel; the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the midst of it is burned. Is it meet for any work?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>完全的时候尚且不合乎什么工用，何况被火烧坏，还能合乎什么工用吗。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, when it was whole, it was meet for no work: how much less shall it be meet yet for any work, when the fire hath devoured it, and it is burned?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以，主耶和华如此说，众树以内的葡萄树，我怎样使它在火中当柴，也必照样待耶路撒冷的居民。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; As the vine tree among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so will I give the inhabitants of Jerusalem.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必向他们变脸。他们虽从火中出来，火却要烧灭他们。我向他们变脸的时候，你们就知道我是耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will set my face against them; they shall go out from one fire, and another fire shall devour them; and ye shall know that I am the LORD, when I set my face against them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>我必使地土荒凉，因为他们行事干犯我。这是主耶和华说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, saith the Lord GOD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
