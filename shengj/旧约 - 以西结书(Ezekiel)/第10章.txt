<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以西结书(Ezekiel) 第10章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我观看，见基路伯头上的穹苍之中，显出蓝宝石的形状，彷佛宝座的形像。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then I looked, and, behold, in the firmament that was above the head of the cherubims there appeared over them as it were a sapphire stone, as the appearance of the likeness of a throne.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主对那穿细麻衣的人说，你进去，在旋转的轮内基路伯以下，从基路伯中间将火炭取满两手，撒在城上。我就见他进去。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he spake unto the man clothed with linen, and said, Go in between the wheels, even under the cherub, and fill thine hand with coals of fire from between the cherubims, and scatter them over the city. And he went in in my sight.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那人进去的时候，基路伯站在殿的右边，云彩充满了内院。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now the cherubims stood on the right side of the house, when the man went in; and the cloud filled the inner court.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的荣耀从基路伯那里上升，停在门槛以上。殿内满了云彩，院宇也被耶和华荣耀的光辉充满。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then the glory of the LORD went up from the cherub, and stood over the threshold of the house; and the house was filled with the cloud, and the court was full of the brightness of the LORD's glory.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>基路伯翅膀的响声听到外院，好像全能神说话的声音。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the sound of the cherubims' wings was heard even to the outer court, as the voice of the Almighty God when he speaketh.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他吩咐那穿细麻衣的人说，要从旋转的轮内基路伯中间取火。那人就进去站在一个轮子旁边。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it came to pass, that when he had commanded the man clothed with linen, saying, Take fire from between the wheels, from between the cherubims; then he went in, and stood beside the wheels.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有一个基路伯从基路伯中伸手到基路伯中间的火那里，取些放在那穿细麻衣的人两手中，那人就拿出去了。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And one cherub stretched forth his hand from between the cherubims unto the fire that was between the cherubims, and took thereof, and put it into the hands of him that was clothed with linen: who took it, and went out.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在基路伯翅膀之下，显出有人手的样式。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And there appeared in the cherubims the form of a man's hand under their wings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我又观看，见基路伯旁边有四个轮子。这基路伯旁有一个轮子，那基路伯旁有一个轮子，每基路伯都是如此。轮子的颜色（原文作形状）彷佛水苍玉。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And when I looked, behold the four wheels by the cherubims, one wheel by one cherub, and another wheel by another cherub: and the appearance of the wheels was as the colour of a beryl stone.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>至于四轮的形状，都是一个样式，彷佛轮中套轮。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And as for their appearances, they four had one likeness, as if a wheel had been in the midst of a wheel.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>轮行走的时候，向四方都能直行，并不掉转。头向何方，他们也随向何方，行走的时候并不掉转。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When they went, they went upon their four sides; they turned not as they went, but to the place whither the head looked they followed it; they turned not as they went.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	<tr>
