<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以西结书(Ezekiel) 第29章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>第十年十月十二日，耶和华的话临到我说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the tenth year, in the tenth month, in the twelfth day of the month, the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人子阿，你要向埃及王法老预言攻击他和埃及全地，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Son of man, set thy face against Pharaoh king of Egypt, and prophesy against him, and against all Egypt:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说主耶和华如此说，埃及王法老阿，我与你这卧在自己河中的大鱼为敌。你曾说，这河是我的，是我为自己造的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Speak, and say, Thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I am against thee, Pharaoh king of Egypt, the great dragon that lieth in the midst of his rivers, which hath said, My river is mine own, and I have made it for myself.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我耶和华必用钩子钩住你的腮颊，又使江河中的鱼贴住你的鳞甲。我必将你和所有贴住你鳞甲的鱼，从江河中拉上来，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But I will put hooks in thy jaws, and I will cause the fish of thy rivers to stick unto thy scales, and I will bring thee up out of the midst of thy rivers, and all the fish of thy rivers shall stick unto thy scales.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>把你并江河中的鱼都抛在旷野。你必倒在田间，不被收殓，不被掩埋。我已将你给地上野兽，空中飞鸟作食物。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will leave thee thrown into the wilderness, thee and all the fish of thy rivers: thou shalt fall upon the open fields; thou shalt not be brought together, nor gathered: I have given thee for meat to the beasts of the field and to the fowls of the heaven.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>埃及一切的居民，因向以色列家成了芦苇的杖，就知道我是耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And all the inhabitants of Egypt shall know that I am the LORD, because they have been a staff of reed to the house of Israel.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们用手持住你，你就断折，伤了他们的肩。他们倚靠你，你就断折，闪了他们的腰。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When they took hold of thee by thy hand, thou didst break, and rend all their shoulder: and when they leaned upon thee, thou brakest, and madest all their loins to be at a stand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以主耶和华如此说，我必使刀剑临到你，从你中间将人与牲畜剪除。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I will bring a sword upon thee, and cut off man and beast out of thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>埃及地必荒废凄凉，他们就知道我是耶和华。因为法老说，这河是我的，是我所造的，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the land of Egypt shall be desolate and waste; and they shall know that I am the LORD: because he hath said, The river is mine, and I have made it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以我必与你并你的江河为敌，使埃及地，从色弗尼塔直到古实境界，全然荒废凄凉。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, therefore I am against thee, and against thy rivers, and I will make the land of Egypt utterly waste and desolate, from the tower of Syene even unto the border of Ethiopia.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人的脚，兽的蹄都不经过，四十年之久并无人居住。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>No foot of man shall pass through it, nor foot of beast shall pass through it, neither shall it be inhabited forty years.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必使埃及地在荒凉的国中成为荒凉，使埃及城在荒废的城中变为荒废，共有四十年。我必将埃及人分散在列国，四散在列邦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will make the land of Egypt desolate in the midst of the countries that are desolate, and her cities among the cities that are laid waste shall be desolate forty years: and I will scatter the Egyptians among the nations, and will disperse them through the countries.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主耶和华如此说，满了四十年，我必招聚分散在各国民中的埃及人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yet thus saith the Lord GOD; At the end of forty years will I gather the Egyptians from the people whither they were scattered:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必叫埃及被掳的人回来，使他们归回本地巴忒罗。在那里必成为低微的国，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will bring again the captivity of Egypt, and will cause them to return into the land of Pathros, into the land of their habitation; and they shall be there a base kingdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>必为列国中最低微的，也不再自高于列国之上。我必减少他们，以致不再辖制列国。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It shall be the basest of the kingdoms; neither shall it exalt itself any more above the nations: for I will diminish them, that they shall no more rule over the nations.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>埃及必不再作以色列家所倚靠的。以色列家仰望埃及人的时候，便思念罪孽。他们就知道我是主耶和华。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it shall be no more the confidence of the house of Israel, which bringeth their iniquity to remembrance, when they shall look after them: but they shall know that I am the Lord GOD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人子阿，巴比伦王尼布甲尼撒使他的军兵大大效劳，攻打推罗，以致头都光秃，肩都磨破。然而他和他的军兵攻打推罗，并没有从那里得什么酬劳。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Son of man, Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon caused his army to serve a great service against Tyrus: every head was made bald, and every shoulder was peeled: yet had he no wages, nor his army, for Tyrus, for the service that he had served against it:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以主耶和华如此说，我必将埃及地赐给巴比伦王尼布甲尼撒。他必掳掠埃及群众，抢其中的财为掳物，夺其中的货为掠物，这就可以作他军兵的酬劳。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I will give the land of Egypt unto Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon; and he shall take her multitude, and take her spoil, and take her prey; and it shall be the wages for his army.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>我将埃及地赐给他，酬他所效的劳，因王与军兵是为我勤劳。这是主耶和华说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have given him the land of Egypt for his labour wherewith he served against it, because they wrought for me, saith the Lord GOD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
