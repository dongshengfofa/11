<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以西结书(Ezekiel) 第11章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>灵将我举起，带到耶和华殿向东的东门。谁知，在门口有二十五个人，我见其中有民间的首领押朔的儿子雅撒尼亚和比拿雅的儿子毗拉提。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Moreover the spirit lifted me up, and brought me unto the east gate of the LORD's house, which looketh eastward: and behold at the door of the gate five and twenty men; among whom I saw Jaazaniah the son of Azur, and Pelatiah the son of Benaiah, princes of the people.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华对我说，人子阿，这就是图谋罪孽的人，在这城中给人设恶谋。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then said he unto me, Son of man, these are the men that devise mischief, and give wicked counsel in this city:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们说，盖房屋的时候尚未临近。这城是锅，我们是肉。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which say, It is not near; let us build houses: this city is the caldron, and we be the flesh.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人子阿，因此你当说预言，说预言攻击他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore prophesy against them, prophesy, O son of man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的灵降在我身上，对我说，你当说，耶和华如此说，以色列家阿，你们口中所说的，心里所想的，我都知道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the Spirit of the LORD fell upon me, and said unto me, Speak; Thus saith the LORD; Thus have ye said, O house of Israel: for I know the things that come into your mind, every one of them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们在这城中杀人增多，使被杀的人充满街道。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ye have multiplied your slain in this city, and ye have filled the streets thereof with the slain.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以主耶和华如此说，你们杀在城中的人就是肉，这城就是锅。你们却要从其中被带出去。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Your slain whom ye have laid in the midst of it, they are the flesh, and this city is the caldron: but I will bring you forth out of the midst of it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们怕刀剑，我必使刀剑临到你们。这是主耶和华说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ye have feared the sword; and I will bring a sword upon you, saith the Lord GOD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必从这城中带出你们去，交在外邦人的手中，且要在你们中间施行审判。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will bring you out of the midst thereof, and deliver you into the hands of strangers, and will execute judgments among you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们必倒在刀下。我必在以色列的境界审判你们，你们就知道我是耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ye shall fall by the sword; I will judge you in the border of Israel; and ye shall know that I am the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这城必不作你们的锅，你们也不作其中的肉。我必在以色列的境界审判你们，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>This city shall not be your caldron, neither shall ye be the flesh in the midst thereof; but I will judge you in the border of Israel:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们就知道我是耶和华。因为你们没有遵行我的律例，也没有顺从我的典章，却随从你们四围列国的恶规。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And ye shall know that I am the LORD: for ye have not walked in my statutes, neither executed my judgments, but have done after the manners of the heathen that are round about you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我正说预言的时候，比拿雅的儿子毗拉提死了。于是我俯伏在地，大声呼叫说，哎。主耶和华阿，你要将以色列剩下的人灭绝净尽吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it came to pass, when I prophesied, that Pelatiah the son of Benaiah died. Then fell I down upon my face, and cried with a loud voice, and said, Ah Lord GOD! wilt thou make a full end of the remnant of Israel?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人子阿，耶路撒冷的居民对你的弟兄，你的本族，你的亲属，以色列全家，就是对大众说，你们远离耶和华吧。这地是赐给我们为业的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Son of man, thy brethren, even thy brethren, the men of thy kindred, and all the house of Israel wholly, are they unto whom the inhabitants of Jerusalem have said, Get you far from the LORD: unto us is this land given in possession.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以你当说，耶和华如此说，我虽将以色列全家远远迁移到列国中，将他们分散在列邦内，我还要在他们所到的列邦，暂作他们的圣所。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore say, Thus saith the Lord GOD; Although I have cast them far off among the heathen, and although I have scattered them among the countries, yet will I be to them as a little sanctuary in the countries where they shall come.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>你当说，主耶和华如此说，我必从万民中招聚你们，从分散的列国内聚集你们，又要将以色列地赐给你们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore say, Thus saith the Lord GOD; I will even gather you from the people, and assemble you out of the countries where ye have been scattered, and I will give you the land of Israel.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>
