<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以西结书(Ezekiel) 第41章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他带我到殿那里量墙柱，这面厚六肘，那面厚六肘，宽窄与会幕相同。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Afterward he brought me to the temple, and measured the posts, six cubits broad on the one side, and six cubits broad on the other side, which was the breadth of the tabernacle.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>门口宽十肘。门两旁，这边五肘，那边五肘。他量殿长四十肘，宽二十肘。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the breadth of the door was ten cubits; and the sides of the door were five cubits on the one side, and five cubits on the other side: and he measured the length thereof, forty cubits: and the breadth, twenty cubits.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他到内殿量墙柱，各厚二肘。门口宽六肘，门两旁各宽七肘。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then went he inward, and measured the post of the door, two cubits; and the door, six cubits; and the breadth of the door, seven cubits.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他量内殿，长二十肘，宽二十肘。他对我说，这是至圣所。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So he measured the length thereof, twenty cubits; and the breadth, twenty cubits, before the temple: and he said unto me, This is the most holy place.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他又量殿墙，厚六肘。围着殿有旁屋，各宽四肘。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>After he measured the wall of the house, six cubits; and the breadth of every side chamber, four cubits, round about the house on every side.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>旁屋有三层，层叠而上，每层排列三十间。旁屋的梁木搁在殿墙坎上，免得插入殿墙。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the side chambers were three, one over another, and thirty in order; and they entered into the wall which was of the house for the side chambers round about, that they might have hold, but they had not hold in the wall of the house.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这围殿的旁屋越高越宽。因旁屋围殿悬叠而上，所以越上越宽，从下一层，由中一层，到上一层。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And there was an enlarging, and a winding about still upward to the side chambers: for the winding about of the house went still upward round about the house: therefore the breadth of the house was still upward, and so increased from the lowest chamber to the highest by the midst.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I saw also the height of the house round about: the foundations of the side chambers were a full reed of six great cubits.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The thickness of the wall, which was for the side chamber without, was five cubits: and that which was left was the place of the side chambers that were within.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

</table>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>
