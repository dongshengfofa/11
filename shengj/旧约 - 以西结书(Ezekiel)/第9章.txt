<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以西结书(Ezekiel) 第9章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他向我耳中大声喊叫说，要使那监管这城的人手中各拿灭命的兵器前来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He cried also in mine ears with a loud voice, saying, Cause them that have charge over the city to draw near, even every man with his destroying weapon in his hand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>忽然有六个人从朝北的上门而来，各人手拿杀人的兵器。内中有一人身穿细麻衣，腰间带着墨盒子。他们进来，站在铜祭坛旁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And, behold, six men came from the way of the higher gate, which lieth toward the north, and every man a slaughter weapon in his hand; and one man among them was clothed with linen, with a writer's inkhorn by his side: and they went in, and stood beside the brasen altar.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以色列神的荣耀本在基路伯上，现今从那里升到殿的门槛。神将那身穿细麻衣，腰间带着墨盒子的人召来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the glory of the God of Israel was gone up from the cherub, whereupon he was, to the threshold of the house. And he called to the man clothed with linen, which had the writer's inkhorn by his side;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华对他说，你去走遍耶路撒冷全城，那些因城中所行可憎之事叹息哀哭的人，画记号在额上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the LORD said unto him, Go through the midst of the city, through the midst of Jerusalem, and set a mark upon the foreheads of the men that sigh and that cry for all the abominations that be done in the midst thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我耳中听见他对其馀的人说，要跟随他走遍全城，以行击杀。你们的眼不要顾惜，也不要可怜他们。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And to the others he said in mine hearing, Go ye after him through the city, and smite: let not your eye spare, neither have ye pity:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要将年老的，年少的，并处女，婴孩，和妇女，从圣所起全都杀尽，只是凡有记号的人不要挨近他。于是他们从殿前的长老杀起。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Slay utterly old and young, both maids, and little children, and women: but come not near any man upon whom is the mark; and begin at my sanctuary. Then they began at the ancient men which were before the house.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他对他们说，要污秽这殿，使院中充满被杀的人。你们出去吧。他们就出去，在城中击杀。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he said unto them, Defile the house, and fill the courts with the slain: go ye forth. And they went forth, and slew in the city.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们击杀的时候，我被留下，我就俯伏在地，说，哎。主耶和华阿，你将忿怒倾在耶路撒冷，岂要将以色列所剩下的人都灭绝吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it came to pass, while they were slaying them, and I was left, that I fell upon my face, and cried, and said, Ah Lord GOD! wilt thou destroy all the residue of Israel in thy pouring out of thy fury upon Jerusalem?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他对我说，以色列家和犹大家的罪孽极其重大。遍地有流血的事，满城有冤屈，因为他们说，耶和华已经离弃这地，他看不见我们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then said he unto me, The iniquity of the house of Israel and Judah is exceeding great, and the land is full of blood, and the city full of perverseness: for they say, The LORD hath forsaken the earth, and the LORD seeth not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>故此，我眼必不顾惜，也不可怜他们，要照他们所行的报应在他们头上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And as for me also, mine eye shall not spare, neither will I have pity, but I will recompense their way upon their head.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那穿细麻衣，腰间带着墨盒子的人将这事回覆说，我已经照你所吩咐的行了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And, behold, the man clothed with linen, which had the inkhorn by his side, reported the matter, saying, I have done as thou hast commanded me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
