<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以西结书(Ezekiel) 第1章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>当三十年四月初五日，以西结（原文作我）在迦巴鲁河边被掳的人中，天就开了，得见神的异象。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now it came to pass in the thirtieth year, in the fourth month, in the fifth day of the month, as I was among the captives by the river of Chebar, that the heavens were opened, and I saw visions of God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>正是约雅斤王被掳去第五年四月初五日，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the fifth day of the month, which was the fifth year of king Jehoiachin's captivity,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在迦勒底人之地，迦巴鲁河边，耶和华的话特特临到布西的儿子祭司以西结。耶和华的灵（原文作手）降在他身上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The word of the LORD came expressly unto Ezekiel the priest, the son of Buzi, in the land of the Chaldeans by the river Chebar; and the hand of the LORD was there upon him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我观看，见狂风从北方刮来，随着有一朵包括闪烁火的大云，周围有光辉。从其中的火内发出好像光耀的精金。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I looked, and, behold, a whirlwind came out of the north, a great cloud, and a fire infolding itself, and a brightness was about it, and out of the midst thereof as the colour of amber, out of the midst of the fire.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又从其中显出四个活物的形像来。他们的形状是这样，有人的形像，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Also out of the midst thereof came the likeness of four living creatures. And this was their appearance; they had the likeness of a man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>各有四个脸面，四个翅膀。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And every one had four faces, and every one had four wings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们的腿是直的，脚掌好像牛犊之蹄，都灿烂如光明的铜。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And their feet were straight feet; and the sole of their feet was like the sole of a calf's foot: and they sparkled like the colour of burnished brass.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在四面的翅膀以下有人的手。这四个活物的脸和翅膀乃是这样，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they had the hands of a man under their wings on their four sides; and they four had their faces and their wings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>翅膀彼此相接，行走并不转身，俱各直往前行。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Their wings were joined one to another; they turned not when they went; they went every one straight forward.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>至于脸的形像，前面各有人的脸，右面各有狮子的脸，左面各有牛的脸，后面各有鹰的脸。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As for the likeness of their faces, they four had the face of a man, and the face of a lion, on the right side: and they four had the face of an ox on the left side; they four also had the face of an eagle.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>各展开上边的两个翅膀相接，各以下边的两个翅膀遮体。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus were their faces: and their wings were stretched upward; two wings of every one were joined one to another, and two covered their bodies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们俱各直往前行。灵往哪里去，他们就往那里去，行走并不转身。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they went every one straight forward: whither the spirit was to go, they went; and they turned not when they went.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>至于四活物的形像，就如烧着火炭的形状，又如火把的形状。火在四活物中间上去下来，这火有光辉，从火中发出闪电。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As for the likeness of the living creatures, their appearance was like burning coals of fire, and like the appearance of lamps: it went up and down among the living creatures; and the fire was bright, and out of the fire went forth lightning.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这活物往来奔走，好像电光一闪。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the living creatures ran and returned as the appearance of a flash of lightning.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我正观看活物的时候，见活物的脸旁各有一轮在地上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now as I beheld the living creatures, behold one wheel upon the earth by the living creatures, with his four faces.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>轮的形状和颜色（原文作作法）好像水苍玉。四轮都是一个样式，形状和作法好像轮中套轮。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The appearance of the wheels and their work was like unto the colour of a beryl: and they four had one likeness: and their appearance and their work was as it were a wheel in the middle of a wheel.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>轮行走的时候，向四方都能直行，并不掉转。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When they went, they went upon their four sides: and they turned not when they went.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>至于轮辋，高而可畏。四个轮辋周围满有眼睛。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As for their rings, they were so high that they were dreadful; and their rings were full of eyes round about them four.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>活物行走，轮也在旁边行走。活物从地上升，轮也都上升。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And when the living creatures went, the wheels went by them: and when the living creatures were lifted up from the earth, the wheels were lifted up.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>灵往哪里去，活物就往那里去。活物上升，轮也在活物旁边上升，因为活物的灵在轮中。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whithersoever the spirit was to go, they went, thither was their spirit to go; and the wheels were lifted up over against them: for the spirit of the living creature was in the wheels.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那些行走，这些也行走。那些站住，这些也站住。那些从地上升，轮也在旁边上升，因为活物的灵在轮中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When those went, these went; and when those stood, these stood; and when those were lifted up from the earth, the wheels were lifted up over against them: for the spirit of the living creature was in the wheels.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>活物的头以上有穹苍的形像，看着像可畏的水晶，铺张在活物的头以上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the likeness of the firmament upon the heads of the living creature was as the colour of the terrible crystal, stretched forth over their heads above.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>穹苍以下，活物的翅膀直张，彼此相对。每活物有两个翅膀遮体。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And under the firmament were their wings straight, the one toward the other: every one had two, which covered on this side, and every one had two, which covered on that side, their bodies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>活物行走的时候，我听见翅膀的响声，像大水的声音，像全能者的声音，也像军队哄囔的声音。活物站住的时候，便将翅膀垂下。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And when they went, I heard the noise of their wings, like the noise of great waters, as the voice of the Almighty, the voice of speech, as the noise of an host: when they stood, they let down their wings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他们头以上的穹苍之上有声音。他们站住的时候，便将翅膀垂下。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And there was a voice from the firmament that was over their heads, when they stood, and had let down their wings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第26段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他们头以上的穹苍之上有宝座的形像，彷佛蓝宝石。在宝座形像以上有彷佛人的形状。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And above the firmament that was over their heads was the likeness of a throne, as the appearance of a sapphire stone: and upon the likeness of the throne was the likeness as the appearance of a man above upon it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第27段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我见从他腰以上有彷佛光耀的精金，周围都有火的形状，又见从他腰以下有彷佛火的形状，周围也有光辉。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I saw as the colour of amber, as the appearance of fire round about within it, from the appearance of his loins even upward, and from the appearance of his loins even downward, I saw as it were the appearance of fire, and it had brightness round about.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第28段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
