	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Sheba, and Dedan, and the merchants of Tarshish, with all the young lions thereof, shall say unto thee, Art thou come to take a spoil? hast thou gathered thy company to take a prey? to carry away silver and gold, to take away cattle and goods, to take a great spoil?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

</table>

</table>
