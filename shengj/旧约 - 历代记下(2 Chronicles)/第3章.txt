<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 历代记下(2 Chronicles) 第3章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所罗门就在耶路撒冷，耶和华向他父大卫显现的摩利亚山上，就是耶布斯人阿珥楠的禾场上，大卫所指定的地方预备好了，开工建造耶和华的殿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then Solomon began to build the house of the LORD at Jerusalem in mount Moriah, where the Lord appeared unto David his father, in the place that David had prepared in the threshingfloor of Ornan the Jebusite.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所罗门作王第四年二月初二日开工建造。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he began to build in the second day of the second month, in the fourth year of his reign.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所罗门建筑神殿的根基，乃是这样，长六十肘，宽二十肘，都按着古时的尺寸。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now these are the things wherein Solomon was instructed for the building of the house of God. The length by cubits after the first measure was threescore cubits, and the breadth twenty cubits.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>殿前的廊子长二十肘，与殿的宽窄一样，高一百二十肘。里面贴上精金。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the porch that was in the front of the house, the length of it was according to the breadth of the house, twenty cubits, and the height was an hundred and twenty: and he overlaid it within with pure gold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>大殿的墙都用松木板遮蔽，又贴了精金，上面雕刻棕树和链子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the greater house he cieled with fir tree, which he overlaid with fine gold, and set thereon palm trees and chains.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又用宝石装饰殿墙，使殿华美。所用的金子都是巴瓦音的金子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he garnished the house with precious stones for beauty: and the gold was gold of Parvaim.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又用金子贴殿和殿的楝梁，门槛，墙壁，门扇。墙上雕刻基路伯。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He overlaid also the house, the beams, the posts, and the walls thereof, and the doors thereof, with gold; and graved cherubims on the walls.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又建造至圣所，长二十肘，与殿的宽窄一样，宽也是二十肘。贴上精金，共用金子六百他连得。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he made the most holy house, the length whereof was according to the breadth of the house, twenty cubits, and the breadth thereof twenty cubits: and he overlaid it with fine gold, amounting to six hundred talents.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>金钉重五十舍客勒。楼房都贴上金子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the weight of the nails was fifty shekels of gold. And he overlaid the upper chambers with gold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在至圣所按造像的法子造两个基路伯，用金子包裹。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And in the most holy house he made two cherubims of image work, and overlaid them with gold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>两个基路伯的翅膀共长二十肘。这基路伯的一个翅膀长五肘，挨着殿这边的墙。那一个翅膀也长五肘，与那基路伯翅膀相接。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the wings of the cherubims were twenty cubits long: one wing of the one cherub was five cubits, reaching to the wall of the house: and the other wing was likewise five cubits, reaching to the wing of the other cherub.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那基路伯的一个翅膀长五肘，挨着殿那边的墙。那一个翅膀也长五肘，与这基路伯的翅膀相接。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And one wing of the other cherub was five cubits, reaching to the wall of the house: and the other wing was five cubits also, joining to the wing of the other cherub.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>两个基路伯张开翅膀，共长二十肘，面向外殿而立。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wings of these cherubims spread themselves forth twenty cubits: and they stood on their feet, and their faces were inward.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又用蓝色，紫色，朱红色线和细麻织幔子，在其上绣出基路伯来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he made the vail of blue, and purple, and crimson, and fine linen, and wrought cherubims thereon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在殿前造了两根柱子，高三十五肘。每柱顶高五肘。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Also he made before the house two pillars of thirty and five cubits high, and the chapiter that was on the top of each of them was five cubits.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又照圣所内链子的样式作链子，安在柱顶上。又作一百石榴，安在链子上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he made chains, as in the oracle, and put them on the heads of the pillars; and made an hundred pomegranates, and put them on the chains.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he reared up the pillars before the temple, one on the right hand, and the other on the left; and called the name of that on the right hand Jachin, and the name of that on the left Boaz.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
