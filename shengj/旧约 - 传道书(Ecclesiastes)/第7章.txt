<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 传道书(Ecclesiastes) 第7章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>名誉强如美好的膏油。人死的日子，胜过人生的日子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A good name is better than precious ointment; and the day of death than the day of one's birth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>往遭丧的家去，强如往宴乐的家去，因为死是众人的结局。活人也必将这事放在心上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is better to go to the house of mourning, than to go to the house of feasting: for that is the end of all men; and the living will lay it to his heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>忧愁强如喜笑，因为面带愁容，终必使心喜乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Sorrow is better than laughter: for by the sadness of the countenance the heart is made better.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人的心，在遭丧之家。愚昧人的心，在快乐之家。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The heart of the wise is in the house of mourning; but the heart of fools is in the house of mirth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>听智慧人的责备，强如听愚昧人的歌唱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is better to hear the rebuke of the wise, than for a man to hear the song of fools.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚昧人的笑声，好像锅下烧荆棘的爆声，这也是虚空。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For as the crackling of thorns under a pot, so is the laughter of the fool: this also is vanity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>勒索使智慧人变为愚妄。贿赂能败坏人的慧心。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Surely oppression maketh a wise man mad; and a gift destroyeth the heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>事情的终局，强如事情的起头。存心忍耐的，胜过居心骄傲的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Better is the end of a thing than the beginning thereof: and the patient in spirit is better than the proud in spirit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你不要心里急躁恼怒，因为恼怒存在愚昧人的怀中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Be not hasty in thy spirit to be angry: for anger resteth in the bosom of fools.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要说，先前的日子强过如今的日子，是什么缘故呢。你这样问，不是出于智慧。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Say not thou, What is the cause that the former days were better than these? for thou dost not enquire wisely concerning this.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧和产业并好。而且见天日的人，得智慧更为有益。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wisdom is good with an inheritance: and by it there is profit to them that see the sun.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为智慧护庇人，好像银钱护庇人一样。惟独智慧能保全智慧人的生命，这就是知识的益处。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For wisdom is a defence, and money is a defence: but the excellency of knowledge is, that wisdom giveth life to them that have it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要察看神的作为，因为神使为曲的，谁能变为直呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Consider the work of God: for who can make that straight, which he hath made crooked?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>遇亨通的日子，你当喜乐。遭患难的日子，你当思想。因为神使这两样并列，为的是叫人查不出身后有什么事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the day of prosperity be joyful, but in the day of adversity consider: God also hath set the one over against the other, to the end that man should find nothing after him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有义人行义，反致灭亡。有恶人行恶，倒享长寿。这都是我在虚度之日中所见过的。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All things have I seen in the days of my vanity: there is a just man that perisheth in his righteousness, and there is a wicked man that prolongeth his life in his wickedness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要行义过分。也不要过于自逞智慧。何必自取败亡呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Be not righteous over much; neither make thyself over wise: why shouldest thou destroy thyself ?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要行恶过分。也不要为人愚昧。何必不到期而死呢。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Be not over much wicked, neither be thou foolish: why shouldest thou die before thy time?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你持守这个为美。那个也不要松手。因为敬畏神的人，必从这两样出来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is good that thou shouldest take hold of this; yea, also from this withdraw not thine hand: for he that feareth God shall come forth of them all.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧使有智慧的人，比城中十个官长更有能力。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wisdom strengtheneth the wise more than ten mighty men which are in the city.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>时常行善而不犯罪的义人，世上实在没有。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For there is not a just man upon earth, that doeth good, and sinneth not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人所说的一切话，你不要放在心上，恐怕听见你的仆人咒诅你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Also take no heed unto all words that are spoken; lest thou hear thy servant curse thee:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为你心里知道，自己也曾屡次咒诅别人。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For oftentimes also thine own heart knoweth that thou thyself likewise hast cursed others.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我曾用智慧试验这一切事。我说，要得智慧，智慧却离我远。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All this have I proved by wisdom: I said, I will be wise; but it was far from me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>万事之理离我甚远，而且最深，谁能测透呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That which is far off, and exceeding deep, who can find it out?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我转念，一心要知道，要考察，要寻求智慧，和万事的理由。又要知道邪恶为愚昧，愚昧为狂妄。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I applied mine heart to know, and to search, and to seek out wisdom, and the reason of things, and to know the wickedness of folly, even of foolishness and madness:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第26段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我得知有等妇人，比死还苦，她的心是网罗，手是锁练。凡蒙神喜悦的人，必能躲避她。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I find more bitter than death the woman, whose heart is snares and nets, and her hands as bands: whoso pleaseth God shall escape from her; but the sinner shall be taken by her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第27段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有罪的人，却被她缠住了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, this have I found, saith the preacher, counting one by one, to find out the account:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第28段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>传道者说，看哪，一千男子中，我找到一个正直人。但众女子中，没有找到一个。我将这事一一比较，要寻求其理，我心仍要寻梢，却未曾找到。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which yet my soul seeketh, but I find not: one man among a thousand have I found; but a woman among all those have I not found.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第29段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我所找到的，只有一件，就是神造人原是正直，但他们寻出许多巧计。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lo, this only have I found, that God hath made man upright; but they have sought out many inventions.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
