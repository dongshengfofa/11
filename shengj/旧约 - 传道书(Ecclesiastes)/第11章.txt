<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 传道书(Ecclesiastes) 第11章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>当将你的粮食撒在水面，因为日久必能得着。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Cast thy bread upon the waters: for thou shalt find it after many days.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要分给七人，或分给八人，因为你不知道将来有什么灾祸临到地上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Give a portion to seven, and also to eight; for thou knowest not what evil shall be upon the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>云若满了雨，就必倾倒在地上。树若向南倒，或向北倒，树倒在何处，就存在何处。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If the clouds be full of rain, they empty themselves upon the earth: and if the tree fall toward the south, or toward the north, in the place where the tree falleth, there it shall be.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看风的必不撒种。望云的必不收割。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that observeth the wind shall not sow; and he that regardeth the clouds shall not reap.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>风从何道来，骨头在怀孕妇人的胎中如何长成，你尚且不得知道，这样，行万事之神的作为，你更不得知道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As thou knowest not what is the way of the spirit, nor how the bones do grow in the womb of her that is with child: even so thou knowest not the works of God who maketh all.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>早晨要撒你的种，晚上也不歇你的手，因为你不知那一样发旺，或是早撒的，或是晚撒的，或是两样都好。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the morning sow thy seed, and in the evening withhold not thine hand: for thou knowest not whether shall prosper, either this or that, or whether they both shall be alike good.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>光本是佳美的，眼见日光也是可悦的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Truly the light is sweet, and a pleasant thing it is for the eyes to behold the sun:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人活多年，就当快乐多年。然而也当想到黑暗的日子，因为这日子必多，所要来的都是虚空。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if a man live many years, and rejoice in them all; yet let him remember the days of darkness; for they shall be many. All that cometh is vanity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>少年人哪，你在幼年时当快乐。在幼年的日子，使你的心欢畅，行你心所愿行的，看你眼所爱看的，却要知道，为这一切的事，神必审问你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Rejoice, O young man, in thy youth; and let thy heart cheer thee in the days of thy youth, and walk in the ways of thine heart, and in the sight of thine eyes: but know thou, that for all these things God will bring thee into judgment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以你当从心中除掉愁烦。从肉体克去邪恶。因为一生的开端，和幼年之时，都是虚空的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore remove sorrow from thy heart, and put away evil from thy flesh: for childhood and youth are vanity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
