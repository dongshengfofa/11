<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 传道书(Ecclesiastes) 第10章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>死苍蝇，使作香的膏油发出臭气。这样，一点愚昧，也能败坏智慧和尊荣。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Dead flies cause the ointment of the apothecary to send forth a stinking savour: so doth a little folly him that is in reputation for wisdom and honour.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人的心居右。愚昧人的心居左。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wise man's heart is at his right hand; but a fool's heart at his left.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>并且愚昧人行路，显出无知。对众人说，他是愚昧人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yea also, when he that is a fool walketh by the way, his wisdom faileth him, and he saith to every one that he is a fool.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>掌权者的心，若向你发怒，不要离开你的本位，因为柔和能免大过。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If the spirit of the ruler rise up against thee, leave not thy place; for yielding pacifieth great offences.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我见日光之下，有一件祸患，似乎出于掌权的错误，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There is an evil which I have seen under the sun, as an error which proceedeth from the ruler:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>就是愚昧人立在高位。富足人坐在低位。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Folly is set in great dignity, and the rich sit in low place.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我见过仆人骑马，王子像仆人在地上步行。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have seen servants upon horses, and princes walking as servants upon the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>挖陷坑的，自己必掉在其中。拆墙垣的，必被蛇所咬。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that diggeth a pit shall fall into it; and whoso breaketh an hedge, a serpent shall bite him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凿开（或作挪移）石头的，必受损伤。擘开木头的，必遭危险。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso removeth stones shall be hurt therewith; and he that cleaveth wood shall be endangered thereby.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>铁器钝了，若不将刃磨快，就必多费气力。但得智慧指教，便有益处。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If the iron be blunt, and he do not whet the edge, then must he put to more strength: but wisdom is profitable to direct.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>未行法术以先，蛇若咬人，后行法术也是无益。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Surely the serpent will bite without enchantment; and a babbler is no better.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人的口，说出恩言。愚昧人的嘴，吞灭自己。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The words of a wise man's mouth are gracious; but the lips of a fool will swallow up himself.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他口中的言语，起头是愚昧。他话的末尾，是奸恶的狂妄。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The beginning of the words of his mouth is foolishness: and the end of his talk is mischievous madness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚昧人多有言语，人却不知将来有什么事。他身后的事，谁能告诉他呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A fool also is full of words: a man cannot tell what shall be; and what shall be after him, who can tell him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡愚昧人，他的劳碌使自己困乏。因为连进城的路他也不知道。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The labour of the foolish wearieth every one of them, because he knoweth not how to go to the city.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>邦国阿，你的王若是孩童，你的群臣早晨宴乐，你就有祸了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Woe to thee, O land, when thy king is a child, and thy princes eat in the morning!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>邦国阿，你的王若是贵胄之子，你的群臣按时吃喝，为要捕力，不为酒醉，你就有福了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Blessed art thou, O land, when thy king is the son of nobles, and thy princes eat in due season, for strength, and not for drunkenness!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因人懒惰，房顶塌下。因人手懒，房屋滴漏。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>By much slothfulness the building decayeth; and through idleness of the hands the house droppeth through.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>设摆筵席，是为喜笑。酒能使人快活，钱能叫万事应心。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A feast is made for laughter, and wine maketh merry: but money answereth all things.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你不可咒诅君王，也不可心怀此念。在你卧房也不可咒诅富户。因为空中的鸟，必传扬这声音。有翅膀的，也必述说这事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Curse not the king, no not in thy thought; and curse not the rich in thy bedchamber: for a bird of the air shall carry the voice, and that which hath wings shall tell the matter.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
