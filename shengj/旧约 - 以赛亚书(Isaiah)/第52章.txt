<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第52章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>锡安哪，兴起，兴起，披上你的能力。圣城耶路撒冷阿，穿上你华美的衣服。因为从今以后，未受割礼不洁净的，必不再进入你中间。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Awake, awake; put on thy strength, O Zion; put on thy beautiful garments, O Jerusalem, the holy city: for henceforth there shall no more come into thee the uncircumcised and the unclean.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶路撒冷阿，要抖下尘土。起来坐在位上。锡安被掳的居民哪，（居民原文作女子）要解开你颈项的锁链。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Shake thyself from the dust; arise, and sit down, O Jerusalem: loose thyself from the bands of thy neck, O captive daughter of Zion.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华如此说，你们是无价被卖的，也必无银被赎。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thus saith the LORD, Ye have sold yourselves for nought; and ye shall be redeemed without money.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主耶和华如此说，起先我的百姓下到埃及，在那里寄居，又有亚述人无故欺压他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thus saith the Lord GOD, My people went down aforetime into Egypt to sojourn there; and the Assyrian oppressed them without cause.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华说，我的百姓既是无价被掳去，如今我在这里作什么呢。耶和华说，辖制他们的人呼叫，我的名整天受亵渎</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now therefore, what have I here, saith the LORD, that my people is taken away for nought? they that rule over them make them to howl, saith the LORD; and my name continually every day is blasphemed.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以我的百姓必知道我的名。到那日他们必知道说这话的就是我，看哪，是我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore my people shall know my name: therefore they shall know in that day that I am he that doth speak: behold, it is I.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How beautiful upon the mountains are the feet of him that bringeth good tidings, that publisheth peace; that bringeth good tidings of good, that publisheth salvation; that saith unto Zion, Thy God reigneth!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy watchmen shall lift up the voice; with the voice together shall they sing: for they shall see eye to eye, when the LORD shall bring again Zion.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>耶路撒冷的荒场阿，要发起欢声，一同歌唱。因为耶和华安慰了他的百姓。救赎了耶路撒冷。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	</tr>
</table>
