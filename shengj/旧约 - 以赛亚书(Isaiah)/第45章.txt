<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第45章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我耶和华所的膏的居鲁士，我搀扶他的右手，使列国降伏在他面前，我也要放松列王的腰带，使城门在他面前敞开，不得关闭，我对他如此说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus saith the LORD to his anointed, to Cyrus, whose right hand I have holden, to subdue nations before him; and I will loose the loins of kings, to open before him the two leaved gates; and the gates shall not be shut;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必在你面前行，修平崎岖之地。我必打破铜门，砍断铁闩。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will go before thee, and make the crooked places straight: I will break in pieces the gates of brass, and cut in sunder the bars of iron:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我要将暗中的宝物，和隐密的财宝赐给你，使你知道题名召你的，就是我耶和华以色列的神。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will give thee the treasures of darkness, and hidden riches of secret places, that thou mayest know that I, the LORD, which call thee by thy name, am the God of Israel.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因我仆人雅各，我所拣选以色列的缘故，我就题名召你。你虽不认识我，我也加给你名号。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For Jacob my servant's sake, and Israel mine elect, I have even called thee by thy name: I have surnamed thee, though thou hast not known me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我是耶和华，在我以外并没有别神，除了我以外再没有神。你虽不认识我，我必给你束腰，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I am the LORD, and there is none else, there is no God beside me: I girded thee, though thou hast not known me:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>从日出之地到日落之处，使人都知道除了我以外，没有别神，我是耶和华，在我以外并没有别神。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That they may know from the rising of the sun, and from the west, that there is none beside me. I am the LORD, and there is none else.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我造光，又造暗。我施平安，又降灾祸。造作这一切的是我耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I form the light, and create darkness: I make peace, and create evil: I the LORD do all these things.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>诸天哪，自上而滴。穹苍降下公义，地面开裂，产出救恩，使公义一同发生，这都是我耶和华所造的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Drop down, ye heavens, from above, and let the skies pour down righteousness: let the earth open, and let them bring forth salvation, and let righteousness spring up together; I the LORD have created it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>祸哉，那与造他的主争论的，他不过是地上瓦片中的一块瓦片。泥土岂可对抟弄他的说，你作什么呢。所作的物，岂可说，你没有手呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Woe unto him that striveth with his Maker! Let the potsherd strive with the potsherds of the earth. Shall the clay say to him that fashioneth it, What makest thou? or thy work, He hath no hands?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>祸哉，那对父亲说，你生的是什么呢。或对母亲（原文作妇人）说，你产的是什么呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Woe unto him that saith unto his father, What begettest thou? or to the woman, What hast thou brought forth?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华以色列的圣者，就是造就以色列的，如此说，将来的事你们可以问我。至于我的众子，并我手的工作，你们可以求我命定。（求我命定原文作吩咐我）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus saith the LORD, the Holy One of Israel, and his Maker, Ask me of things to come concerning my sons, and concerning the work of my hands command ye me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我造地，又造人在地上，我亲手铺张诸天，天上万象也是我所命定的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have made the earth, and created man upon it: I, even my hands, have stretched out the heavens, and all their host have I commanded.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我凭公义兴起居鲁士，（居鲁士原文作他）又要修直他一切道路。他必建造我的城，释放我被掳的民，不是为工价，也不是为赏赐。这是万军之耶和华说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have raised him up in righteousness, and I will direct all his ways: he shall build my city, and he shall let go my captives, not for price nor reward, saith the LORD of hosts.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>
