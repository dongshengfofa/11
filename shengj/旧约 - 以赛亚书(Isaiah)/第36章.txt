<p>第1段</p>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now it came to pass in the fourteenth year of king Hezekiah, that Sennacherib king of Assyria came up against all the defenced cities of Judah, and took them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>亚述王从拉吉差遣拉伯沙基，率领大军，往耶路撒冷到希西家王那里去。他就站在上池的水沟旁，在漂布地的大路上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the king of Assyria sent Rabshakeh from Lachish to Jerusalem unto king Hezekiah with a great army. And he stood by the conduit of the upper pool in the highway of the fuller's field.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>于是希勒家的儿子家宰以利亚敬，并书记舍伯那，和亚萨的儿子史官约亚，出来见拉伯沙基。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then came forth unto him Eliakim, Hilkiah's son, which was over the house, and Shebna the scribe, and Joah, Asaph's son, the recorder.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>拉伯沙基对他们说，你们去告诉希西家说，亚述大王如此说，你所倚靠的有什么可仗赖的呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Rabshakeh said unto them, Say ye now to Hezekiah, Thus saith the great king, the king of Assyria, What confidence is this wherein thou trustest?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你说，有打仗的计谋和能力。我看不过是虚话，你到底倚靠谁，才背叛我呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I say, sayest thou, (but they are but vain words) I have counsel and strength for war: now on whom dost thou trust, that thou rebellest against me?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看哪，你所倚靠的埃及，是那压伤的苇仗，人若靠这仗，就必刺透他的手。埃及王法老向一切倚靠他的人，也是这样。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lo, thou trustest in the staff of this broken reed, on Egypt; whereon if a man lean, it will go into his hand, and pierce it: so is Pharaoh king of Egypt to all that trust in him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if thou say to me, We trust in the LORD our God: is it not he, whose high places and whose altars Hezekiah hath taken away, and said to Judah and to Jerusalem, Ye shall worship before this altar?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now therefore give pledges, I pray thee, to my master the king of Assyria, and I will give thee two thousand horses, if thou be able on thy part to set riders upon them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And am I now come up without the LORD against this land to destroy it? the LORD said unto me, Go up against this land, and destroy it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then said Eliakim and Shebna and Joah unto Rabshakeh, Speak, I pray thee, unto thy servants in the Syrian language; for we understand it: and speak not to us in the Jews' language, in the ears of the people that are on the wall.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>

	<tr>

		<td>拉伯沙基说，我主差遣我来，岂是单对你和你的主说这些话吗，不也是对这些坐在城上，要与你们一同吃自己粪喝自己尿的人说吗。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But Rabshakeh said, Hath my master sent me to thy master and to thee to speak these words? hath he not sent me to the men that sit upon the wall, that they may eat their own dung, and drink their own piss with you?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>
