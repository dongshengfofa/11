<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第33章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>祸哉，你这毁灭人的，自己倒不被毁灭。行事诡诈的，人倒不以诡诈待你。你毁灭吧休了，自己必被毁灭。你行完了诡诈，人必以诡诈待你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Woe to thee that spoilest, and thou wast not spoiled; and dealest treacherously, and they dealt not treacherously with thee! when thou shalt cease to spoil, thou shalt be spoiled; and when thou shalt make an end to deal treacherously, they shall deal treacherously with thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，求你施恩于我们。我们等候你。求你每早晨作我们的膀臂。遭难的时候，为我们的拯救。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O LORD, be gracious unto us; we have waited for thee: be thou their arm every morning, our salvation also in the time of trouble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

</table>
