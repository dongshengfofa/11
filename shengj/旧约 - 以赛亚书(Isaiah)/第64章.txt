	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>

	<tr>

		<td>从古以来人未曾听见，未曾耳闻，未曾眼见，在你以外有什么神为等候他的人行事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For since the beginning of the world men have not heard, nor perceived by the ear, neither hath the eye seen, O God, beside thee, what he hath prepared for him that waiteth for him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你迎接那欢喜行义记念你道的人，你曾发怒，我们仍犯罪。这景况以久。我们还能得救吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou meetest him that rejoiceth and worketh righteousness, those that remember thee in thy ways: behold, thou art wroth; for we have sinned: in those is continuance, and we shall be saved.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们都像不洁净的人，所有的义都像污秽的衣服。我们都像叶子渐渐枯乾。我们的罪孽好像风把我们吹去。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But we are all as an unclean thing, and all our righteousnesses are as filthy rags; and we all do fade as a leaf; and our iniquities, like the wind, have taken us away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>并且无人求告你的名，无人奋刀抓住你。原来你掩面不顾我们，使我们因罪孽消化。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And there is none that calleth upon thy name, that stirreth up himself to take hold of thee: for thou hast hid thy face from us, and hast consumed us, because of our iniquities.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td>耶和华阿，现在你仍是我们的父。我们是泥，你是窑匠。我们都是你手的工作。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>
