<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第62章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我因锡安必不静默，为耶路撒冷必不息声，直到他的公义如光辉发出，他的救恩如明灯发亮。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For Zion's sake will I not hold my peace, and for Jerusalem's sake I will not rest, until the righteousness thereof go forth as brightness, and the salvation thereof as a lamp that burneth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>列国必见你的公义，列王必见你的荣耀。你必得新名的称呼，是耶和华亲口所起的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the Gentiles shall see thy righteousness, and all kings thy glory: and thou shalt be called by a new name, which the mouth of the LORD shall name.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你在耶和华的手中要作为华冠，在你神的掌上必作为冕旒。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou shalt also be a crown of glory in the hand of the LORD, and a royal diadem in the hand of thy God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你必不再称为撇弃的，你的地也不再称为荒凉的，你却要称为我所喜悦的，你的地也必称为有夫之妇。因为耶和华喜悦你，你的地也必归他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou shalt no more be termed Forsaken; neither shall thy land any more be termed Desolate: but thou shalt be called Hephzibah, and thy land Beulah: for the LORD delighteth in thee, and thy land shall be married.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>少年人怎样娶处女，你的众民（民原文作子）也要照样娶你。新郎怎样喜悦新妇，你的神也照样喜悦你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For as a young man marrieth a virgin, so shall thy sons marry thee: and as the bridegroom rejoiceth over the bride, so shall thy God rejoice over thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶路撒冷阿，我在你城上设立守望的。他们昼夜必不静默。呼吁耶和华的，你们不要歇息，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have set watchmen upon thy walls, O Jerusalem, which shall never hold their peace day nor night: ye that make mention of the LORD, keep not silence,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>也不要使他歇息，直等他建立耶路撒冷，使耶路撒冷在地上成为可赞美的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And give him no rest, till he establish, and till he make Jerusalem a praise in the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华指着自己的右手和大能的膀臂起誓，说，我必不再将你的五谷给你的仇敌作食物，外邦人也不再喝你劳碌得来的新酒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD hath sworn by his right hand, and by the arm of his strength, Surely I will no more give thy corn to be meat for thine enemies; and the sons of the stranger shall not drink thy wine, for the which thou hast laboured:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟有那收割的要吃，并赞美耶和华。那聚敛的要在我圣所的院内喝。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But they that have gathered it shall eat it, and praise the LORD; and they that have brought it together shall drink it in the courts of my holiness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>你们当从门经过经过。豫备百姓的路。修筑修筑大道。捡去石头。为万民竖立大旗。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Go through, go through the gates; prepare ye the way of the people; cast up, cast up the highway; gather out the stones; lift up a standard for the people.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
</table>
