<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第1章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>当乌西雅，约坦，亚哈斯，希西家，作犹大王的时候，亚摩斯的儿子以赛亚得默示，论到犹大和耶路撒冷。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The vision of Isaiah the son of Amoz, which he saw concerning Judah and Jerusalem in the days of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>天哪，要听，地阿，侧耳而听。因为耶和华说，我养育儿女，将他们养大，他们竟悖逆我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear, O heavens, and give ear, O earth: for the LORD hath spoken, I have nourished and brought up children, and they have rebelled against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>牛认识主人，驴认识主人得的槽。以色列却不认识，我的民却不留意。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The ox knoweth his owner, and the ass his master's crib: but Israel doth not know, my people doth not consider.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>？？，犯罪的国民，担着罪孽的百姓，行恶的种类，败坏的儿女。他们离弃耶和华，藐视以色列的圣者，与他生疏，往后退步。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ah sinful nation, a people laden with iniquity, a seed of evildoers, children that are corrupters: they have forsaken the LORD, they have provoked the Holy One of Israel unto anger, they are gone away backward.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们为什么屡次悖逆，还要受责打吗。你们已经满头疼痛，全心发昏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Why should ye be stricken any more? ye will revolt more and more: the whole head is sick, and the whole heart faint.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>从脚掌到头顶，没有一处完全的。竟是伤口，青肿，与新打的伤痕。都没有收口，没有缠裹，也没有用膏滋润。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>From the sole of the foot even unto the head there is no soundness in it; but wounds, and bruises, and putrifying sores: they have not been closed, neither bound up, neither mollified with ointment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们的地土已经荒凉。你们的城邑被火焚毁。你们的田地，在你们的眼前为外邦人所侵吞，既被外邦人倾覆，就成为荒凉。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Your country is desolate, your cities are burned with fire: your land, strangers devour it in your presence, and it is desolate, as overthrown by strangers.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>仅存锡安城（城原文作女子），好像葡萄园的草棚，瓜田的茅屋，被围困的城邑。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the daughter of Zion is left as a cottage in a vineyard, as a lodge in a garden of cucumbers, as a besieged city.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>若不是万军之耶和华给我们稍留馀种，我们早以像所多玛，蛾摩拉的样子了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Except the LORD of hosts had left unto us a very small remnant, we should have been as Sodom, and we should have been like unto Gomorrah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们这所多玛的官长阿，要听耶和华的话。你们这蛾摩拉的百姓阿，要侧耳听我们的神的训诲。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear the word of the LORD, ye rulers of Sodom; give ear unto the law of our God, ye people of Gomorrah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华说，你们所献的许多祭物，与我何益呢。公绵羊的燔祭，和肥畜的脂油，我已经彀了。公牛的血，羊羔的血，公山羊的血，我都不喜悦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To what purpose is the multitude of your sacrifices unto me? saith the LORD: I am full of the burnt offerings of rams, and the fat of fed beasts; and I delight not in the blood of bullocks, or of lambs, or of he goats.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们来朝见我，谁向你们讨这些，使你们践踏我的院宇呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When ye come to appear before me, who hath required this at your hand, to tread my courts?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们不要再献虚浮的供物。香品是我所憎恶的。月朔，和安息日，并宣召的大会，也是我所憎恶的。作罪孽，又守严肃会，我也不能容忍。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Bring no more vain oblations; incense is an abomination unto me; the new moons and sabbaths, the calling of assemblies, I cannot away with; it is iniquity, even the solemn meeting.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们的月朔，和节期，我心里恨恶，我都以为麻烦。我担当，便不耐烦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Your new moons and your appointed feasts my soul hateth: they are a trouble unto me; I am weary to bear them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们举手祷告，我必遮眼不看。就是你们多多的祈祷，我也不听。你们的手都满了杀人的血。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And when ye spread forth your hands, I will hide mine eyes from you: yea, when ye make many prayers, I will not hear: your hands are full of blood.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要洗濯，自洁。从我眼前除掉你们的恶行。要止住作恶，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wash you, make you clean; put away the evil of your doings from before mine eyes; cease to do evil;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>学习行善。寻求公平，解救受欺压的，给孤儿伸冤，为寡妇辨屈。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Learn to do well; seek judgment, relieve the oppressed, judge the fatherless, plead for the widow.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华说，你们来，我们彼此辩论。你们的罪虽像朱红，必变成雪白。虽红如丹颜，必白如羊毛。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Come now, and let us reason together, saith the LORD: though your sins be as scarlet, they shall be as white as snow; though they be red like crimson, they shall be as wool.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们若甘心听从，必吃地上的美物。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If ye be willing and obedient, ye shall eat the good of the land:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>若不听从，反倒悖逆，必被刀剑吞灭。这是耶和华亲口说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if ye refuse and rebel, ye shall be devoured with the sword: for the mouth of the LORD hath spoken it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>可叹忠信的城，变为妓女。从前充满了公平，公义居住在其中，现今却有凶手居住。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How is the faithful city become an harlot! it was full of judgment; righteousness lodged in it; but now murderers.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的银子，变为渣滓，你的酒，用水搀对。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy silver is become dross, thy wine mixed with water:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的官长居心悖逆，与盗贼作伴。各都喜爱贿赂，追求赃私。他们不为孤儿伸冤，寡妇的案件，也不得呈到他们面前。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy princes are rebellious, and companions of thieves: every one loveth gifts, and followeth after rewards: they judge not the fatherless, neither doth the cause of the widow come unto them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因此主万军之耶和华以色列的大能者说，我要向我的对头雪恨，向我的敌人报仇。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore saith the LORD, the LORD of hosts, the mighty One of Israel, Ah, I will ease me of mine adversaries, and avenge me of mine enemies:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will turn my hand upon thee, and purely purge away thy dross, and take away all thy tin:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will restore thy judges as at the first, and thy counsellors as at the beginning: afterward thou shalt be called, The city of righteousness, the faithful city.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
