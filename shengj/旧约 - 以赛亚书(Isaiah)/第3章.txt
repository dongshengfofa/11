	</tr>
	<tr>

</table>

	<tr>

		<td>The mighty man, and the man of war, the judge, and the prophet, and the prudent, and the ancient,</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will give children to be their princes, and babes shall rule over them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>百姓要彼此欺压，各人受邻舍的欺压。少年人必侮慢老年人，卑贱人必侮慢尊贵人。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the people shall be oppressed, every one by another, and every one by his neighbour: the child shall behave himself proudly against the ancient, and the base against the honourable.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人在父家拉住弟兄，说，你有衣服，可以作我们的官长，这败落的事归在你手下吧。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When a man shall take hold of his brother of the house of his father, saying, Thou hast clothing, be thou our ruler, and let this ruin be under thy hand:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那时他必扬声说，我不作医治你们的人。因我家中没有粮食，也没有衣服。你们不可立我作百姓的官长。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In that day shall he swear, saying, I will not be an healer; for in my house is neither bread nor clothing: make me not a ruler of the people.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶路撒冷败落，犹大倾倒。因为他们的舌头，和行为，与耶和华反对，惹了他荣光的眼目。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For Jerusalem is ruined, and Judah is fallen: because their tongue and their doings are against the LORD, to provoke the eyes of his glory.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们的面色证明自己的不正。他们述说自己的罪恶，并不隐瞒，好像所多玛一样。他们有祸了。因为作恶自害。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The shew of their countenance doth witness against them; and they declare their sin as Sodom, they hide it not. Woe unto their soul! for they have rewarded evil unto themselves.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
