<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第61章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主耶和华的灵在我身上。因为耶和华用膏膏我，叫我传好信息给谦卑的人，（或作传福音给贫穷的人）差遣我医好伤心的人，报告被掳的得释放，被囚的出监牢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The Spirit of the Lord GOD is upon me; because the LORD hath anointed me to preach good tidings unto the meek; he hath sent me to bind up the brokenhearted, to proclaim liberty to the captives, and the opening of the prison to them that are bound;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>报告耶和华的恩年，和我们神报仇的日子。安慰一切悲哀的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To proclaim the acceptable year of the LORD, and the day of vengeance of our God; to comfort all that mourn;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>赐华冠与锡安悲哀的人，代替灰尘，喜乐油，代替悲哀，赞美衣，代替忧伤之灵。使他们称为公义树，是耶和华所栽的，叫他得荣耀。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To appoint unto them that mourn in Zion, to give unto them beauty for ashes, the oil of joy for mourning, the garment of praise for the spirit of heaviness; that they might be called trees of righteousness, the planting of the LORD, that he might be glorified.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们必修造已久的荒场，建立先前凄凉之处，重修历代荒凉之城。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they shall build the old wastes, they shall raise up the former desolations, and they shall repair the waste cities, the desolations of many generations.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那时，外人必起来牧放你们的羊群，外邦人必做你们耕种田地的，修理葡萄园的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And strangers shall stand and feed your flocks, and the sons of the alien shall be your plowmen and your vinedressers.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们倒要称为耶和华的祭司。人必称你们为我们神的仆役。你们必吃用列国的财物，因得他们的荣耀自夸。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But ye shall be named the Priests of the LORD: men shall call you the Ministers of our God: ye shall eat the riches of the Gentiles, and in their glory shall ye boast yourselves.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们必得加倍的好处，代替所受的修辱。分中所得的喜乐，必代替所受的凌辱。在境内必得加倍的产业。永远之乐必归与你们。（原文他们）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For your shame ye shall have double; and for confusion they shall rejoice in their portion: therefore in their land they shall possess the double: everlasting joy shall be unto them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为我耶和华喜爱公平，恨恶抢夺和罪孽。我要凭诚实施行报应，并要与我的百姓立永约。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For I the LORD love judgment, I hate robbery for burnt offering; and I will direct their work in truth, and I will make an everlasting covenant with them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们的后裔必在列国中被人认识，他们的子孙在众民中也是如此。凡看见他们的，必认他们是耶和华赐福的后裔。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And their seed shall be known among the Gentiles, and their offspring among the people: all that see them shall acknowledge them, that they are the seed which the LORD hath blessed.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我因耶和华大大欢喜，我的心靠神快乐。因他以拯救为衣给我穿上，以公义为袍给我披上，好像新郎戴上华冠，又像新妇佩戴妆饰。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will greatly rejoice in the LORD, my soul shall be joyful in my God; for he hath clothed me with the garments of salvation, he hath covered me with the robe of righteousness, as a bridegroom decketh himself with ornaments, and as a bride adorneth herself with her jewels.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>田地怎样使百谷发芽，园子怎样使所种的发生，主耶和华必照样使公义和赞美在万民中发出。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For as the earth bringeth forth her bud, and as the garden causeth the things that are sown in it to spring forth; so the Lord GOD will cause righteousness and praise to spring forth before all the nations.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
