</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Enflaming yourselves with idols under every green tree, slaying the children in the valleys under the clifts of the rocks?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Among the smooth stones of the stream is thy portion; they, they are thy lot: even to them hast thou poured a drink offering, thou hast offered a meat offering. Should I receive comfort in these?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Upon a lofty and high mountain hast thou set thy bed: even thither wentest thou up to offer sacrifice.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behind the doors also and the posts hast thou set up thy remembrance: for thou hast discovered thyself to another than me, and art gone up; thou hast enlarged thy bed, and made thee a covenant with them; thou lovedst their bed where thou sawest it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	</tr>
</table>
