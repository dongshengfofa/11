<p>第1段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Bel boweth down, Nebo stoopeth, their idols were upon the beasts, and upon the cattle: your carriages were heavy loaden; they are a burden to the weary beast.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>都一同弯腰屈身，不能保全重驮，自己倒被掳去。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They stoop, they bow down together; they could not deliver the burden, but themselves are gone into captivity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>雅各家，以色列家一切剩馀的，要听我言，你们自从生下，就蒙我保抱，自从出胎，便蒙我怀搋。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hearken unto me, O house of Jacob, and all the remnant of the house of Israel, which are borne by me from the belly, which are carried from the womb:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>直到你们年老，我仍这样，直到你们发白，我仍怀搋，我已造作，也必保抱，我必怀抱，也必拯救。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And even to your old age I am he; and even to hoar hairs will I carry you: I have made, and I will bear; even I will carry, and will deliver you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们将谁与我相比，与我同等，可以与我比较，使我们相同呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To whom will ye liken me, and make me equal, and compare me, that we may be like?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那从囊中抓金子，用天平平银子的人，雇银匠制造神像，他们又俯伏，又叩拜。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They lavish gold out of the bag, and weigh silver in the balance, and hire a goldsmith; and he maketh it a god: they fall down, yea, they worship.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们将神像抬起，扛在肩上，安置在定处，他就站立，不离本位，人呼求他，他不能答应，也不能救人脱离患难。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They bear him upon the shoulder, they carry him, and set him in his place, and he standeth; from his place shall he not remove: yea, one shall cry unto him, yet can he not answer, nor save him out of his trouble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td>你们当想念这事，自己作大丈夫。悖逆的人哪，要心里思想。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Remember this, and shew yourselves men: bring it again to mind, O ye transgressors.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要追念上古的事，因为我是神，并无别神，我是神，再没有能比我的。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Remember the former things of old: for I am God, and there is none else; I am God, and there is none like me,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我从起初指明末后的事，从古时言明未成的事，说，我的筹算必立定，凡我所喜悦的，我必成就。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Declaring the end from the beginning, and from ancient times the things that are not yet done, saying, My counsel shall stand, and I will do all my pleasure:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Calling a ravenous bird from the east, the man that executeth my counsel from a far country: yea, I have spoken it, I will also bring it to pass; I have purposed it, I will also do it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
