	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou hast broken the yoke of his burden, and the staff of his shoulder, the rod of his oppressor, as in the day of Midian.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For every battle of the warrior is with confused noise, and garments rolled in blood; but this shall be with burning and fuel of fire.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
