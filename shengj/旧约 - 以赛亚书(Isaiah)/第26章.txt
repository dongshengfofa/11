<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第26章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Open ye the gates, that the righteous nation which keepeth the truth may enter in.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>坚心倚赖你的，你必保守他十分平安，因为他倚靠你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们当倚靠耶和华直到永远。因为耶和华是永久的磐石。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Trust ye in the LORD for ever: for in the LORD JEHOVAH is everlasting strength:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他使住高处的，与高城一并败落，将城拆毁，拆平直到尘埃。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For he bringeth down them that dwell on high; the lofty city, he layeth it low; he layeth it low, even to the ground; he bringeth it even to the dust.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要被脚践踏，就是被困苦人的脚，和穷乏人的脚践踏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The foot shall tread it down, even the feet of the poor, and the steps of the needy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人的道，是正直的，你为正直的主，必修平义人的路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The way of the just is uprightness: thou, most upright, dost weigh the path of the just.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我们在你行审判的路上等候你。我们心里所羡慕的是你的名，就是你那可记念的名。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yea, in the way of thy judgments, O LORD, have we waited for thee; the desire of our soul is to thy name, and to the remembrance of thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>夜间我心中羡慕你。我里面的灵切切寻求你。因为你在世上行审判的时候，地上的居民就学习公义。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>With my soul have I desired thee in the night; yea, with my spirit within me will I seek thee early: for when thy judgments are in the earth, the inhabitants of the world will learn righteousness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以恩惠待恶人，他仍不学习公义。在正直的地上，他必行事不义，也不注意耶和华的威严。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let favour be shewed to the wicked, yet will he not learn righteousness: in the land of uprightness will he deal unjustly, and will not behold the majesty of the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，你的手高举，他们仍然不看。却要看你为百姓发的热心，因而抱愧。并且有火烧灭你的敌人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>LORD, when thy hand is lifted up, they will not see: but they shall see, and be ashamed for their envy at the people; yea, the fire of thine enemies shall devour them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，你必派定我们得平安。因为我们所作的事，都是你给我们成就的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>LORD, thou wilt ordain peace for us: for thou also hast wrought all our works in us.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华我们的神阿，在你以外曾有别的主管辖我们，但我们专要倚靠你，题你的名。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O LORD our God, other lords beside thee have had dominion over us: but by thee only will we make mention of thy name.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们死了，必不能再活。他们去世，必不能再起。因为你刑罚他们，他们的名号就全然消灭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are dead, they shall not live; they are deceased, they shall not rise: therefore hast thou visited and destroyed them, and made all their memory to perish.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，你增添国民，你增添国民。你得了荣耀，又扩张地的四境。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast increased the nation, O LORD, thou hast increased the nation: thou art glorified: thou hadst removed it far unto all the ends of the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，他们在急难中寻求你。你的惩罚临到他们身上，他们就倾心吐胆祷告你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>LORD, in trouble have they visited thee, they poured out a prayer when thy chastening was upon them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>妇人怀孕，临产疼痛，在痛苦之中喊叫，耶和华阿，我们在你面前，也是如此。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Like as a woman with child, that draweth near the time of her delivery, is in pain, and crieth out in her pangs; so have we been in thy sight, O LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们也曾怀孕疼痛，所产的竟像风一样，我们在地上未曾行什么拯救的事。世上的居民也未曾败落。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>We have been with child, we have been in pain, we have as it were brought forth wind; we have not wrought any deliverance in the earth; neither have the inhabitants of the world fallen.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy dead men shall live, together with my dead body shall they arise. Awake and sing, ye that dwell in dust: for thy dew is as the dew of herbs, and the earth shall cast out the dead.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
