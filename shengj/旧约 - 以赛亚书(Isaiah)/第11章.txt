	<tr>

		<td>耶和华的灵必住在他身上，就是使他有智慧和聪明的灵，谋略和能力的灵，知识和敬畏耶和华的灵。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the spirit of the LORD shall rest upon him, the spirit of wisdom and understanding, the spirit of counsel and might, the spirit of knowledge and of the fear of the LORD;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他必以敬畏耶和华为乐。行审判不凭眼见，断是非也不凭耳闻。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And shall make him of quick understanding in the fear of the LORD: and he shall not judge after the sight of his eyes, neither reprove after the hearing of his ears:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>却要以公义审判贫穷人，以正直判断世上的谦卑人。以口中的杖击打世界。以嘴里的气杀戮恶人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But with righteousness shall he judge the poor, and reprove with equity for the meek of the earth: and he shall smite the earth with the rod of his mouth, and with the breath of his lips shall he slay the wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>公义必当他的腰带，信实必当他胁下的带子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And righteousness shall be the girdle of his loins, and faithfulness the girdle of his reins.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>豺狼必与绵羊羔同居，豹子与山羊羔同卧。少壮狮子，与牛犊，并肥畜同群。小孩子要牵引他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wolf also shall dwell with the lamb, and the leopard shall lie down with the kid; and the calf and the young lion and the fatling together; and a little child shall lead them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>牛必与熊同食。牛犊必与小熊同卧。狮子必吃草与牛一样。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the cow and the bear shall feed; their young ones shall lie down together: and the lion shall eat straw like the ox.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>吃奶的孩子必玩耍在虺蛇的洞口，断奶的婴儿必按手在毒蛇的穴上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the sucking child shall play on the hole of the asp, and the weaned child shall put his hand on the cockatrice' den.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在我圣山的遍处，这一切都不伤人，不害物。因为认识耶和华的知识要充满遍地，好像水充满洋海一般。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They shall not hurt nor destroy in all my holy mountain: for the earth shall be full of the knowledge of the LORD, as the waters cover the sea.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>到那日，耶西的根立作万民的大旗。外邦人必寻求他。他安息之所大有荣耀。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And in that day there shall be a root of Jesse, which shall stand for an ensign of the people; to it shall the Gentiles seek: and his rest shall be glorious.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>当那日，主必二次伸手救回自己百姓中所馀剩的，就是在亚述，埃及，巴忒罗，古实，以拦，示拿，哈马，并众海岛所剩下的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it shall come to pass in that day, that the Lord shall set his hand again the second time to recover the remnant of his people, which shall be left, from Assyria, and from Egypt, and from Pathros, and from Cush, and from Elam, and from Shinar, and from Hamath, and from the islands of the sea.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>他必向列国竖立大旗，招回以色列被赶散的人，又从地的四方聚集分散的犹大人。</td>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>
