<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第25章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>

		<td>耶和华阿，你是我的神。我要尊崇你，我要称赞你的名。因为你以忠信诚实行过奇妙的事。成就你古时所定的。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你使城变为乱堆，使坚固城变为荒场，使外邦人宫殿的城，不再为城，永远不再建造。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou hast made of a city an heap; of a defenced city a ruin: a palace of strangers to be no city; it shall never be built.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>所以刚强的民，必荣耀你。强暴之国的城，必敬畏你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore shall the strong people glorify thee, the city of the terrible nations shall fear thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为当强暴人催逼人的时候，如同暴风直吹墙壁，你就作贫穷人的保障，作困乏人急难中的保障，作躲暴风之处，作避炎热的阴凉。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou hast been a strength to the poor, a strength to the needy in his distress, a refuge from the storm, a shadow from the heat, when the blast of the terrible ones is as a storm against the wall.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要压制外邦人的喧哗，好像乾燥地的热气下落，禁止强暴人的凯歌，好像热气被云影消化。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou shalt bring down the noise of strangers, as the heat in a dry place; even the heat with the shadow of a cloud: the branch of the terrible ones shall be brought low.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>在这山上万军之耶和华，必为万民用肥甘设摆筵席，用陈酒和满髓的肥甘，并澄清的陈酒，设摆筵席。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And in this mountain shall the LORD of hosts make unto all people a feast of fat things, a feast of wines on the lees, of fat things full of marrow, of wines on the lees well refined.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

</table>

</table>
