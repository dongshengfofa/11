<p>第1段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>必有一人像避风所，和避暴雨的隐密处，又像河流在乾旱之地，像大磐石的影子在疲乏之地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And a man shall be as an hiding place from the wind, and a covert from the tempest; as rivers of water in a dry place, as the shadow of a great rock in a weary land.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那能看的人，眼不再昏迷，能听的人，耳必得听闻。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the eyes of them that see shall not be dim, and the ears of them that hear shall hearken.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>冒失人的心，必明白知识，结吧人的舌，必说话通快。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The heart also of the rash shall understand knowledge, and the tongue of the stammerers shall be ready to speak plainly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚顽人不再称为高明，吝啬人不再称为大方。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The vile person shall be no more called liberal, nor the churl said to be bountiful.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为愚顽人，必说愚顽话，心里想作罪孽，惯行亵渎的事，说错谬的话，攻击耶和华，使饥饿的人，无食可吃，使口渴的人，无水可喝。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the vile person will speak villany, and his heart will work iniquity, to practise hypocrisy, and to utter error against the LORD, to make empty the soul of the hungry, and he will cause the drink of the thirsty to fail.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>吝啬人所用的法子是恶的。他图谋恶计，用谎言毁灭谦卑人。穷乏人讲公理的时候，他也是这样行。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The instruments also of the churl are evil: he deviseth wicked devices to destroy the poor with lying words, even when the needy speaketh right.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But the liberal deviseth liberal things; and by liberal things shall he stand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>
