	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The burden of Babylon, which Isaiah the son of Amoz did see.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>应当在净光的山竖立大旗，向群众扬声招手，使他们进入贵胄的门。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lift ye up a banner upon the high mountain, exalt the voice unto them, shake the hand, that they may go into the gates of the nobles.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我吩咐我所挑出来的人。我招呼我的勇士，就是那矜夸高傲之辈，为要成就我怒中所定的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have commanded my sanctified ones, I have also called my mighty ones for mine anger, even them that rejoice in my highness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>山间有多人的声音，好像是大国人民。有许多国的民聚集哄囔的声音。这是万军之耶和华点齐军队，豫备打仗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The noise of a multitude in the mountains, like as of a great people; a tumultuous noise of the kingdoms of nations gathered together: the LORD of hosts mustereth the host of the battle.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们从远方来，从天边来，就是耶和华并他恼恨的兵器，要毁灭这全地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They come from a far country, from the end of heaven, even the LORD, and the weapons of his indignation, to destroy the whole land.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要哀号，因为耶和华的日子临近了。这日来到，好像毁灭从全能者来到。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Howl ye; for the day of the LORD is at hand; it shall come as a destruction from the Almighty.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以人手都必软弱，人心都必消化。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore shall all hands be faint, and every man's heart shall melt:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们必惊惶悲痛。愁苦必将他们抓住。他们疼痛，好像产难的妇人一样。彼此惊奇相看，脸如火焰。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they shall be afraid: pangs and sorrows shall take hold of them; they shall be in pain as a woman that travaileth: they shall be amazed one at another; their faces shall be as flames.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的日子临到，必有残忍，忿恨，烈怒。使这地荒凉，从其中除灭罪人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, the day of the LORD cometh, cruel both with wrath and fierce anger, to lay the land desolate: and he shall destroy the sinners thereof out of it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>天上得众星群宿，都不发光。日头一出，就变黑暗，月亮也不放光。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the stars of heaven and the constellations thereof shall not give their light: the sun shall be darkened in his going forth, and the moon shall not cause her light to shine.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必因邪恶，刑罚世界，因罪孽，刑罚恶人。使骄傲人的狂妄止息，制伏强暴人的狂傲。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will punish the world for their evil, and the wicked for their iniquity; and I will cause the arrogancy of the proud to cease, and will lay low the haughtiness of the terrible.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必使人比精金还少，使人比俄斐纯金更少。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will make a man more precious than fine gold; even a man than the golden wedge of Ophir.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我万军之耶和华在忿恨中发烈怒的日子，必使天震动，使地摇憾，离其本位。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore I will shake the heavens, and the earth shall remove out of her place, in the wrath of the LORD of hosts, and in the day of his fierce anger.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人必像被追赶的鹿，像无人收聚的羊，各归回本族，各逃到本土。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it shall be as the chased roe, and as a sheep that no man taketh up: they shall every man turn to his own people, and flee every one into his own land.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡被仇敌追上的，必被刺死。凡被捉住的，必被刀杀。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Every one that is found shall be thrust through; and every one that is joined unto them shall fall by the sword.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们的婴孩，必在他们眼前摔碎。他们的房屋，必被抢夺。他们的妻子，必被玷污。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Their children also shall be dashed to pieces before their eyes; their houses shall be spoiled, and their wives ravished.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必激动玛代人来攻击他们。玛代人不注重银子，也不喜爱金子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, I will stir up the Medes against them, which shall not regard silver; and as for gold, they shall not delight in it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们必用弓击碎少年人，不怜悯妇人所生的，眼也不顾惜孩子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Their bows also shall dash the young men to pieces; and they shall have no pity on the fruit of the womb; their eye shall not spare children.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>巴比伦素来为列国的荣耀，为迦勒底人所矜夸的华美，必像神所倾覆的所多玛蛾摩拉一样。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Babylon, the glory of kingdoms, the beauty of the Chaldees' excellency, shall be as when God overthrew Sodom and Gomorrah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It shall never be inhabited, neither shall it be dwelt in from generation to generation: neither shall the Arabian pitch tent there; neither shall the shepherds make their fold there.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>
