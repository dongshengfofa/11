<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第51章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要追想你们的祖宗亚伯拉罕，和生养你们的撒拉。因为亚伯拉罕独自一人的时候，我选召他，赐福与他，使他人数增多。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Look unto Abraham your father, and unto Sarah that bare you: for I called him alone, and blessed him, and increased him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华已经安慰锡安，和锡安一切的荒场，使旷野像伊甸，使沙漠像耶和华的园囿。在其中必有欢喜，快乐，感谢和歌唱的声音。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the LORD shall comfort Zion: he will comfort all her waste places; and he will make her wilderness like Eden, and her desert like the garden of the LORD; joy and gladness shall be found therein, thanksgiving, and the voice of melody.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的百姓阿，要向我留心。我的国民哪，要向我侧耳。因为训诲必从我而出，我必坚定我的公理为万民之光。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hearken unto me, my people; and give ear unto me, O my nation: for a law shall proceed from me, and I will make my judgment to rest for a light of the people.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的公义临近，我的救恩发出，我的膀臂要审判万民。海岛都要等候我，倚赖我的膀臂。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My righteousness is near; my salvation is gone forth, and mine arms shall judge the people; the isles shall wait upon me, and on mine arm shall they trust.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要向天举目，观看下地。因为天必像烟云消散，地必如衣服渐渐旧了。其上的居民，也要如此死亡。（如此死亡或作像蠓虫死亡）惟有我的救恩永远长存，我的公义也不废掉。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lift up your eyes to the heavens, and look upon the earth beneath: for the heavens shall vanish away like smoke, and the earth shall wax old like a garment, and they that dwell therein shall die in like manner: but my salvation shall be for ever, and my righteousness shall not be abolished.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>知道公义，将我训诲存在心中的民，要听我言不要怕人的辱骂，也不要因人的毁谤惊惶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hearken unto me, ye that know righteousness, the people in whose heart is my law; fear ye not the reproach of men, neither be ye afraid of their revilings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>

		<td>因为蛀虫必咬他们，好像咬衣服，虫子必咬他们，如同咬羊绒。惟有我的公义永远长存，我的救恩直到万代。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the moth shall eat them up like a garment, and the worm shall eat them like wool: but my righteousness shall be for ever, and my salvation from generation to generation.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

		<td>耶和华的膀臂阿，兴起，兴起，以能力为衣穿上，像古时的年日，上古的世代兴起一样。从前砍碎拉哈伯，刺透大鱼的，不是你吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Awake, awake, put on strength, O arm of the LORD; awake, as in the ancient days, in the generations of old. Art thou not it that hath cut Rahab, and wounded the dragon?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>

</table>
