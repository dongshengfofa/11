<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我说，正在我中年（或作晌午）之日，必进入阴间的门。我馀剩的年岁不得享受。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	<tr>

		<td>I said, I shall not see the LORD, even the LORD, in the land of the living: I shall behold man no more with the inhabitants of the world.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Mine age is departed, and is removed from me as a shepherd's tent: I have cut off like a weaver my life: he will cut me off with pining sickness: from day even to night wilt thou make an end of me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>
