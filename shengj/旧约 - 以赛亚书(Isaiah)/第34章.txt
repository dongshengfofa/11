<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 以赛亚书(Isaiah) 第34章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>列国阿，要近前来听。众民哪，要侧耳而听。地和其上所充满的，世界和其中一切所出的，都应当听。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Come near, ye nations, to hear; and hearken, ye people: let the earth hear, and all that is therein; the world, and all things that come forth of it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为耶和华向万国发忿怒，向他们的全军发烈怒，将他们灭尽，交出他们受杀戮。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the indignation of the LORD is upon all nations, and his fury upon all their armies: he hath utterly destroyed them, he hath delivered them to the slaughter.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>被杀的必然抛弃，尸首臭气上腾。诸山被他们的血融化。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Their slain also shall be cast out, and their stink shall come up out of their carcases, and the mountains shall be melted with their blood.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>天上的万象都要消没，天被卷起，好像书卷。其上的万象要残败，像葡萄树的叶子残败，又像无花果树的叶子残败一样</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And all the host of heaven shall be dissolved, and the heavens shall be rolled together as a scroll: and all their host shall fall down, as the leaf falleth off from the vine, and as a falling fig from the fig tree.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为我的刀在天上已经喝足。这刀必临到以东，和我所咒诅的民，要施行审判。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For my sword shall be bathed in heaven: behold, it shall come down upon Idumea, and upon the people of my curse, to judgment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的刀满了血，用脂油和羊羔公山羊的血，并公绵羊腰子的脂油滋润的。因为耶和华在波斯拉有献祭的事，在以东地大行杀戮。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The sword of the LORD is filled with blood, it is made fat with fatness, and with the blood of lambs and goats, with the fat of the kidneys of rams: for the LORD hath a sacrifice in Bozrah, and a great slaughter in the land of Idumea.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>野牛，牛犊，和公牛，要一同下来。他们的地喝醉了血，他们的尘土因脂油肥润。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the unicorns shall come down with them, and the bullocks with the bulls; and their land shall be soaked with blood, and their dust made fat with fatness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For it is the day of the LORD's vengeance, and the year of recompences for the controversy of Zion.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>昼夜总不熄灭，烟气永远上腾，必世世代代成为荒废，永永远远无人经过。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It shall not be quenched night nor day; the smoke thereof shall go up for ever: from generation to generation it shall lie waste; none shall pass through it for ever and ever.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>
