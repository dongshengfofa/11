<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第3章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我的敌人何其加增。有许多人起来攻击我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lord, how are they increased that trouble me! many are they that rise up against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有许多人议论我说，他得不着神的帮助。（细拉）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Many there be which say of my soul, There is no help for him in God. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但你耶和华是我四围的盾牌。是我的荣耀，又是叫我抬起头来的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But thou, O LORD, art a shield for me; my glory, and the lifter up of mine head.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我用我的声音求告耶和华，他就从他的圣山上应允我。（细拉）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I cried unto the LORD with my voice, and he heard me out of his holy hill. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我躺下睡觉。我醒着。耶和华都保佑我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I laid me down and slept; I awaked; for the LORD sustained me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>虽有成万的百姓来周围攻击我，我也不怕。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people, that have set themselves against me round about.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，求你起来。我的神阿，求你救我。因为你打了我一切仇敌的腮骨，敲碎了恶人的牙齿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God: for thou hast smitten all mine enemies upon the cheek bone; thou hast broken the teeth of the ungodly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>救恩属乎耶和华。愿你赐福给你的百姓。（细拉）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Salvation belongeth unto the LORD: thy blessing is upon thy people. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
