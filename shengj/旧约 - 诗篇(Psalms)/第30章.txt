<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第30章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我要尊崇你，因为你曾提拔我，不叫仇敌向我夸耀。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will extol thee, O LORD; for thou hast lifted me up, and hast not made my foes to rejoice over me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华我的神阿，我曾呼求你，你医治了我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O LORD my God, I cried unto thee, and thou hast healed me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，你曾把我的灵魂从阴间救上来，使我存活，不至于下坑。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O LORD, thou hast brought up my soul from the grave: thou hast kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的圣民哪，你们要歌颂他，称赞他可记念的圣名。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Sing unto the LORD, O ye saints of his, and give thanks at the remembrance of his holiness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为他的怒气不过是转眼之间。他的恩典乃是一生之久。一宿虽然有哭泣，早晨便必欢呼。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For his anger endureth but a moment; in his favour is life: weeping may endure for a night, but joy cometh in the morning.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>至于我，我凡事平顺，便说，我永不动摇。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And in my prosperity I said, I shall never be moved.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，你曾施恩，叫我的江山稳固。你掩了面，我就惊惶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>LORD, by thy favour thou hast made my mountain to stand strong: thou didst hide thy face, and I was troubled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我曾求告你。我向耶和华恳求，说，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I cried to thee, O LORD; and unto the LORD I made supplication.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我被害流血，下到坑中，有什么益处呢。尘土岂能称赞你，传说你的诚实吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>What profit is there in my blood, when I go down to the pit? Shall the dust praise thee? shall it declare thy truth?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，求你应允我，怜恤我。耶和华阿，求你帮助我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear, O LORD, and have mercy upon me: LORD, be thou my helper.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你已将我的哀哭变为跳舞，将我的麻衣脱去，给我披上喜乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast turned for me my mourning into dancing: thou hast put off my sackcloth, and girded me with gladness;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>好叫我的灵（原文作荣耀）歌颂你，并不住声。耶和华我的神阿，我要称谢你，直到永远。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To the end that my glory may sing praise to thee, and not be silent. O LORD my God, I will give thanks unto thee for ever.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
