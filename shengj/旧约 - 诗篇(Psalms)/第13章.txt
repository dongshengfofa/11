<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第13章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，你忘记我要到几时呢。要到永远吗。你掩面不顾我要到几时呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How long wilt thou forget me, O LORD? for ever? how long wilt thou hide thy face from me?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我心里筹算，终日愁苦，要到几时呢。我的仇敌升高压制我，要到几时呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How long shall I take counsel in my soul, having sorrow in my heart daily? how long shall mine enemy be exalted over me?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华我的神阿，求你看顾我，应允我，使我眼目光明，免得我沉睡至死。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Consider and hear me, O LORD my God: lighten mine eyes, lest I sleep the sleep of death;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>免得我的仇敌说，我胜了他。免得我的敌人在我摇动的时候喜乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed against him; and those that trouble me rejoice when I am moved.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但我倚靠你的慈爱。我的心因你的救恩快乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But I have trusted in thy mercy; my heart shall rejoice in thy salvation.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我要向耶和华歌唱，因他用厚恩待我。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will sing unto the LORD, because he hath dealt bountifully with me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
