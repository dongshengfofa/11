<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第14章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚顽人心里说，没有神。他们都是邪恶，行了可憎恶的事。没有一个人行善。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fool hath said in his heart, There is no God. They are corrupt, they have done abominable works, there is none that doeth good.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华从天上垂看世人，要看有明白的没有，有寻求神的没有。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD looked down from heaven upon the children of men, to see if there were any that did understand, and seek God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们都偏离正路，一同变为污秽。并没有行善的，连一个也没有。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are all gone aside, they are all together become filthy: there is none that doeth good, no, not one.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>作孽的都没有知识吗。他们吞吃我的百姓，如同吃饭一样，并不求告耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Have all the workers of iniquity no knowledge? who eat up my people as they eat bread, and call not upon the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们在那里大大的害怕，因为神在义人的族类中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There were they in great fear: for God is in the generation of the righteous.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们叫困苦人的谋算，变为羞辱。然而耶和华是他的避难所。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ye have shamed the counsel of the poor, because the LORD is his refuge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但愿以色列的救恩从锡安而出。耶和华救回他被掳的子民，那时雅各要快乐，以色列要欢喜。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Oh that the salvation of Israel were come out of Zion! when the LORD bringeth back the captivity of his people, Jacob shall rejoice, and Israel shall be glad.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
