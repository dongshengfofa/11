<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第15章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，谁能寄居你的帐幕。能能住在你的圣山。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lord, who shall abide in thy tabernacle? who shall dwell in thy holy hill?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>就是行为正直，作事公义，心里说实话的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that walketh uprightly, and worketh righteousness, and speaketh the truth in his heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他不以舌头谗谤人，不恶待朋友，也不随夥毁谤邻里。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that backbiteth not with his tongue, nor doeth evil to his neighbour, nor taketh up a reproach against his neighbour.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他眼中藐视匪类，却尊重那敬畏耶和华的人。他发了誓，虽然自己吃亏，也不更改。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In whose eyes a vile person is contemned; but he honoureth them that fear the LORD. He that sweareth to his own hurt, and changeth not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他不放债取利，不受贿赂以害无辜。行这些事的人，必永不动摇。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that putteth not out his money to usury, nor taketh reward against the innocent. He that doeth these things shall never be moved.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
