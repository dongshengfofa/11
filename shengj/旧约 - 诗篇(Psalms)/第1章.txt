<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第1章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不从恶人的计谋，不站罪人的道路，不坐亵慢人的座位。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟喜爱耶和华的律法，昼夜思想，这人便为有福。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But his delight is in the law of the LORD; and in his law doth he meditate day and night.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他要像一棵树栽在溪水旁，按时候结果子，叶子也不枯乾。凡他所作的，尽都顺利。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that bringeth forth his fruit in his season; his leaf also shall not wither; and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人并不是这样，乃像糠毗被风吹散。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The ungodly are not so: but are like the chaff which the wind driveth away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因此当审判的时候，恶人必站立不住，罪人在义人的会中，也是如此。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为耶和华知道义人的道路。恶人的道路，却必灭亡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the LORD knoweth the way of the righteous: but the way of the ungodly shall perish.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
