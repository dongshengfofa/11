<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第39章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>（大卫的诗，交与伶长耶杜顿）我曾说，我要谨慎我的言行，免得我舌头犯罪。恶人在我面前的时候，我要用嚼回环勒住我的口。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I said, I will take heed to my ways, that I sin not with my tongue: I will keep my mouth with a bridle, while the wicked is before me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我默然无声，连好话也不出口。我的愁苦就发动了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I was dumb with silence, I held my peace, even from good; and my sorrow was stirred.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的心在我里面发热。我默想的时候，火就烧起，我便用舌头说话。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My heart was hot within me, while I was musing the fire burned: then spake I with my tongue,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，求你叫我晓得我身之终，我的寿数几何，叫我知道我的生命不长。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>LORD, make me to know mine end, and the measure of my days, what it is: that I may know how frail I am.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你使我的年日，窄如手掌。我一生的年数，在你面前，如同无有。各人最稳妥的时候，真是全然虚幻。（细拉）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, thou hast made my days as an handbreadth; and mine age is as nothing before thee: verily every man at his best state is altogether vanity. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>世人行动实系幻影。他们忙乱，真是枉然。积蓄财宝，不知将来有谁收取。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Surely every man walketh in a vain shew: surely they are disquieted in vain: he heapeth up riches, and knoweth not who shall gather them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主阿，如今我等什么呢。我的指望在乎你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And now, Lord, what wait I for? my hope is in thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你救我脱离一切的过犯。不要使我受愚顽人的羞辱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Deliver me from all my transgressions: make me not the reproach of the foolish.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因我所遭遇的是出于你，我默然不语。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I was dumb, I opened not my mouth; because thou didst it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你把你的责罚，从我身上免去。因你手的责打，我便消灭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Remove thy stroke away from me: I am consumed by the blow of thine hand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你因人的罪恶，惩罚他的时候，叫他的笑容消灭，（的笑容或作所喜爱的）如衣被虫所咬。世人真是虚幻。（细拉）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When thou with rebukes dost correct man for iniquity, thou makest his beauty to consume away like a moth: surely every man is vanity. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿。求你听我的祷告，留心听我的呼求。我流泪，求你不要静默无声。因为我在你面前是客旅，是寄居的，像我列祖一般。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear my prayer, O LORD, and give ear unto my cry; hold not thy peace at my tears: for I am a stranger with thee, and a sojourner, as all my fathers were.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你宽容我，使我在去而不返之先，可以力量复原。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O spare me, that I may recover strength, before I go hence, and be no more.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
