<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第60章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>（大卫与两河间的亚兰并琐巴的亚兰争战的时候，约押转回，在盐谷攻击以东，杀了一万二千人。那时大卫作这金诗，叫人学习，交与伶长，调用为证的百合花）神阿，你丢弃了我们，使我们破坏；你向我们发怒，求你使我们复兴。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O God, thou hast cast us off, thou hast scattered us, thou hast been displeased; O turn thyself to us again.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你使地震动，而且崩裂。求你将裂口医好。因为地摇动。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast made the earth to tremble; thou hast broken it: heal the breaches thereof; for it shaketh.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你叫你的民遇见艰难。你叫我们喝那使人东倒西歪的酒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast shewed thy people hard things: thou hast made us to drink the wine of astonishment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你把旌旗赐给敬畏你的人，可以为真理杨起来。（细拉）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast given a banner to them that fear thee, that it may be displayed because of the truth. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你应允我们，用右手拯救我们，好叫你所亲爱的人得救。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That thy beloved may be delivered; save with thy right hand, and hear me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神已经指着他的圣洁说，（说或作应许我）我要欢乐。我要分开示剑，丈量疏割谷。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>基列是我的。玛拿西也是我的。以法莲是护卫我头的。犹大是我的杖。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the strength of mine head; Judah is my lawgiver;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>摩押是我的沭浴盆。我要向以东抛鞋。非利士阿，你还能因我欢呼吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Moab is my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe: Philistia, triumph thou because of me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谁能领我进坚固城。谁能引我到以东地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神阿，你不是丢弃了我们吗。神阿，你不和我们的军兵同去吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wilt not thou, O God, which hadst cast us off? and thou, O God, which didst not go out with our armies?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你帮助我们攻击敌人，因为人的帮助是枉然的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们倚靠神，才得施展大能，因为践踏我们敌人的就是他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
