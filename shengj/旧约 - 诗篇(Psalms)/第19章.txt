<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第19章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>诸天述说神的荣耀，穹苍传扬他的手段。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The heavens declare the glory of God; and the firmament sheweth his handywork.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这日到那日发出言语。这夜到那夜传出知识。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Day unto day uttereth speech, and night unto night sheweth knowledge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>无言无语，也无声音可听。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There is no speech nor language, where their voice is not heard.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的量带通遍天下，他的言语传到地极。神在其间为太阳安设帐幕。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Their line is gone out through all the earth, and their words to the end of the world. In them hath he set a tabernacle for the sun,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>太阳如同新郎出洞房，又如勇士欢然奔路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber, and rejoiceth as a strong man to run a race.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他从天这边出来，绕到天那边。没有一物被隐藏不得他的热气。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His going forth is from the end of the heaven, and his circuit unto the ends of it: and there is nothing hid from the heat thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的律法全备，能苏醒人心。耶和华的法度确定，能使愚人有智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The law of the LORD is perfect, converting the soul: the testimony of the LORD is sure, making wise the simple.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的训词正直，能快活人心。耶和华的命令清洁，能明亮人的眼目。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The statutes of the LORD are right, rejoicing the heart: the commandment of the LORD is pure, enlightening the eyes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的道理洁净，存到永远。耶和华的典章真实，全然公义。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fear of the LORD is clean, enduring for ever: the judgments of the LORD are true and righteous altogether.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>都比金子可羡慕，且比极多的精金可羡慕。比蜜甘甜，且比蜂房下滴的蜜甘甜。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>More to be desired are they than gold, yea, than much fine gold: sweeter also than honey and the honeycomb.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>况且你的仆人因此受警戒。守着这些便有大赏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Moreover by them is thy servant warned: and in keeping of them there is great reward.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谁能知道自己的错失呢。愿你赦免我隐而未现的过错。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who can understand his errors? cleanse thou me from secret faults.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你拦阻仆人，不犯任意妄为的罪。不容这罪辖制我。我便完全，免犯大罪。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Keep back thy servant also from presumptuous sins; let them not have dominion over me: then shall I be upright, and I shall be innocent from the great transgression.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华我的磐石，我的救赎主阿，愿我口中的言语，心中的意念，在你面前蒙悦纳。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let the words of my mouth, and the meditation of my heart, be acceptable in thy sight, O LORD, my strength, and my redeemer.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
