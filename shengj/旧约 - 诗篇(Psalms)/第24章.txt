<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第24章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>地和其中所充满的，世界和住在其间的，都属耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The earth is the LORD's, and the fulness thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他把地建立在海上，安定在大水之上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For he hath founded it upon the seas, and established it upon the floods.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谁能登耶和华的山。谁能站在他的圣所。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who shall ascend into the hill of the LORD? or who shall stand in his holy place?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>就是手洁心清，不向虚妄，起誓不怀诡诈的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that hath clean hands, and a pure heart; who hath not lifted up his soul unto vanity, nor sworn deceitfully.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他必蒙耶和华赐福，又蒙救他的神使他成义。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall receive the blessing from the LORD, and righteousness from the God of his salvation.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这是寻求耶和华的族类，是寻求你面的雅各（细拉）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>This is the generation of them that seek him, that seek thy face, O Jacob. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
