<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第41章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>（大卫的诗，交与伶长）眷顾贫穷的有福了。他遭难的日子，耶和华必搭救他。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Blessed is he that considereth the poor: the LORD will deliver him in time of trouble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华必保全他，使他存活。他必在地上享福。求你不要把他交给仇敌，逐其所愿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD will preserve him, and keep him alive; and he shall be blessed upon the earth: and thou wilt not deliver him unto the will of his enemies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他病重在榻，耶和华必扶持他。他在病中，你必给他铺床。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD will strengthen him upon the bed of languishing: thou wilt make all his bed in his sickness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我曾说，耶和华阿，求你怜恤我，医治我。因为我得罪了你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I said, LORD, be merciful unto me: heal my soul; for I have sinned against thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的仇敌用恶言议论我，说，他几时死，他的名才灭亡呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Mine enemies speak evil of me, When shall he die, and his name perish?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他来看我，就说假话。他心存奸恶，走到外边才说出来。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And if he come to see me, he speaketh vanity: his heart gathereth iniquity to itself; when he goeth abroad, he telleth it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>一切恨我的，都交头接耳的议论我。他们设计要害我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All that hate me whisper together against me: against me do they devise my hurt.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们说，有怪病贴在他身上。他已躺卧，必不能再起来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>An evil disease, say they, cleaveth fast unto him: and now that he lieth he shall rise up no more.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>连我知己的朋友，我所倚靠吃过我饭的，也用脚踢我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yea, mine own familiar friend, in whom I trusted, which did eat of my bread, hath lifted up his heel against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，求你怜恤我，使我起来，好报复他们。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But thou, O LORD, be merciful unto me, and raise me up, that I may requite them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因我的仇敌不得向我夸胜。我从此便知道你喜爱我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>By this I know that thou favourest me, because mine enemy doth not triumph over me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你因我纯正，就扶持我，使我永远站在你的面前。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And as for me, thou upholdest me in mine integrity, and settest me before thy face for ever.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华以色列的神，是应当称颂的，从亘古直到永远。阿们，阿们。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Blessed be the LORD God of Israel from everlasting, and to everlasting. Amen, and Amen.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
