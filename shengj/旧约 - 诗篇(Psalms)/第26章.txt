<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第26章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，求你为我伸冤，因我向来行事纯全，我又倚靠耶和华并不摇动。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Judge me, O LORD; for I have walked in mine integrity: I have trusted also in the LORD; therefore I shall not slide.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，求你察看我，试验我，熬炼我的肺腑心肠。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Examine me, O LORD, and prove me; try my reins and my heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为你的慈爱常在我眼前。我也按你的真理而行。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes: and I have walked in thy truth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我没有和虚谎人同坐。也不与瞒哄人同群。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have not sat with vain persons, neither will I go in with dissemblers.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我恨恶恶人的会，必不与恶人同坐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have hated the congregation of evil doers; and will not sit with the wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我要洗手表明无辜，才环绕你的祭坛。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine altar, O LORD:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我好发称谢的声音。也要述说你一切奇妙的作为。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving, and tell of all thy wondrous works.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我喜爱你所住的殿，和你显荣耀的居所。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>LORD, I have loved the habitation of thy house, and the place where thine honour dwelleth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要把我的灵魂和罪人一同除掉，不要把我的性命和流人血的一同除掉。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Gather not my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloody men:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们的手中有奸恶，右手满有贿赂。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In whose hands is mischief, and their right hand is full of bribes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>至于我，却要行事纯全。求你救赎我，怜恤我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity: redeem me, and be merciful unto me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的脚站在平坦地方。在众会中我要称颂耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My foot standeth in an even place: in the congregations will I bless the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
