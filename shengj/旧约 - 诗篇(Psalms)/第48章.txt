<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第48章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>（可拉后裔的诗歌）耶和华本为大，在我们神的城中，在他的圣山上，该受大赞美。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Great is the LORD, and greatly to be praised in the city of our God, in the mountain of his holiness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>锡安山，大君王的城，在北面居高华美，为全地所喜悦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Beautiful for situation, the joy of the whole earth, is mount Zion, on the sides of the north, the city of the great King.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神在其宫中自显为避难所。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>God is known in her palaces for a refuge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看哪，众王会合，一同经过。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For, lo, the kings were assembled, they passed by together.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们见了这城，就惊奇丧胆，急忙逃跑。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They saw it, and so they marvelled; they were troubled, and hasted away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们在那里被战兢疼痛抓住，好像产难的妇人一样。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Fear took hold upon them there, and pain, as of a woman in travail.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神阿，你用东风打破他施的船只。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou breakest the ships of Tarshish with an east wind.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们在万君之耶和华的城中，就是我们神的城中，所看见的，正如我们所听见的。神必坚立这城，直到永远。（细拉）。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As we have heard, so have we seen in the city of the LORD of hosts, in the city of our God: God will establish it for ever. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神阿，我们在你的殿中，想念你的慈爱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>We have thought of thy lovingkindness, O God, in the midst of thy temple.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神阿，你受的赞美，正与你的名相称，直到地极。你的右手满了公义。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>According to thy name, O God, so is thy praise unto the ends of the earth: thy right hand is full of righteousness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因你的判断，锡安山应当欢喜，犹大的城邑，应当快乐。（城邑原文作女子）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let mount Zion rejoice, let the daughters of Judah be glad, because of thy judgments.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们当周游锡安，四围旋绕，数点城楼，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Walk about Zion, and go round about her: tell the towers thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>细看他的外郭，察看他的宫殿，为要传说到后代。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Mark ye well her bulwarks, consider her palaces; that ye may tell it to the generation following.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
