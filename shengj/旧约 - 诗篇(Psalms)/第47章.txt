<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 诗篇(Psalms) 第47章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>（可拉后裔的诗，交与伶长）万民哪，你们都要拍掌。要用夸胜的声音向神呼喊。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O clap your hands, all ye people; shout unto God with the voice of triumph.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为耶和华至高者是可畏的。他是治理全地的大君王。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the LORD most high is terrible; he is a great King over all the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他叫万民服在我们以下，又叫列邦服在我们脚下。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall subdue the people under us, and the nations under our feet.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他为我们选择产业，就是他所爱之雅各的荣耀。（细拉）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall choose our inheritance for us, the excellency of Jacob whom he loved. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神上升，有喊声相送。耶和华上升，有角声相送。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>God is gone up with a shout, the LORD with the sound of a trumpet.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要向神歌颂，歌颂，向我们王歌颂，歌颂。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Sing praises to God, sing praises: sing praises unto our King, sing praises.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为神是全地的王。你们要用悟性歌颂。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For God is the King of all the earth: sing ye praises with understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神作王治理万国。神坐在他的圣宝座上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>God reigneth over the heathen: God sitteth upon the throne of his holiness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>列邦的君王聚集，要作亚伯拉罕之神的民。因为世界的盾牌，是属神的。他为至高。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The princes of the people are gathered together, even the people of the God of Abraham: for the shields of the earth belong unto God: he is greatly exalted.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
