<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 哈巴谷书(Habakkuk) 第3章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>先知哈巴谷的祷告，调用流离歌。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A prayer of Habakkuk the prophet upon Shigionoth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我听见你的名声（或作言语），就惧怕。耶和华阿，求你在这些年间复兴你的作为，在这些年间显明出来，在发怒的时候，以怜悯为念。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O LORD, I have heard thy speech, and was afraid: O LORD, revive thy work in the midst of the years, in the midst of the years make known; in wrath remember mercy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神从毯螅而来，圣者从巴兰山临到。（细拉）他的荣光遮蔽诸天，颂赞充满大地。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>God came from Teman, and the Holy One from mount Paran. Selah. His glory covered the heavens, and the earth was full of his praise.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的辉煌如同日光。从他手里射出光线。在其中藏着他的能力。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And his brightness was as the light; he had horns coming out of his hand: and there was the hiding of his power.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他前面有瘟疫流行，在他脚下有热症发出。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Before him went the pestilence, and burning coals went forth at his feet.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他站立，量了大地（或作使地震动），观看，赶散万民。永久的山崩裂，长存的岭塌陷。他的作为与古时一样。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He stood, and measured the earth: he beheld, and drove asunder the nations; and the everlasting mountains were scattered, the perpetual hills did bow: his ways are everlasting.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我见古珊的帐棚遭难，米甸的幔子战兢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I saw the tents of Cushan in affliction: and the curtains of the land of Midian did tremble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，你乘在马上，坐在得胜的车上，岂是不喜悦江河，向江河发怒气，向洋海发愤恨吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Was the LORD displeased against the rivers? was thine anger against the rivers? was thy wrath against the sea, that thou didst ride upon thine horses and thy chariots of salvation?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的弓全然显露。向众支派所起的誓，都是可信的。（细拉）你以江河分开大地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy bow was made quite naked, according to the oaths of the tribes, even thy word. Selah. Thou didst cleave the earth with rivers.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>山岭见你，无不战惧。大水泛滥过去，深渊发声，汹涌翻腾。（原文作向上举手）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The mountains saw thee, and they trembled: the overflowing of the water passed by: the deep uttered his voice, and lifted up his hands on high.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因你的箭射出发光，你的枪闪出光耀，日月都在本宫停住。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The sun and moon stood still in their habitation: at the light of thine arrows they went, and at the shining of thy glittering spear.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你发愤恨通行大地。发怒气责打列国，如同打粮。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou didst march through the land in indignation, thou didst thresh the heathen in anger.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你出来要拯救你的百姓，拯救你的受膏者，打破恶人家长的头。露出他的脚，（脚原文是根基），直到颈项。（细拉）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou wentest forth for the salvation of thy people, even for salvation with thine anointed; thou woundedst the head out of the house of the wicked, by discovering the foundation unto the neck. Selah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你用敌人的戈矛刺透他战士的头。他们来如旋风，要将我们分散。他们所喜爱的，是暗中吞吃贫民。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou didst strike through with his staves the head of his villages: they came out as a whirlwind to scatter me: their rejoicing was as to devour the poor secretly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你乘马践踏红海，就是践踏汹涌的大水。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou didst walk through the sea with thine horses, through the heap of great waters.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我听见耶和华的声音，身体战兢，嘴唇发颤，骨中朽烂。我在所立之处战兢。我只可安静等候灾难之日临到，犯境之民上来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When I heard, my belly trembled; my lips quivered at the voice: rottenness entered into my bones, and I trembled in myself, that I might rest in the day of trouble: when he cometh up unto the people, he will invade them with his troops.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>虽然无花果树不发旺，葡萄树不结果，橄榄树也不效力，田地不出粮食，圈中绝了羊，棚内也没有牛。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Although the fig tree shall not blossom, neither shall fruit be in the vines; the labour of the olive shall fail, and the fields shall yield no meat; the flock shall be cut off from the fold, and there shall be no herd in the stalls:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>然而我要因耶和华欢欣，因救我的神喜乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yet I will rejoice in the LORD, I will joy in the God of my salvation.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主耶和华是我的力量。他使我的脚快如母鹿的蹄。又使我稳行在高处。这歌交与伶长，用丝弦的乐器。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD God is my strength, and he will make my feet like hinds' feet, and he will make me to walk upon mine high places. To the chief singer on my stringed instruments.
</td>
	</tr>
</table>
