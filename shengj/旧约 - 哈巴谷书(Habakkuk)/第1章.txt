<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 哈巴谷书(Habakkuk) 第1章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>先知哈巴谷所得的默示。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The burden which Habakkuk the prophet did see.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他说，耶和华阿，我呼求你，你不应允，要到几时呢。我因强暴哀求你，你还不拯救。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O LORD, how long shall I cry, and thou wilt not hear! even cry out unto thee of violence, and thou wilt not save!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你为何使我看见罪孽。你为何看着奸恶而不理呢。毁灭和强暴在我面前。又起了争端和相斗的事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Why dost thou shew me iniquity, and cause me to behold grievance? for spoiling and violence are before me: and there are that raise up strife and contention.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因此律法放松，公理也不显明。恶人围困义人。所以公理显然颠倒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore the law is slacked, and judgment doth never go forth: for the wicked doth compass about the righteous; therefore wrong judgment proceedeth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华说，你们要向列国中观看，大大惊奇。因为在你们的时候，我行一件事，虽有人告诉你们，你们总是不信。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold ye among the heathen, and regard, and wonder marvellously: for I will work a work in your days, which ye will not believe, though it be told you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必兴起迦勒底人，就是那残忍暴躁之民，通行遍地，占据那不属自己的住处。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For, lo, I raise up the Chaldeans, that bitter and hasty nation, which shall march through the breadth of the land, to possess the dwellingplaces that are not theirs.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他威武可畏。判断和势力，都任意发出。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are terrible and dreadful: their judgment and their dignity shall proceed of themselves.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的马比豹更快，比晚上的豺狼更猛。马兵踊跃争先，都从远方而来，他们飞跑如鹰抓食。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Their horses also are swifter than the leopards, and are more fierce than the evening wolves: and their horsemen shall spread themselves, and their horsemen shall come from far; they shall fly as the eagle that hasteth to eat.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>都为行强暴而来。定住脸面向前，将掳掠的人聚集，多如尘沙。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They shall come all for violence: their faces shall sup up as the east wind, and they shall gather the captivity as the sand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们讥诮君王，笑话首领，嗤笑一切保障，筑垒攻取。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they shall scoff at the kings, and the princes shall be a scorn unto them: they shall deride every strong hold; for they shall heap dust, and take it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他以自己的势力为神，像风猛然扫过，显为有罪。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then shall his mind change, and he shall pass over, and offend, imputing this his power unto his god.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华我的神，我的圣者阿，你不是从亘古而有吗。我们必不致死。耶和华阿，你派定他为要刑罚人。磐石阿，你设立他为要惩治人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Art thou not from everlasting, O LORD my God, mine Holy One? we shall not die. O LORD, thou hast ordained them for judgment; and, O mighty God, thou hast established them for correction.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你眼目清洁，不看邪僻，不看奸恶。行诡诈的，你为何看着不理呢。恶人吞灭比自己公义的，你为何静默不语呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou art of purer eyes than to behold evil, and canst not look on iniquity: wherefore lookest thou upon them that deal treacherously, and holdest thy tongue when the wicked devoureth the man that is more righteous than he?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你为何使人如海中的鱼，又如没有管辖的爬物呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And makest men as the fishes of the sea, as the creeping things, that have no ruler over them?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他用钩钩住，用网捕获，用拉网聚集他们。因此，他欢喜快乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They take up all of them with the angle, they catch them in their net, and gather them in their drag: therefore they rejoice and are glad.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>就向网献祭，向网烧香。因他由此得肥美的分，和富裕的食物。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore they sacrifice unto their net, and burn incense unto their drag; because by them their portion is fat, and their meat plenteous.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他岂可屡次倒空网罗，将列国的人时常杀戮，毫不顾惜呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Shall they therefore empty their net, and not spare continually to slay the nations?</td>
	</tr>
</table>
