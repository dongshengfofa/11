	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I know it is so of a truth: but how should man be just with God?</td>

</table>

		<td>若愿意与他争辩，千中之一也不能回答。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If he will contend with him, he cannot answer him one of a thousand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td>他心里有智慧，且大有能力。谁向神刚硬而得亨通呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He is wise in heart, and mighty in strength: who hath hardened himself against him, and hath prospered?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which removeth the mountains, and they know not: which overturneth them in his anger.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which shaketh the earth out of her place, and the pillars thereof tremble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他吩咐日头不出来，就不出来，又封闭众星。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which commandeth the sun, and it riseth not; and sealeth up the stars.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他独自铺张苍天，步行在海浪之上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which alone spreadeth out the heavens, and treadeth upon the waves of the sea.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which maketh Arcturus, Orion, and Pleiades, and the chambers of the south.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他行大事，不可测度，行奇事，不可胜数。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which doeth great things past finding out; yea, and wonders without number.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他从我旁边经过，我却不看见。他在我面前行走，我倒不知觉。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lo, he goeth by me, and I see him not: he passeth on also, but I perceive him not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他夺取，谁能阻挡。谁敢问他，你做什么。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, he taketh away, who can hinder him? who will say unto him, What doest thou?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神必不收回他的怒气。扶助拉哈伯的，屈身在他以下。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If God will not withdraw his anger, the proud helpers do stoop under him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>既是这样，我怎敢回答他，怎敢选择言语与他辩论呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How much less shall I answer him, and choose out my words to reason with him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我虽有义，也不回答他。只要向那审判我的恳求。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whom, though I were righteous, yet would I not answer, but I would make supplication to my judge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我若呼吁，他应允我。我仍不信他真听我的声音。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If I had called, and he had answered me; yet would I not believe that he had hearkened unto my voice.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For he breaketh me with a tempest, and multiplieth my wounds without cause.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He will not suffer me to take my breath, but filleth me with bitterness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>若论力量，他真有能力。若论审判，他说谁能将我传来呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If I speak of strength, lo, he is strong: and if of judgment, who shall set me a time to plead?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If I justify myself, mine own mouth shall condemn me: if I say, I am perfect, it shall also prove me perverse.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though I were perfect, yet would I not know my soul: I would despise my life.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
