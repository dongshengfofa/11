<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第11章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>拿玛人琐法回答说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then answered Zophar the Naamathite, and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这许多的言语岂不该回答吗。多嘴多舌的人岂可称为义吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Should not the multitude of words be answered? and should a man full of talk be justified?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你夸大的话，岂能使人不作声吗ⅶ你戏笑的时候，岂没有人叫你害羞吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Should thy lies make men hold their peace? and when thou mockest, shall no man make thee ashamed?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你说，我的道理纯全，我在你眼前洁净。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou hast said, My doctrine is pure, and I am clean in thine eyes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟愿神说话，愿他开口攻击你。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But oh that God would speak, and open his lips against thee;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>并将智慧的奥秘指示你，他有诸般的智识。所以当知道神追讨你，比你罪孽该得的还少。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And that he would shew thee the secrets of wisdom, that they are double to that which is! Know therefore that God exacteth of thee less than thine iniquity deserveth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你考察，就能测透神吗。你岂能尽情测透全能者吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Canst thou by searching find out God? canst thou find out the Almighty unto perfection?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的智慧高于天，你还能做什么。深于阴间，你还能知道什么。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is as high as heaven; what canst thou do? deeper than hell; what canst thou know?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>其量比地长，比海宽。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The measure thereof is longer than the earth, and broader than the sea.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他若经过，将人拘禁，招人受审，谁能阻挡他呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If he cut off, and shut up, or gather together, then who can hinder him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他本知道虚妄的人。人的罪孽，他虽不留意，还是无所不见。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For he knoweth vain men: he seeth wickedness also; will he not then consider it?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>空虚的人却毫无知识。人生在世好像野驴的驹子。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For vain man would be wise, though man be born like a wild ass's colt.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你若将心安正，又向主举手。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thou prepare thine heart, and stretch out thine hands toward him;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你手里若有罪孽，就当远远地除掉，也不容非义住在你帐棚之中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If iniquity be in thine hand, put it far away, and let not wickedness dwell in thy tabernacles.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那时，你必仰起脸来毫无斑点。你也必坚固，无所惧怕。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For then shalt thou lift up thy face without spot; yea, thou shalt be stedfast, and shalt not fear:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你必忘记你的苦楚，就是想起也如流过去的水一样。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Because thou shalt forget thy misery, and remember it as waters that pass away:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你在世的日子要比正午更明，虽有黑暗仍像早晨。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And thine age shall be clearer than the noonday: thou shalt shine forth, thou shalt be as the morning.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你因有指望就必稳固，也必四围巡查，坦然安息。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And thou shalt be secure, because there is hope; yea, thou shalt dig about thee, and thou shalt take thy rest in safety.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你躺卧，无人惊吓，且有许多人向你求恩。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Also thou shalt lie down, and none shall make thee afraid; yea, many shall make suit unto thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但恶人的眼目必要失明。他们无路可逃。他们的指望就是气绝。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But the eyes of the wicked shall fail, and they shall not escape, and their hope shall be as the giving up of the ghost.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
