<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第16章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>约伯回答说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then Job answered and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这样的话我听了许多。你们安慰人，反叫人愁烦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have heard many such things: miserable comforters are ye all.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>虚空的言语有穷尽吗。有什么话惹动你回答呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Shall vain words have an end? or what emboldeneth thee that thou answerest?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我也能说你们那样的话。你们若处在我的境遇，我也会联络言语攻击你们，又能向你们摇头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I also could speak as ye do: if your soul were in my soul's stead, I could heap up words against you, and shake mine head at you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但我必用口坚固你们，用嘴消解你们的忧愁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But I would strengthen you with my mouth, and the moving of my lips should asswage your grief.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我虽说话，忧愁仍不得消解。我虽停住不说，忧愁就离开我吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though I speak, my grief is not asswaged: and though I forbear, what am I eased?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但现在神使我困倦，使亲友远离我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But now he hath made me weary: thou hast made desolate all my company.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又抓住我，作见证攻击我。我身体的枯瘦，也当面见证我的不是。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And thou hast filled me with wrinkles, which is a witness against me: and my leanness rising up in me beareth witness to my face.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主发怒撕裂我，逼迫我，向我切齿。我的敌人怒目看我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He teareth me in his wrath, who hateth me: he gnasheth upon me with his teeth; mine enemy sharpeneth his eyes upon me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们向我开口，打我的脸羞辱我，聚会攻击我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They have gaped upon me with their mouth; they have smitten me upon the cheek reproachfully; they have gathered themselves together against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神把我交给不敬虔的人，把我扔到恶人的手中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>God hath delivered me to the ungodly, and turned me over into the hands of the wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我素来安逸，他折断我，掐住我的颈项，把我摔碎。又立我为他的箭靶子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I was at ease, but he hath broken me asunder: he hath also taken me by my neck, and shaken me to pieces, and set me up for his mark.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的弓箭手四面围绕我。他破裂我的肺腑，并不留情，把我的胆倾倒在地上，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His archers compass me round about, he cleaveth my reins asunder, and doth not spare; he poureth out my gall upon the ground.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>将我破裂又破裂，如同勇士向我直闯。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He breaketh me with breach upon breach, he runneth upon me like a giant.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我缝麻布在我皮肤上，把我的角放在尘土中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have sewed sackcloth upon my skin, and defiled my horn in the dust.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的脸因哭泣发紫，在我的眼皮上有死荫。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My face is foul with weeping, and on my eyelids is the shadow of death;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的手中却无强暴。我的祈祷也是清洁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Not for any injustice in mine hands: also my prayer is pure.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>地阿，不要遮盖我的血。不要阻挡我的哀求。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O earth, cover not thou my blood, and let my cry have no place.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>现今，在天有我的见证，在上有我的中保。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Also now, behold, my witness is in heaven, and my record is on high.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的朋友讥诮我，我却向神眼泪汪汪。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My friends scorn me: but mine eye poureth out tears unto God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿人得与神辩白，如同人与朋友辩白一样。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O that one might plead for a man with God, as a man pleadeth for his neighbour!</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>因为再过几年，我必走那往而不返之路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When a few years are come, then I shall go the way whence I shall not return.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
