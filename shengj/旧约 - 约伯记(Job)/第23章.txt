<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第23章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then Job answered and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>如今我的哀告还算为悖逆。我的责罚比我的唉哼还重。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Even to day is my complaint bitter: my stroke is heavier than my groaning.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟愿我能知道在那里可以寻见神，能到他的台前。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Oh that I knew where I might find him! that I might come even to his seat!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我就在他面前将我的案件陈明，满口辩白。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I would order my cause before him, and fill my mouth with arguments.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必知道他回答我的言语，明白他向我所说的话。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I would know the words which he would answer me, and understand what he would say unto me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他岂用大能与我争辩吗。必不这样，他必理会我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Will he plead against me with his great power? No; but he would put strength in me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他那里，正直人可以与他辩论。这样，我必永远脱离那审判我的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There the righteous might dispute with him; so should I be delivered for ever from my judge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>只是，我往前行，他不在那里，往后退，也不能见他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, I go forward, but he is not there; and backward, but I cannot perceive him:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他在左边行事，我却不能看见，在右边隐藏，我也不能见他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>On the left hand, where he doth work, but I cannot behold him: he hideth himself on the right hand, that I cannot see him:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>然而他知道我所行的路。他试炼我之后，我必如精金。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But he knoweth the way that I take: when he hath tried me, I shall come forth as gold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我脚追随他的步履。我谨守他的道，并不偏离。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My foot hath held his steps, his way have I kept, and not declined.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他嘴唇的命令，我未曾背弃。我看重他口中的言语，过于我需用的饮食。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Neither have I gone back from the commandment of his lips; I have esteemed the words of his mouth more than my necessary food.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>只是他心志已定，谁能使他转意呢。他心里所愿的，就行出来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But he is in one mind, and who can turn him? and what his soul desireth, even that he doeth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他向我所定的，就必做成。这类的事他还有许多。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For he performeth the thing that is appointed for me: and many such things are with him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以我在他面前惊惶，我思念这事，便惧怕他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore am I troubled at his presence: when I consider, I am afraid of him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
