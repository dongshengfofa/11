<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第12章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>约伯回答说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Job answered and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们真是子民哪，你们死亡，智慧也就灭没了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>No doubt but ye are the people, and wisdom shall die with you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但我也有聪明，与你们一样，并非不及你们。你们所说的，谁不知道呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But I have understanding as well as you; I am not inferior to you: yea, who knoweth not such things as these?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我这求告神，蒙他应允的人，竟成了朋友所讥笑的。公义完全人，竟受了人的讥笑。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I am as one mocked of his neighbour, who calleth upon God, and he answereth him: the just upright man is laughed to scorn.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>安逸的人，心里藐视灾祸。这灾祸常常等待滑脚的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that is ready to slip with his feet is as a lamp despised in the thought of him that is at ease.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>强盗的帐棚兴旺，惹神的人稳固，神多将财物送到他们手中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The tabernacles of robbers prosper, and they that provoke God are secure; into whose hand God bringeth abundantly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你且问走兽，走兽必指教你。又问空中的飞鸟，飞鸟必告诉你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But ask now the beasts, and they shall teach thee; and the fowls of the air, and they shall tell thee:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>或与地说话，地必指教你。海中的鱼也必向你说明。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Or speak to the earth, and it shall teach thee: and the fishes of the sea shall declare unto thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看这一切，谁不知道是耶和华的手做成的呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who knoweth not in all these that the hand of the LORD hath wrought this?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡活物的生命，和人类的气息，都在他手中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In whose hand is the soul of every living thing, and the breath of all mankind.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耳朵岂不试验言语，正如上膛尝食物吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Doth not the ear try words? and the mouth taste his meat?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>年老的有智慧，寿高的有知识。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>With the ancient is wisdom; and in length of days understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在神有智慧和能力，他有谋略和知识。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>With him is wisdom and strength, he hath counsel and understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他拆毁的，就不能再建造。他捆住人，便不得开释。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, he breaketh down, and it cannot be built again: he shutteth up a man, and there can be no opening.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他把水留住，水便枯乾。他再发出水来，水就翻地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, he withholdeth the waters, and they dry up: also he sendeth them out, and they overturn the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他有能力和智慧。被诱惑的，与诱惑人的，都是属他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>With him is strength and wisdom: the deceived and the deceiver are his.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He poureth contempt upon princes, and weakeneth the strength of the mighty.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He discovereth deep things out of darkness, and bringeth out to light the shadow of death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He increaseth the nations, and destroyeth them: he enlargeth the nations, and straiteneth them again.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They grope in the dark without light, and he maketh them to stagger like a drunken man.</td>

</table>
