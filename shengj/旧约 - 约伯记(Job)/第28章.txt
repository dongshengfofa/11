<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第28章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Iron is taken out of the earth, and brass is molten out of the stone.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人为黑暗定界限，查究幽暗阴翳的石头，直到极处。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He setteth an end to darkness, and searcheth out all perfection: the stones of darkness, and the shadow of death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The flood breaketh out from the inhabitant; even the waters forgotten of the foot: they are dried up, they are gone away from men.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As for the earth, out of it cometh bread: and under it is turned up as it were fire.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>
