<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第27章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>约伯接着说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Moreover Job continued his parable, and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神夺去我的理，全能者使我心中愁苦。我指着永生的神起誓。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As God liveth, who hath taken away my judgment; and the Almighty, who hath vexed my soul;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的生命尚在我里面，神所赐呼吸之气，仍在我的鼻孔内。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the while my breath is in me, and the spirit of God is in my nostrils;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的嘴决不说非义之言，我的舌也不说诡诈之语。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My lips shall not speak wickedness, nor my tongue utter deceit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我断不以你们为是，我至死必不以自己为不正。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>God forbid that I should justify you: till I die I will not remove mine integrity from me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我持定我的义，必不放松。在世的日子，我心必不责备我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My righteousness I hold fast, and will not let it go: my heart shall not reproach me so long as I live.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿我的仇敌如恶人一样。愿那起来攻击我的，如不义之人一般。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let mine enemy be as the wicked, and he that riseth up against me as the unrighteous.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不敬虔的人虽然得利，神夺取其命的时候，还有什么指望呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For what is the hope of the hypocrite, though he hath gained, when God taketh away his soul?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>患难临到他，神岂能听他的呼求。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Will God hear his cry when trouble cometh upon him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他岂以全能者为乐，随时求告神呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Will he delight himself in the Almighty? will he always call upon God?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神的作为，我要指教你们。全能者所行的，我也不隐瞒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will teach you by the hand of God: that which is with the Almighty will I not conceal.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们自己也都见过，为何全然变为虚妄呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, all ye yourselves have seen it; why then are ye thus altogether vain?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神为恶人所定的分，强暴人从全能者所得的报（报原文作产业）乃是这样。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>This is the portion of a wicked man with God, and the heritage of oppressors, which they shall receive of the Almighty.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>倘或他的儿女增多，还是被刀所杀。他的子孙必不得饱食。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If his children be multiplied, it is for the sword: and his offspring shall not be satisfied with bread.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>他所遗留的人必死而埋葬，他的寡妇也不哀哭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Those that remain of him shall be buried in death: and his widows shall not weep.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他虽积蓄银子如尘沙，预备衣服如泥土。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though he heap up silver as the dust, and prepare raiment as the clay;</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He buildeth his house as a moth, and as a booth that the keeper maketh.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The rich man shall lie down, but he shall not be gathered: he openeth his eyes, and he is not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
