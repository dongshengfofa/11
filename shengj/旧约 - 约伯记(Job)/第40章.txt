</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Once have I spoken; but I will not answer: yea, twice; but I will proceed no further.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>于是，耶和华从旋风中回答约伯说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then answered the LORD unto Job out of the whirlwind, and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要如勇士束腰。我问你，你可以指示我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Gird up thy loins now like a man: I will demand of thee, and declare thou unto me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你岂可废弃我所拟定的。岂可定我有罪，好显自己为义吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wilt thou also disannul my judgment? wilt thou condemn me, that thou mayest be righteous?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你有神那样的膀臂吗。你能像他发雷声吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hast thou an arm like God? or canst thou thunder with a voice like him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td>你要以荣耀庄严为妆饰，以尊荣威严为衣服。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Deck thyself now with majesty and excellency; and array thyself with glory and beauty.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

</table>
