<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第5章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For wrath killeth the foolish man, and envy slayeth the silly one.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我曾见愚妄人扎下根，但我忽然咒诅他的住处。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have seen the foolish taking root: but suddenly I cursed his habitation.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的儿女远离稳妥的地步，在城门口被压，并无人搭救。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His children are far from safety, and they are crushed in the gate, neither is there any to deliver them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的庄稼有饥饿的人吃尽了，就是在荆棘里的也抢去了。他的财宝有网罗张口吞灭了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whose harvest the hungry eateth up, and taketh it even out of the thorns, and the robber swalloweth up their substance.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>祸患原不是从土中出来。患难也不是从地里发生。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Although affliction cometh not forth of the dust, neither doth trouble spring out of the ground;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yet man is born unto trouble, as the sparks fly upward.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I would seek unto God, and unto God would I commit my cause:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>他行大事不可测度，行奇事不可胜数。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which doeth great things and unsearchable; marvellous things without number:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who giveth rain upon the earth, and sendeth waters upon the fields:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To set up on high those that be low; that those which mourn may be exalted to safety.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

		<td>破坏狡猾人的计谋，使他们所谋的不得成就。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He disappointeth the devices of the crafty, so that their hands cannot perform their enterprise.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他叫有智慧的中了自己的诡计，使狡诈人的计谋速速灭亡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He taketh the wise in their own craftiness: and the counsel of the froward is carried headlong.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

		<td>他们白昼遇见黑暗，午间摸索如在夜间。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They meet with darkness in the day time, and grope in the noonday as in the night.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

</table>

</table>
