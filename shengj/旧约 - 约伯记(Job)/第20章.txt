	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>我已听见那羞辱我，责备我的话。我的悟性叫我回答。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have heard the check of my reproach, and the spirit of my understanding causeth me to answer.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你岂不知亘古以来，自从人生在地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Knowest thou not this of old, since man was placed upon earth,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人夸胜是暂时的，不敬虔人的喜乐，不过转眼之间吗。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That the triumphing of the wicked is short, and the joy of the hypocrite but for a moment?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的尊荣虽达到天上，头虽顶到云中，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though his excellency mount up to the heavens, and his head reach unto the clouds;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他终必灭亡，像自己的粪一样。素来见他的人要说，他在那里呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yet he shall perish for ever like his own dung: they which have seen him shall say, Where is he?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他必飞去如梦，不再寻见，速被赶去，如夜间的异象。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall fly away as a dream, and shall not be found: yea, he shall be chased away as a vision of the night.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>亲眼见过他的，必不再见他。他的本处也再见不着他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The eye also which saw him shall see him no more; neither shall his place any more behold him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的儿女要求穷人的恩。他的手要赔还不义之财。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His children shall seek to please the poor, and his hands shall restore their goods.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的骨头虽然有青年之力，却要和他一同躺卧在尘土中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His bones are full of the sin of his youth, which shall lie down with him in the dust.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他口内虽以恶为甘甜，藏在舌头底下。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though wickedness be sweet in his mouth, though he hide it under his tongue;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>爱恋不舍，含在口中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though he spare it, and forsake it not; but keep it still within his mouth:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>他的食物在肚里却要化为酸，在他里面成为虺蛇的恶毒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yet his meat in his bowels is turned, it is the gall of asps within him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他吞了财宝，还要吐出。神要从他腹中掏出来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He hath swallowed down riches, and he shall vomit them up again: God shall cast them out of his belly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他必吸饮虺蛇的毒气。蝮蛇的舌头也必杀他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall suck the poison of asps: the viper's tongue shall slay him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall not see the rivers, the floods, the brooks of honey and butter.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That which he laboured for shall he restore, and shall not swallow it down: according to his substance shall the restitution be, and he shall not rejoice therein.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Because he hath oppressed and hath forsaken the poor; because he hath violently taken away an house which he builded not;</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall flee from the iron weapon, and the bow of steel shall strike him through.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is drawn, and cometh out of the body; yea, the glittering sword cometh out of his gall: terrors are upon him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
