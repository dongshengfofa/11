<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第10章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我厌烦我的性命，必由着自己述说我的哀情。因心里苦恼，我要说话。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My soul is weary of my life; I will leave my complaint upon myself; I will speak in the bitterness of my soul.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>对神说，不要定我有罪。要指示我，你为何与我争辩。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will say unto God, Do not condemn me; shew me wherefore thou contendest with me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你手所造的，你又欺压，又藐视，却光照恶人的计谋。这事你以为美吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Is it good unto thee that thou shouldest oppress, that thou shouldest despise the work of thine hands, and shine upon the counsel of the wicked?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的眼岂是肉眼，你查看岂像人查看吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hast thou eyes of flesh? or seest thou as man seeth?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的日子岂像人的日子，你的年岁岂像人的年岁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Are thy days as the days of man? are thy years as man's days,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>就追问我的罪孽，寻察我的罪过吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That thou enquirest after mine iniquity, and searchest after my sin?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>其实，你知道我没有罪恶，并没有能救我脱离你手的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou knowest that I am not wicked; and there is none that can deliver out of thine hand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的手创造我，造就我的四肢百体，你还要毁灭我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thine hands have made me and fashioned me together round about; yet thou dost destroy me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你记念制造我如抟泥一般，你还要使我归于麈土吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Remember, I beseech thee, that thou hast made me as the clay; and wilt thou bring me into dust again?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你不是倒出我来好像奶，使我凝结如同奶饼吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hast thou not poured me out as milk, and curdled me like cheese?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你以皮和肉为衣给我穿上，用骨与筋把我全体联络。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast clothed me with skin and flesh, and hast fenced me with bones and sinews.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你将生命和慈爱赐给我，你也眷顾保全我的心灵。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast granted me life and favour, and thy visitation hath preserved my spirit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>然而，你待我的这些事早已藏在你心里，我知道你久有此意。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And these things hast thou hid in thine heart: I know that this is with thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我若犯罪，你就察看我，并不赦免我的罪孽。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If I sin, then thou markest me, and thou wilt not acquit me from mine iniquity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我若行恶，便有了祸。我若为义，也不敢抬头。正是满心羞愧，眼见我的苦情。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If I be wicked, woe unto me; and if I be righteous, yet will I not lift up my head. I am full of confusion; therefore see thou mine affliction;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我若昂首自得，你就追捕我如狮子，又在我身上显出奇能。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For it increaseth. Thou huntest me as a fierce lion: and again thou shewest thyself marvellous upon me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你重立见证攻击我，向我加增恼怒，如军兵更换着攻击我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou renewest thy witnesses against me, and increasest thine indignation upon me; changes and war are against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你为何使我出母胎呢。不如我当时气绝，无人得见我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wherefore then hast thou brought me forth out of the womb? Oh that I had given up the ghost, and no eye had seen me!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这样，就如没有我一般，一出母胎就被送入坟墓。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I should have been as though I had not been; I should have been carried from the womb to the grave.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的日子不是甚少吗。求你停手宽容我，叫我在往而不返之先，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Are not my days few? cease then, and let me alone, that I may take comfort a little,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>就是往黑暗，和死荫之地以先，可以稍得畅快。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Before I go whence I shall not return, even to the land of darkness and the shadow of death;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那地甚是幽暗，是死荫混沌之地。那里的光好像幽暗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A land of darkness, as darkness itself; and of the shadow of death, without any order, and where the light is as darkness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
