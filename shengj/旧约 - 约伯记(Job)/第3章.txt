<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第3章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>此后，约伯开口咒诅自己的生日，说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>After this opened Job his mouth, and cursed his day.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿我生的那日，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Job spake, and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>*和说怀了男胎的那夜都灭没。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let the day perish wherein I was born, and the night in which it was said, There is a man child conceived.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿那日变为黑暗。愿神不从上面寻梢它。愿亮光不照于其上。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let that day be darkness; let not God regard it from above, neither let the light shine upon it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿黑暗和死荫索取那日。愿密云停在其上。愿日蚀恐吓它。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let darkness and the shadow of death stain it; let a cloud dwell upon it; let the blackness of the day terrify it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿那夜被幽暗夺取，不在年中的日子同乐，也不入月中的数目。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As for that night, let darkness seize upon it; let it not be joined unto the days of the year, let it not come into the number of the months.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿那夜没有生育，其间也没有欢乐的声音。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lo, let that night be solitary, let no joyful voice come therein.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿那咒诅日子且能惹动鳄鱼的，咒诅那夜。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let them curse it that curse the day, who are ready to raise up their mourning.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿那夜黎明的星宿变为黑暗，盼亮却不亮，也不见早晨的光线（光线原文作眼皮）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let the stars of the twilight thereof be dark; let it look for light, but have none; neither let it see the dawning of the day:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因没有把怀我胎的门关闭，也没有将患难对我的眼隐藏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Because it shut not up the doors of my mother's womb, nor hid sorrow from mine eyes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我为何不出母胎而死。为何不出母腹绝气。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Why died I not from the womb? why did I not give up the ghost when I came out of the belly?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>为何有膝接收我。为何有奶哺养我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Why did the knees prevent me? or why the breasts that I should suck?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不然，我就早已躺卧安睡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For now should I have lain still and been quiet, I should have slept: then had I been at rest,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>和地上为自己重造荒邱的君王，谋士。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>With kings and counsellors of the earth, which built desolate places for themselves;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>或与有金子，将银子装满了房屋的王子一同安息。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Or with princes that had gold, who filled their houses with silver:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>或像隐而未现，不到期而落的胎，归于无有，如同未见光的婴孩。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Or as an hidden untimely birth I had not been; as infants which never saw light.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在那里恶人止息搅扰，困乏人得享安息。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There the wicked cease from troubling; and there the weary be at rest.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>被囚的人同得安逸，不听见督工的声音。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There the prisoners rest together; they hear not the voice of the oppressor.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>大小都在那里。奴仆脱离主人的辖制。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The small and great are there; and the servant is free from his master.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>受患难的人，为何有光赐给他呢。心中愁苦的人，为何有生命赐给他呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wherefore is light given to him that is in misery, and life unto the bitter in soul;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们切望死，却不得死。求死，胜于求隐藏的珍宝。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which long for death, but it cometh not; and dig for it more than for hid treasures;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们寻见坟墓就快乐，极其欢喜。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which rejoice exceedingly, and are glad, when they can find the grave?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人的道路既然遮隐，神又把他四面围困，为何有光赐给他呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Why is light given to a man whose way is hid, and whom God hath hedged in?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我未曾吃饭就发出叹息。我唉哼的声音涌出如水。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For my sighing cometh before I eat, and my roarings are poured out like the waters.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因我所恐惧的临到我身，我所惧怕的迎我而来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the thing which I greatly feared is come upon me, and that which I was afraid of is come unto me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第26段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我不得安逸，不得平静，也不得安息，却有患难来到。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I was not in safety, neither had I rest, neither was I quiet; yet trouble came.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
