<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第33章</strong></p>

</table>

	</tr>

		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My words shall be of the uprightness of my heart: and my lips shall utter knowledge clearly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, my terror shall not make thee afraid, neither shall my hand be heavy upon thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>你所说的，我听见了，也听见你的言语，说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>
