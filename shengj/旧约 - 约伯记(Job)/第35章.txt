<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第35章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以利户又说，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Elihu spake moreover, and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你以为有理，或以为你的公义胜于神的公义，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thinkest thou this to be right, that thou saidst, My righteousness is more than God's?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>才说这与我有什么益处。我不犯罪比犯罪有什么好处呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou saidst, What advantage will it be unto thee? and, What profit shall I have, if I be cleansed from my sin?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我要回答你，和在你这里的朋友。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will answer thee, and thy companions with thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要向天观看，瞻望那高于你的穹苍。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Look unto the heavens, and see; and behold the clouds which are higher than thou.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你若犯罪，能使神受何害呢。你的过犯加增，能使神受何损呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thou sinnest, what doest thou against him? or if thy transgressions be multiplied, what doest thou unto him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你若是公义，还能加增他什么呢。他从你手里还接受什么呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thou be righteous, what givest thou him? or what receiveth he of thine hand?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的过恶，或能害你这类的人。你的公义，或能叫世人得益处。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy wickedness may hurt a man as thou art; and thy righteousness may profit the son of man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人因多受欺压就哀求，因受能者的辖制（辖制原文作膀臂）便求救。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>By reason of the multitude of oppressions they make the oppressed to cry: they cry out by reason of the arm of the mighty.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>却无人说，造我的神在那里。他使人夜间歌唱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But none saith, Where is God my maker, who giveth songs in the night;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>教训我们胜于地上的走兽，使我们有聪明胜于空中的飞鸟。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who teacheth us more than the beasts of the earth, and maketh us wiser than the fowls of heaven?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们在那里，因恶人的骄傲呼求，却无人答应。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There they cry, but none giveth answer, because of the pride of evil men.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>虚妄的呼求，神必不垂听。全能者也必不眷顾。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Surely God will not hear vanity, neither will the Almighty regard it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>何况你说，你不得见他。你的案件在他面前，你等候他吧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Although thou sayest thou shalt not see him, yet judgment is before him; therefore trust thou in him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但如今因他未曾发怒降罚，也不甚理会狂傲，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But now, because it is not so, he hath visited in his anger; yet he knoweth it not in great extremity:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以约伯开口说虚妄的话，多发无知识的言语。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore doth Job open his mouth in vain; he multiplieth words without knowledge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
