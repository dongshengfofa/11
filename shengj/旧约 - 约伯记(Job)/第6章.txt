<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟愿我的烦恼称一称，我一切的灾害放在天平里。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Oh that my grief were throughly weighed, and my calamity laid in the balances together!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>现今都比海沙更重，所以我的言语急躁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For now it would be heavier than the sand of the sea: therefore my words are swallowed up.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因全能者的箭射入我身，其毒，我的灵喝尽了。神的惊吓摆阵攻击我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the arrows of the Almighty are within me, the poison whereof drinketh up my spirit: the terrors of God do set themselves in array against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>野驴有草岂能叫唤，牛有料，岂能吼叫。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Doth the wild ass bray when he hath grass? or loweth the ox over his fodder?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>物淡而无盐岂可吃吗，蛋青有什么滋味呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Can that which is unsavoury be eaten without salt? or is there any taste in the white of an egg?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看为可厌的食物，我心不肯挨近。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The things that my soul refused to touch are as my sorrowful meat.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟愿我得着所求的，愿神赐我所切望的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Oh that I might have my request; and that God would grant me the thing that I long for!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>就是愿神把我压碎，伸手将我剪除。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Even that it would please God to destroy me; that he would let loose his hand, and cut me off!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我因没有违弃那圣者的言语，就仍以此为安慰，在不止息的痛苦中还可踊跃。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then should I yet have comfort; yea, I would harden myself in sorrow: let him not spare; for I have not concealed the words of the Holy One.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我有什么气力使我等候。我有什么结局使我忍耐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>What is my strength, that I should hope? and what is mine end, that I should prolong my life?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的气力岂是石头的气力。我的肉身岂是铜的呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Is my strength the strength of stones? or is my flesh of brass?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
