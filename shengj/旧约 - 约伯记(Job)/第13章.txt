<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第13章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lo, mine eye hath seen all this, mine ear hath heard and understood it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们所知道的，我也知道，并非不及你们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>What ye know, the same do I know also: I am not inferior unto you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我真要对全能者说话。我愿与神理论。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Surely I would speak to the Almighty, and I desire to reason with God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们是编造谎言的，都是无用的医生。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But ye are forgers of lies, ye are all physicians of no value.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟愿你们全然不作声。这就算为你们的智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O that ye would altogether hold your peace! and it should be your wisdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>请你们听我的辩论，留心听我口中的分诉。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear now my reasoning, and hearken to the pleadings of my lips.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要为神说不义的话吗，为他说诡诈的言语吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Will ye speak wickedly for God? and talk deceitfully for him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要为神徇情吗，要为他争论吗。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Will ye accept his person? will ye contend for God?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他查出你们来，这岂是好吗。人欺哄人，你们也要照样欺哄他吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Is it good that he should search you out? or as one man mocketh another, do ye so mock him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们若暗中徇情，他必要责备你们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He will surely reprove you, if ye do secretly accept persons.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的尊荣岂不叫你们惧怕吗。他的惊吓岂不临到你们吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Shall not his excellency make you afraid? and his dread fall upon you?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们以为可记念的箴言是炉灰的箴言。你们以为可靠的坚垒是淤泥的坚垒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Your remembrances are like unto ashes, your bodies to bodies of clay.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们不要作声，任凭我吧。让我说话，无论如何我都承当。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hold your peace, let me alone, that I may speak, and let come on me what will.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我何必把我的肉挂在牙上，将我的命放在手中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wherefore do I take my flesh in my teeth, and put my life in mine hand?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他必杀我。我虽无指望，然而我在他面前还要辩明我所行的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though he slay me, yet will I trust in him: but I will maintain mine own ways before him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这要成为我的拯救，因为不虔诚的人，不得到他面前。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He also shall be my salvation: for an hypocrite shall not come before him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear diligently my speech, and my declaration with your ears.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold now, I have ordered my cause; I know that I shall be justified.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who is he that will plead with me? for now, if I hold my tongue, I shall give up the ghost.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Only do not two things unto me: then will I not hide myself from thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Withdraw thine hand far from me: and let not thy dread make me afraid.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou writest bitter things against me, and makest me to possess the iniquities of my youth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou puttest my feet also in the stocks, and lookest narrowly unto all my paths; thou settest a print upon the heels of my feet.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
