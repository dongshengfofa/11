<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第4章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then Eliphaz the Temanite answered and said,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人若想与你说话，你就厌烦吗。但谁能忍住不说呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If we assay to commune with thee, wilt thou be grieved? but who can withhold himself from speaking?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你素来教导许多的人，又坚固软弱的手。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, thou hast instructed many, and thou hast strengthened the weak hands.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的言语曾扶助那将要跌倒的人。你又使软弱的膝稳固。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy words have upholden him that was falling, and thou hast strengthened the feeble knees.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但现在祸患临到你，你就昏迷，挨近你，你便惊惶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But now it is come upon thee, and thou faintest; it toucheth thee, and thou art troubled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的倚靠不是在你敬畏神吗。你的盼望不是在你行事纯正吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Is not this thy fear, thy confidence, thy hope, and the uprightness of thy ways?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>请你追想，无辜的人有谁灭亡。正直的人在何处剪除。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Remember, I pray thee, who ever perished, being innocent? or where were the righteous cut off?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>按我所见，耕罪孽，种毒害的人，都照样收割。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Even as I have seen, they that plow iniquity, and sow wickedness, reap the same.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神一出气，他们就灭亡。神一发怒，他们就消没。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>By the blast of God they perish, and by the breath of his nostrils are they consumed.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>狮子的吼叫，和猛狮的声音，尽都止息。少壮狮子的牙齿，也都敲掉。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The roaring of the lion, and the voice of the fierce lion, and the teeth of the young lions, are broken.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>老狮子因绝食而死。母狮之子也都离散。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The old lion perisheth for lack of prey, and the stout lion's whelps are scattered abroad.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我暗暗地得了默示。我耳朵也听其细微的声音。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now a thing was secretly brought to me, and mine ear received a little thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在思念夜中，异象之间，世人沉睡的时候。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In thoughts from the visions of the night, when deep sleep falleth on men,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恐惧，战兢临到我身，使我百骨打战。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Fear came upon me, and trembling, which made all my bones to shake.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有灵从我面前经过，我身上的毫毛直立。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then a spirit passed before my face; the hair of my flesh stood up:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那灵停住，我却不能辨其形状。有影像在我眼前。我在静默中听见有声音说，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It stood still, but I could not discern the form thereof: an image was before mine eyes, there was silence, and I heard a voice, saying,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>必死的人岂能比神公义吗。人岂能比造他的主洁净吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Shall mortal man be more just than God? shall a man be more pure than his maker?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主不信靠他的臣仆，并且指他的使者为愚昧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, he put no trust in his servants; and his angels he charged with folly:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>何况那住在土房，根基在尘土里，被蠹虫所毁坏的人呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How much less in them that dwell in houses of clay, whose foundation is in the dust, which are crushed before the moth?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>早晚之间，就被毁灭，永归无有，无人理会。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are destroyed from morning to evening: they perish for ever without any regarding it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>他帐棚的绳索岂不从中抽出来呢。他死，且是无智慧而死。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Doth not their excellency which is in them go away? they die, even without wisdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
