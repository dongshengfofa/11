<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第24章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>全能者既定期罚恶，为何不使认识他的人看见那日子呢。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Why, seeing times are not hidden from the Almighty, do they that know him not see his days?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有人挪移地界，抢夺群畜而牧养。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Some remove the landmarks; they violently take away flocks, and feed thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们拉去孤儿的驴，强取寡妇的牛为当头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They drive away the ass of the fatherless, they take the widow's ox for a pledge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们使穷人离开正道，世上的贫民尽都隐藏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They turn the needy out of the way: the poor of the earth hide themselves together.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这些贫穷人，如同野驴出到旷野，殷勤寻梢食物。他们靠着野地给儿女糊口，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, as wild asses in the desert, go they forth to their work; rising betimes for a prey: the wilderness yieldeth food for them and for their children.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>收割别人田间的禾稼，摘取恶人馀剩的葡萄。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They reap every one his corn in the field: and they gather the vintage of the wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>终夜赤身无衣，天气寒冷毫无遮盖，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They cause the naked to lodge without clothing, that they have no covering in the cold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在山上被大雨淋湿，因没有避身之处就挨近磐石。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are wet with the showers of the mountains, and embrace the rock for want of a shelter.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又有人从母怀中抢夺孤儿，强取穷人的衣服为当头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They pluck the fatherless from the breast, and take a pledge of the poor.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>使人赤身无衣，到处流行，且因饥饿扛抬禾捆，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They cause him to go naked without clothing, and they take away the sheaf from the hungry;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在那些人的围墙内造油，榨酒，自己还口渴。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which make oil within their walls, and tread their winepresses, and suffer thirst.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在多民的城内有人唉哼，受伤的人哀号。神却不理会（那恶人的愚妄。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Men groan from out of the city, and the soul of the wounded crieth out: yet God layeth not folly to them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>又有人背弃光明，不认识光明的道，不住在光明的路上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are of those that rebel against the light; they know not the ways thereof, nor abide in the paths thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>杀人的黎明起来，杀害困苦穷乏人，夜间又作盗贼。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The murderer rising with the light killeth the poor and needy, and in the night is as a thief.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>奸夫等候黄昏，说，必无眼能见我，就把脸蒙蔽。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The eye also of the adulterer waiteth for the twilight, saying, No eye shall see me: and disguiseth his face.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>盗贼黑夜挖窟窿，白日躲藏，并不认识光明。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the dark they dig through houses, which they had marked for themselves in the daytime: they know not the light.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们看早晨如幽暗，因为他们晓得幽暗的惊骇。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the morning is to them even as the shadow of death: if one know them, they are in the terrors of the shadow of death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这些恶人犹如浮萍快快飘去。他们所得的分在世上被咒诅。他们不得再走葡萄园的路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He is swift as the waters; their portion is cursed in the earth: he beholdeth not the way of the vineyards.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>乾旱炎热消没雪水，阴间也如此消没犯罪之辈。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Drought and heat consume the snow waters: so doth the grave those which have sinned.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>怀他的母（原文是胎）要忘记他。虫子要吃他，觉得甘甜。他不再被人记念。不义的人必如树折断。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The womb shall forget him; the worm shall feed sweetly on him; he shall be no more remembered; and wickedness shall be broken as a tree.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他恶待（或作他吞灭）不怀孕不生养的妇人，不善待寡妇。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He evil entreateth the barren that beareth not: and doeth not good to the widow.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>然而神用能力保全有势力的人，那性命难保的人仍然兴起。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He draweth also the mighty with his power: he riseth up, and no man is sure of life.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神使他们安稳，他们就有所倚靠。神的眼目也看顾他们的道路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though it be given him to be in safety, whereon he resteth; yet his eyes are upon their ways.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们被高举，不过片时就没有了。他们降为卑，被除灭，与众人一样，又如谷穗被割。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are exalted for a little while, but are gone and brought low; they are taken out of the way as all other, and cut off as the tops of the ears of corn.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>若不是这样，谁能证实我是说谎的，将我的言语驳为虚空呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And if it be not so now, who will make me a liar, and make my speech nothing worth?</td>
	</tr>
</table>
