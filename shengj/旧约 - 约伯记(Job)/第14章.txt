<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第14章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人为妇人所生，日子短少，多有患难。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Man that is born of a woman is of few days, and full of trouble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>出来如花，又被割下。飞去如影，不能存留。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He cometh forth like a flower, and is cut down: he fleeth also as a shadow, and continueth not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这样的人你岂睁眼看他吗。又叫我来受审吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And dost thou open thine eyes upon such an one, and bringest me into judgment with thee?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谁能使洁净之物出于污秽之中呢。无论谁也不能。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who can bring a clean thing out of an unclean? not one.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人的日子既然限定，他的月数在你那里，你也派定他的界限，使他不能越过。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Seeing his days are determined, the number of his months are with thee, thou hast appointed his bounds that he cannot pass;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>便求你转眼不看他，使他得歇息。直等他像雇工人完毕他的日子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Turn from him, that he may rest, till he shall accomplish, as an hireling, his day.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>树若被砍下，还可指望发芽，嫩枝生长不息。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For there is hope of a tree, if it be cut down, that it will sprout again, and that the tender branch thereof will not cease.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>其根虽然衰老在地里，干也死在土中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though the root thereof wax old in the earth, and the stock thereof die in the ground;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>及至得了水气，还要发芽，又长枝条，像新栽的树一样。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yet through the scent of water it will bud, and bring forth boughs like a plant.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但人死亡而消灭。他气绝，竟在何处呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But man dieth, and wasteth away: yea, man giveth up the ghost, and where is he?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>海中的水绝尽，江河消散乾涸。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As the waters fail from the sea, and the flood decayeth and drieth up:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人也是如此，躺下不再起来。等到天没有了，仍不得复醒，也不得从睡中唤醒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So man lieth down, and riseth not: till the heavens be no more, they shall not awake, nor be raised out of their sleep.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟愿你把我藏在阴间，存于隐密处，等你的忿怒过去。愿你为我定了日期，记念我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O that thou wouldest hide me in the grave, that thou wouldest keep me secret, until thy wrath be past, that thou wouldest appoint me a set time, and remember me!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人若死了岂能再活呢。我只要在我一切争战的日子，等我被释放（被释放或作改变）的时候来到。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If a man die, shall he live again? all the days of my appointed time will I wait, till my change come.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你呼叫，我便回答。你手所做的，你必羡慕。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou shalt call, and I will answer thee: thou wilt have a desire to the work of thine hands.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但如今你数点我的脚步，岂不窥察我的罪过吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For now thou numberest my steps: dost thou not watch over my sin?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的过犯被你封在囊中，也缝严了我的罪孽。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My transgression is sealed up in a bag, and thou sewest up mine iniquity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>山崩变为无有。磐石挪开原处。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And surely the mountain falling cometh to nought, and the rock is removed out of his place.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>水流消磨石头，所流溢的洗去地上的尘土。你也照样灭绝人的指望。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The waters wear the stones: thou washest away the things which grow out of the dust of the earth; and thou destroyest the hope of man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你攻击人常常得胜，使他去世。你改变他的容貌，叫他往而不回。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou prevailest for ever against him, and he passeth: thou changest his countenance, and sendest him away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他儿子得尊荣，他也不知道。降为卑，他也不觉得。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His sons come to honour, and he knoweth it not; and they are brought low, but he perceiveth it not of them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
