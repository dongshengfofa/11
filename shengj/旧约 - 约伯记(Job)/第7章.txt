<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第7章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人在世上岂无争战吗。他的日子不像雇工人的日子吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Is there not an appointed time to man upon earth? are not his days also like the days of an hireling?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>像奴仆切慕黑影，像雇工人盼望工价。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As a servant earnestly desireth the shadow, and as an hireling looketh for the reward of his work:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我也照样经过困苦的日月，夜间的疲乏为我而定。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So am I made to possess months of vanity, and wearisome nights are appointed to me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我躺卧的时候便说，我何时起来，黑夜就过去呢。我尽是反来覆去，直到天亮。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When I lie down, I say, When shall I arise, and the night be gone? and I am full of tossings to and fro unto the dawning of the day.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的肉体以虫子和尘土为衣。我的皮肤才收了口又重新破裂。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My flesh is clothed with worms and clods of dust; my skin is broken, and become loathsome.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的日子比梭更快，都消耗在无指望之中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My days are swifter than a weaver's shuttle, and are spent without hope.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你想念我，我的生命不过是一口气。我的眼睛必不再见福乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O remember that my life is wind: mine eye shall no more see good.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>观看我的人，他的眼必不再见我。你的眼目要看我，我却不在了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The eye of him that hath seen me shall see me no more: thine eyes are upon me, and I am not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>云彩消散而过。照样，人下阴间也不再上来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As the cloud is consumed and vanisheth away: so he that goeth down to the grave shall come up no more.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他不再回自己的家，故土也不再认识他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall return no more to his house, neither shall his place know him any more.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我不禁止我口。我灵愁苦，要发出言语。我心苦恼，要吐露哀情。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore I will not refrain my mouth; I will speak in the anguish of my spirit; I will complain in the bitterness of my soul.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我对神说，我岂是洋海，岂是大鱼，你竟防守我呢。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Am I a sea, or a whale, that thou settest a watch over me?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>若说，我的床必安慰我，我的榻必解释我的苦情。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When I say, My bed shall comfort me, my couch shall ease my complaint;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你就用梦惊骇我，用异象恐吓我。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then thou scarest me with dreams, and terrifiest me through visions:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>甚至我宁肯噎死，宁肯死亡，胜似留我这一身的骨头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So that my soul chooseth strangling, and death rather than my life.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我厌弃性命，不愿永活。你任凭我吧，因我的日子都是虚空。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I loathe it; I would not live alway: let me alone; for my days are vanity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人算什么，你竟看他为大，将他放在心上，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>What is man, that thou shouldest magnify him? and that thou shouldest set thine heart upon him?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>每早晨鉴察他，时刻试验他。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And that thou shouldest visit him every morning, and try him every moment?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你到何时才转眼不看我，才任凭我咽下唾沫呢。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How long wilt thou not depart from me, nor let me alone till I swallow down my spittle?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>鉴察人的主阿，我若有罪，于你何妨。为何以我当你的箭靶子，使我厌弃自己的性命。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have sinned; what shall I do unto thee, O thou preserver of men? why hast thou set me as a mark against thee, so that I am a burden to myself?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>为何不赦免我的过犯，除掉我的罪孽。我现今要躺卧在尘土中。你要殷勤地寻梢我，我却不在了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And why dost thou not pardon my transgression, and take away mine iniquity? for now shall I sleep in the dust; and thou shalt seek me in the morning, but I shall not be.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
