<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第32章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>于是这三个人，因约伯自以为义就，不再回答他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So these three men ceased to answer Job, because he was righteous in his own eyes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那时有布西人，兰族巴拉迦的儿子，以利户向约伯发怒。因约伯自以为义，不以神为义。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then was kindled the wrath of Elihu the son of Barachel the Buzite, of the kindred of Ram: against Job was his wrath kindled, because he justified himself rather than God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他又向约伯的三个朋友发怒。因为他们想不出回答的话来，仍以约伯为有罪。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Also against his three friends was his wrath kindled, because they had found no answer, and yet had condemned Job.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以利户要与约伯说话，就等候他们，因为他们比自己年老。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now Elihu had waited till Job had spoken, because they were elder than he.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以利户见这三个人口中无话回答，就怒气发作。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When Elihu saw that there was no answer in the mouth of these three men, then his wrath was kindled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>布西人，巴拉迦的儿子，以利户回答说，我年轻，你们老迈。因此我退让，不敢向你们陈说我的意见。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Elihu the son of Barachel the Buzite answered and said, I am young, and ye are very old; wherefore I was afraid, and durst not shew you mine opinion.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我说，年老的当先说话。寿高的当以智慧教训人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I said, Days should speak, and multitude of years should teach wisdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但在人里面有灵，全能者的气使趟有聪明。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But there is a spirit in man: and the inspiration of the Almighty giveth them understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

		<td>你们查究所要说的话。那时我等候你们的话，侧耳听你们的辩论。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, I waited for your words; I gave ear to your reasons, whilst ye searched out what to say.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	<tr>
