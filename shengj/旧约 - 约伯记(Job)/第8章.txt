</table>

	<tr>

		<td>Doth God pervert judgment? or doth the Almighty pervert justice?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thou wouldest seek unto God betimes, and make thy supplication to the Almighty;</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你若清洁正直，他必定为你起来，使你公义的居所兴旺。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thou wert pure and upright; surely now he would awake for thee, and make the habitation of thy righteousness prosperous.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

		<td>你起初虽然微小，终久必甚发达。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

</table>
