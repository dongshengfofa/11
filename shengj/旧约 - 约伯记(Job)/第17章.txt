<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约伯记(Job) 第17章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的心灵消耗，我的日子灭尽。坟墓为我预备好了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My breath is corrupt, my days are extinct, the graves are ready for me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>真有戏笑我的在我这里，我眼常见他们惹动我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Are there not mockers with me? and doth not mine eye continue in their provocation?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿主拿凭据给我，自己为我作保。在你以外谁肯与我击掌呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lay down now, put me in a surety with thee; who is he that will strike hands with me?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因你使他们心不明理，所以你必不高举他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou hast hid their heart from understanding: therefore shalt thou not exalt them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>控告他的朋友，以朋友为可抢夺的，连他儿女的眼睛也要失明。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that speaketh flattery to his friends, even the eyes of his children shall fail.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>神使我作了民中的笑谈。他们也吐唾沫在我脸上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He hath made me also a byword of the people; and aforetime I was as a tabret.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的眼睛因忧愁昏花。我的百体好像影儿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Mine eye also is dim by reason of sorrow, and all my members are as a shadow.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>正直人因此必惊奇。无辜的人，要兴起攻击不敬虔之辈。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Upright men shall be astonied at this, and the innocent shall stir up himself against the hypocrite.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>然而，义人要持守所行的道。手洁的人要力上加力。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The righteous also shall hold on his way, and he that hath clean hands shall be stronger and stronger.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>至于你们众人，可以再来辩论吧。你们中间，我找不着一个智慧人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But as for you all, do ye return, and come now: for I cannot find one wise man among you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的日子已经过了。我的谋算，我心所想望的已经断绝。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My days are past, my purposes are broken off, even the thoughts of my heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们以黑夜为白昼，说，亮光近乎黑暗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They change the night into day: the light is short because of darkness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我若盼望阴间为我的房屋，若下榻在黑暗中，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If I wait, the grave is mine house: I have made my bed in the darkness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>若对朽坏说，你是我的父。对虫说，你是我的母亲姊妹。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have said to corruption, Thou art my father: to the worm, Thou art my mother, and my sister.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这样，我的指望在那里呢。我所指望的谁能看见呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And where is now my hope? as for my hope, who shall see it?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>
