<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约拿书(Jonah) 第2章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>约拿在鱼腹中祷告耶和华他的神，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then Jonah prayed unto the LORD his God out of the fish's belly,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说，我遭遇患难求告耶和华，你就应允我。从阴间的深处呼求，你就俯听我的声音。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And said, I cried by reason of mine affliction unto the LORD, and he heard me; out of the belly of hell cried I, and thou heardest my voice.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你将我投下深渊，就是海的深处。大水环绕我。你的波浪洪涛都漫过我身。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou hadst cast me into the deep, in the midst of the seas; and the floods compassed me about: all thy billows and thy waves passed over me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我说，我从你眼前虽被驱逐，我仍要仰望你的圣殿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then I said, I am cast out of thy sight; yet I will look again toward thy holy temple.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>诸水环绕我，几乎淹没我。深渊围住我，海草缠绕我的头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The waters compassed me about, even to the soul: the depth closed me round about, the weeds were wrapped about my head.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我下到山根。地的门将我永远关住。耶和华我的神阿，你却将我的性命，从坑中救出来。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I went down to the bottoms of the mountains; the earth with her bars was about me for ever: yet hast thou brought up my life from corruption, O LORD my God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我心在我里面发昏的时候，我就想念耶和华。我的祷告进入你的圣殿，达到你的面前。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When my soul fainted within me I remembered the LORD: and my prayer came in unto thee, into thine holy temple.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那信奉虚无之神的人，离弃怜爱他们的主。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They that observe lying vanities forsake their own mercy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但我必用感谢的声音献祭与你。我所许的愿，我必偿还。救恩出于耶和华。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But I will sacrifice unto thee with the voice of thanksgiving; I will pay that that I have vowed. Salvation is of the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华吩咐鱼，鱼就把约拿吐在旱地上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the LORD spake unto the fish, and it vomited out Jonah upon the dry land.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
