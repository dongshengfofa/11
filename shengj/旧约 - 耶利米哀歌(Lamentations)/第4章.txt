<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 耶利米哀歌(Lamentations) 第4章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>黄金何其失光；纯金何其变色。圣所的石头倒在各市口上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How is the gold become dim! how is the most fine gold changed! the stones of the sanctuary are poured out in the top of every street.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>锡安宝贵的众子好比精金，现在何竟算为窑匠手所作的瓦瓶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The precious sons of Zion, comparable to fine gold, how are they esteemed as earthen pitchers, the work of the hands of the potter!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>野狗尚且把奶乳哺其子，我民的妇人倒成为残忍，好像旷野的鸵鸟一般。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Even the sea monsters draw out the breast, they give suck to their young ones: the daughter of my people is become cruel, like the ostriches in the wilderness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>吃奶孩子的舌头因乾渴贴住上膛。孩童求饼，无人擘给他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The tongue of the sucking child cleaveth to the roof of his mouth for thirst: the young children ask bread, and no man breaketh it unto them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>素来吃美好食物的，现今在街上变为孤寒。素来卧朱红褥子的，现今躺卧粪堆。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They that did feed delicately are desolate in the streets: they that were brought up in scarlet embrace dunghills.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>都因我众民的罪孽比所多玛的罪还大。所多玛虽然无人加手于她，还是转眼之间被倾覆。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the punishment of the iniquity of the daughter of my people is greater than the punishment of the sin of Sodom, that was overthrown as in a moment, and no hands stayed on her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>锡安的贵胄素来比雪纯净，比奶更白。他们的身体比红宝玉（或作珊瑚）更红，像光润的蓝宝石一样。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Her Nazarites were purer than snow, they were whiter than milk, they were more ruddy in body than rubies, their polishing was of sapphire:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>现在他们的面貌比煤炭更黑，以致在街上无人认识。他们的皮肤紧贴骨头，枯乾如同槁木。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Their visage is blacker than a coal; they are not known in the streets: their skin cleaveth to their bones; it is withered, it is become like a stick.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>饿死的不如被刀杀的，因为这是缺了田间的土产，就身体衰弱，渐渐消灭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They that be slain with the sword are better than they that be slain with hunger: for these pine away, stricken through for want of the fruits of the field.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>慈悲的妇人，当我众民被毁灭的时候，亲手煮自己的儿女作为食物。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The hands of the pitiful women have sodden their own children: they were their meat in the destruction of the daughter of my people.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华发怒成就他所定的，倒出他的烈怒。在锡安使火着起，烧毁锡安的根基。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD hath accomplished his fury; he hath poured out his fierce anger, and hath kindled a fire in Zion, and it hath devoured the foundations thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>地上的君王和世上的居民都不信敌人和仇敌能进耶路撒冷的城门。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The kings of the earth, and all the inhabitants of the world, would not have believed that the adversary and the enemy should have entered into the gates of Jerusalem.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这都因她先知的罪恶和祭司的罪孽。他们在城中流了义人的血。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the sins of her prophets, and the iniquities of her priests, that have shed the blood of the just in the midst of her,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>他们在街上如瞎子乱走，又被血玷污，以致人不能摸他们的衣服。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They have wandered as blind men in the streets, they have polluted themselves with blood, so that men could not touch their garments.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人向他们喊着说，不洁净的，躲开，躲开。不要挨近我。他们逃走飘流的时候，列国中有人说，他们不可仍在这里寄居。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They cried unto them, Depart ye; it is unclean; depart, depart, touch not: when they fled away and wandered, they said among the heathen, They shall no more sojourn there.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华发怒，将他们分散，不再眷顾他们。人不重看祭司，也不厚待长老。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The anger of the LORD hath divided them; he will no more regard them: they respected not the persons of the priests, they favoured not the elders.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>我们仰望人来帮助，以致眼目失明，还是枉然。我们所盼望的，竟盼望一个不能救人的国。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As for us, our eyes as yet failed for our vain help: in our watching we have watched for a nation that could not save us.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They hunt our steps, that we cannot go in our streets: our end is near, our days are fulfilled; for our end is come.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Our persecutors are swifter than the eagles of the heaven: they pursued us upon the mountains, they laid wait for us in the wilderness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>
