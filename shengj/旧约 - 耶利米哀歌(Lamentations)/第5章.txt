<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 耶利米哀歌(Lamentations) 第5章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Remember, O LORD, what is come upon us: consider, and behold our reproach.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Our inheritance is turned to strangers, our houses to aliens.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们是无父的孤儿。我们的母亲好像寡妇。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>We are orphans and fatherless, our mothers are as widows.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们出钱才得水喝。我们的柴是人卖给我们的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>We have drunken our water for money; our wood is sold unto us.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>追赶我们的，到了我们的颈项上。我们疲乏不得歇息。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Our necks are under persecution: we labour, and have no rest.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们投降埃及人和亚述人，为要得粮吃饱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>We have given the hand to the Egyptians, and to the Assyrians, to be satisfied with bread.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们列祖犯罪，而今不在了。我们担当他们的罪孽。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Our fathers have sinned, and are not; and we have borne their iniquities.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>奴仆辖制我们，无人救我们脱离他们的手。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Servants have ruled over us: there is none that doth deliver us out of their hand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为旷野的刀剑，我们冒着险才得粮食。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>We gat our bread with the peril of our lives because of the sword of the wilderness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Our skin was black like an oven because of the terrible famine.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>

</table>

	</tr>
</table>
