<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约&nbsp;-&nbsp;耶利米书(Jeremiah) 第28章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it came to pass the same year, in the beginning of the reign of Zedekiah king of Judah, in the fourth year, and in the fifth month, that Hananiah the son of Azur the prophet, which was of Gibeon, spake unto me in the house of the LORD, in the presence of the priests and of all the people, saying,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>万军之耶和华以色列的神如此说，我已经折断巴比伦王的轭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus speaketh the LORD of hosts, the God of Israel, saying, I have broken the yoke of the king of Babylon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>二年之内，我要将巴比伦王尼布甲尼撒从这地掠到巴比伦的器皿，就是耶和华殿中的一切器皿都带回此地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Within two full years will I bring again into this place all the vessels of the LORD's house, that Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon took away from this place, and carried them to Babylon:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我又要将犹大王约雅敬的儿子耶哥尼雅，和被掳到巴比伦去的一切犹大人，带回此地，因为我要折断巴比伦王的轭。这是耶和华说的。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will bring again to this place Jeconiah the son of Jehoiakim king of Judah, with all the captives of Judah, that went into Babylon, saith the LORD: for I will break the yoke of the king of Babylon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>先知耶利米当着祭司和站在耶和华殿里的众民对先知哈拿尼雅说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then the prophet Jeremiah said unto the prophet Hananiah in the presence of the priests, and in the presence of all the people that stood in the house of the LORD,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>阿们。愿耶和华如此行，愿耶和华成就你所预言的话，将耶和华殿中的器皿和一切被掳去的人从巴比伦带回此地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Even the prophet Jeremiah said, Amen: the LORD do so: the LORD perform thy words which thou hast prophesied, to bring again the vessels of the LORD's house, and all that is carried away captive, from Babylon into this place.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>然而我向你和众民耳中所要说的话，你应当听。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Nevertheless hear thou now this word that I speak in thine ears, and in the ears of all the people;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>从古以来，在你我以前的先知，向多国和大邦说预言，论到争战，灾祸，瘟疫的事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The prophets that have been before me and before thee of old prophesied both against many countries, and against great kingdoms, of war, and of evil, and of pestilence.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>先知预言的平安，到话语成就的时候，人便知道他真是耶和华所差来的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The prophet which prophesieth of peace, when the word of the prophet shall come to pass, then shall the prophet be known, that the LORD hath truly sent him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>于是，先知哈拿尼雅将先知耶利米颈项上的轭取下来，折断了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then Hananiah the prophet took the yoke from off the prophet Jeremiah's neck, and brake it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>哈拿尼雅又当着众民说，耶和华如此说，二年之内我必照样从列国人的颈项上折断巴比伦王尼布甲尼撒的轭。于是先知耶利米就走了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Hananiah spake in the presence of all the people, saying, Thus saith the LORD; Even so will I break the yoke of Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon from the neck of all nations within the space of two full years. And the prophet Jeremiah went his way.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>先知哈拿尼雅把先知耶利米颈项上的轭折断以后，耶和华的话临到耶利米说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then the word of the LORD came unto Jeremiah the prophet, after that Hananiah the prophet had broken the yoke from off the neck of the prophet Jeremiah, saying,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你去告诉哈拿尼雅说，耶和华如此说，你折断木轭，却换了铁轭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Go and tell Hananiah, saying, Thus saith the LORD; Thou hast broken the yokes of wood; but thou shalt make for them yokes of iron.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为万军之耶和华以色列的神如此说，我已将铁轭加在这些国的颈项上，使他们服事巴比伦王尼布甲尼撒，他们总要服事他。我也把田野的走兽给了他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thus saith the LORD of hosts, the God of Israel; I have put a yoke of iron upon the neck of all these nations, that they may serve Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon; and they shall serve him: and I have given him the beasts of the field also.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>于是先知耶利米对先知哈拿尼雅说，哈拿尼雅阿，你应当听。耶和华并没有差遣你，你竟使这百姓倚靠谎言。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then said the prophet Jeremiah unto Hananiah the prophet, Hear now, Hananiah; The LORD hath not sent thee; but thou makest this people to trust in a lie.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore thus saith the LORD; Behold, I will cast thee from off the face of the earth: this year thou shalt die, because thou hast taught rebellion against the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
