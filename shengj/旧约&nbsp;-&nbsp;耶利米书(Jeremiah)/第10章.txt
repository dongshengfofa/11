<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约&nbsp;-&nbsp;耶利米书(Jeremiah) 第10章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以色列家阿，要听耶和华对你们所说的话。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear ye the word which the LORD speaketh unto you, O house of Israel:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华如此说，你们不要效法列国的行为，也不要为天象惊惶，因列国为此事惊惶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus saith the LORD, Learn not the way of the heathen, and be not dismayed at the signs of heaven; for the heathen are dismayed at them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>众民的风俗是虚空的。他们在树林中用斧子砍伐一棵树，匠人用手工造成偶像。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the customs of the people are vain: for one cutteth a tree out of the forest, the work of the hands of the workman, with the axe.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们用金银妆饰它，用钉子和锤子钉稳，使它不动摇。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They deck it with silver and with gold; they fasten it with nails and with hammers, that it move not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>它好像棕树，是镟成的，不能说话，不能行走，必须有人抬着。你们不要怕它。它不能降祸，也无力降福。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are upright as the palm tree, but speak not: they must needs be borne, because they cannot go. Be not afraid of them; for they cannot do evil, neither also is it in them to do good.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，没有能比你的。你本为大，有大能大力的名。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Forasmuch as there is none like unto thee, O LORD; thou art great, and thy name is great in might.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>万国的王阿，谁不敬畏你。敬畏你本是合宜的。因为在列国的智慧人中，虽有政权的尊荣，也不能比你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who would not fear thee, O King of nations? for to thee doth it appertain: forasmuch as among all the wise men of the nations, and in all their kingdoms, there is none like unto thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们尽都是畜类，是愚昧的。偶像的训诲算什么呢。偶像不过是木头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But they are altogether brutish and foolish: the stock is a doctrine of vanities.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有银子打成片，是从他施带来的，并有从乌法来的金子，都是匠人和银匠的手工，又有蓝色紫色料的衣服，都是巧匠的工作。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Silver spread into plates is brought from Tarshish, and gold from Uphaz, the work of the workman, and of the hands of the founder: blue and purple is their clothing: they are all the work of cunning men.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟耶和华是真神，是活神，是永远的王。他一发怒，大地震动。他一恼恨，列国都担当不起。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But the LORD is the true God, he is the living God, and an everlasting king: at his wrath the earth shall tremble, and the nations shall not be able to abide his indignation.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要对他们如此说，不是那创造天地的神，必从地上从天下被除灭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus shall ye say unto them, The gods that have not made the heavens and the earth, even they shall perish from the earth, and from under these heavens.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华用能力创造大地，用智慧建立世界，用聪明铺张穹苍。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He hath made the earth by his power, he hath established the world by his wisdom, and hath stretched out the heavens by his discretion.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他一发声，空中便有多水激动。他使云雾从地极上腾。他造电随雨而闪，从他府库中带出风来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When he uttereth his voice, there is a multitude of waters in the heavens, and he causeth the vapours to ascend from the ends of the earth; he maketh lightnings with rain, and bringeth forth the wind out of his treasures.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>各人都成了畜类，毫无知识。各银匠都因他雕刻的偶像羞愧。他所铸的偶像本是虚假的，其中并无气息，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Every man is brutish in his knowledge: every founder is confounded by the graven image: for his molten image is falsehood, and there is no breath in them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>都是虚无的，是迷惑人的工作。到追讨的时候必被除灭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are vanity, and the work of errors: in the time of their visitation they shall perish.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>雅各的分不像这些，因他是造作万有的主。以色列也是他产业的支派，万军之耶和华是他的名。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The portion of Jacob is not like them: for he is the former of all things; and Israel is the rod of his inheritance: The LORD of hosts is his name.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>受围困的人哪，当收拾你的财物，从国中带出去。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Gather up thy wares out of the land, O inhabitant of the fortress.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为耶和华如此说，这时候，我必将此地的居民，好像用机弦甩出去，又必加害在他们身上，使他们觉悟。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thus saith the LORD, Behold, I will sling out the inhabitants of the land at this once, and will distress them, that they may find it so.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>民说，祸哉。我受损伤。我的伤痕极其重大。我却说，这真是我的痛苦，必须忍受。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Woe is me for my hurt! my wound is grievous: but I said, Truly this is a grief, and I must bear it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的帐棚毁坏。我的绳索折断。我的儿女离我出去，没有了。无人再支搭我的帐棚，挂起我的幔子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My tabernacle is spoiled, and all my cords are broken: my children are gone forth of me, and they are not: there is none to stretch forth my tent any more, and to set up my curtains.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为牧人都成为畜类，没有求问耶和华，所以不得顺利。他们的羊群也都分散。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the pastors are become brutish, and have not sought the LORD: therefore they shall not prosper, and all their flocks shall be scattered.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有风声。看哪，敌人来了。有大扰乱从北方出来，要使犹大城邑变为荒凉，成为野狗的住处。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, the noise of the bruit is come, and a great commotion out of the north country, to make the cities of Judah desolate, and a den of dragons.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我晓得人的道路不由自己，行路的人也不能定自己的脚步。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O LORD, I know that the way of man is not in himself: it is not in man that walketh to direct his steps.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，求你从宽惩治我，不要在你的怒中惩治我，恐怕使我归于无有。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O LORD, correct me, but with judgment; not in thine anger, lest thou bring me to nothing.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿你将忿怒倾在不认识你的列国中，和不求告你名的各族上。因为他们吞了雅各，不但吞了，而且灭绝，把他的住处变为荒场。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Pour out thy fury upon the heathen that know thee not, and upon the families that call not on thy name: for they have eaten up Jacob, and devoured him, and consumed him, and have made his habitation desolate.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
