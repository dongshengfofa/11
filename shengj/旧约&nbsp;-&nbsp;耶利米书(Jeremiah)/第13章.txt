	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>

		<td>耶和华说，腰带怎样紧贴人腰，照样，我也使以色列全家和犹大全家紧贴我，，好叫他们属我为子民，使我得名声，得颂赞，得荣耀。他们却不肯听。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For as the girdle cleaveth to the loins of a man, so have I caused to cleave unto me the whole house of Israel and the whole house of Judah, saith the LORD; that they might be unto me for a people, and for a name, and for a praise, and for a glory: but they would not hear.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>
