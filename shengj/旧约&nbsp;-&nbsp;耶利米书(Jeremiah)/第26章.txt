	<tr>

		<td>In the beginning of the reign of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah king of Judah came this word from the LORD, saying,</td>

</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus saith the LORD; Stand in the court of the LORD's house, and speak unto all the cities of Judah, which come to worship in the LORD's house, all the words that I command thee to speak unto them; diminish not a word:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>或者他们肯听从，各人回头离开恶道，使我后悔不将我因他们所行的恶，想要施行的灾祸降与他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If so be they will hearken, and turn every man from his evil way, that I may repent me of the evil, which I purpose to do unto them because of the evil of their doings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要对他们说，耶和华如此说，你们若不听从我，不遵行我设立在你们面前的律法，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith the LORD; If ye will not hearken to me, to walk in my law, which I have set before you,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不听我从早起来差遣到你们那里去我仆人众先知的话（你们还是没有听从），</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To hearken to the words of my servants the prophets, whom I sent unto you, both rising up early, and sending them, but ye have not hearkened;</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then will I make this house like Shiloh, and will make this city a curse to all the nations of the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But know ye for certain, that if ye put me to death, ye shall surely bring innocent blood upon yourselves, and upon this city, and upon the inhabitants thereof: for of a truth the LORD hath sent me unto you to speak all these words in your ears.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>
