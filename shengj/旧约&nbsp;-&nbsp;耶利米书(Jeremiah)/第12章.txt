<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约&nbsp;-&nbsp;耶利米书(Jeremiah) 第12章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，我与你争辩的时候，你显为义。但有一件，我还要与你理论，恶人的道路为何亨通呢。大行诡诈的为何得安逸呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Righteous art thou, O LORD, when I plead with thee: yet let me talk with thee of thy judgments: Wherefore doth the way of the wicked prosper? wherefore are all they happy that deal very treacherously?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你栽培了他们，他们也扎了根，长大，而且结果。他们的口是与你相近，心却与你远离。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast planted them, yea, they have taken root: they grow, yea, they bring forth fruit: thou art near in their mouth, and far from their reins.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华阿，你晓得我，看见我，察验我向你是怎样的心。求你将他们拉出来，好像将宰的羊，叫他们等候杀戮的日子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But thou, O LORD, knowest me: thou hast seen me, and tried mine heart toward thee: pull them out like sheep for the slaughter, and prepare them for the day of slaughter.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这地悲哀，通国的青草枯乾，要到几时呢。因其上居民的恶行，牲畜和飞鸟都灭绝了。他们曾说，他看不见我们的结局。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How long shall the land mourn, and the herbs of every field wither, for the wickedness of them that dwell therein? the beasts are consumed, and the birds; because they said, He shall not see our last end.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华说，你若与步行的人同跑，尚且觉累，怎能与马赛跑呢。你在平安之地，虽然安稳，在约旦河边的丛林要怎样行呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thou hast run with the footmen, and they have wearied thee, then how canst thou contend with horses? and if in the land of peace, wherein thou trustedst, they wearied thee, then how wilt thou do in the swelling of Jordan?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为连你弟兄和你父家都用奸诈待你。他们也在你后边大声喊叫，虽向你说好话，你也不要信他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For even thy brethren, and the house of thy father, even they have dealt treacherously with thee; yea, they have called a multitude after thee: believe them not, though they speak fair words unto thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我离了我的殿宇，撇弃我的产业，将我心里所亲爱的交在她仇敌的手中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have forsaken mine house, I have left mine heritage; I have given the dearly beloved of my soul into the hand of her enemies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的产业向我如林中的狮子，她发声攻击我，因此我恨恶她。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Mine heritage is unto me as a lion in the forest; it crieth out against me: therefore have I hated it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的产业向我岂如斑点的鸷鸟呢。鸷鸟岂在她四围攻击她呢。你们要去聚集田野的百兽，带来吞吃吧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Mine heritage is unto me as a speckled bird, the birds round about are against her; come ye, assemble all the beasts of the field, come to devour.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>许多牧人毁坏我的葡萄园，践踏我的分，使我美好的分变为荒凉的旷野。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Many pastors have destroyed my vineyard, they have trodden my portion under foot, they have made my pleasant portion a desolate wilderness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们使地荒凉。地既荒凉，便向我悲哀。全地荒凉，因无人介意。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They have made it desolate, and being desolate it mourneth unto me; the whole land is made desolate, because no man layeth it to heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>灭命的都来到旷野中一切净光的高处。耶和华的刀从地这边直到地那边尽行杀灭。凡有血气的都不得平安。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The spoilers are come upon all high places through the wilderness: for the sword of the LORD shall devour from the one end of the land even to the other end of the land: no flesh shall have peace.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们种的是麦子，收的是荆棘。劳劳苦苦却毫无益处。因耶和华的烈怒，你们必为自己的土产羞愧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They have sown wheat, but shall reap thorns: they have put themselves to pain, but shall not profit: and they shall be ashamed of your revenues because of the fierce anger of the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华如此说，一切恶邻，就是占据我使百姓以色列所承受产业的，我要将他们拔出本地，又要将犹大家从他们中间拔出来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus saith the LORD against all mine evil neighbours, that touch the inheritance which I have caused my people Israel to inherit; Behold, I will pluck them out of their land, and pluck out the house of Judah from among them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我拔出他们以后，我必转过来怜悯他们，把他们再带回来，各归本业，各归故土。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it shall come to pass, after that I have plucked them out I will return, and have compassion on them, and will bring them again, every man to his heritage, and every man to his land.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们若殷勤学习我百姓的道，指着我的名起誓，说，我指着永生的耶和华起誓，正如他们从前教我百姓指着巴力起誓，他们就必建立在我百姓中间。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it shall come to pass, if they will diligently learn the ways of my people, to swear by my name, The LORD liveth; as they taught my people to swear by Baal; then shall they be built in the midst of my people.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们若是不听，我必拔出那国，拔出而且毁灭。这是耶和华说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if they will not obey, I will utterly pluck up and destroy that nation, saith the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
