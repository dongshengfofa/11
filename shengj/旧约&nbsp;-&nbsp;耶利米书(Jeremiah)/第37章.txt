	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Zedekiah the king sent Jehucal the son of Shelemiah and Zephaniah the son of Maaseiah the priest to the prophet Jeremiah, saying, Pray now unto the LORD our God for us.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then came the word of the LORD unto the prophet Jeremiah, saying,</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>耶和华以色列的神如此说，犹大王打发你们来求问我，你们要如此对他说，那出来帮助你们法老的军队必回埃及本国去。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus saith the LORD, the God of Israel; Thus shall ye say to the king of Judah, that sent you unto me to enquire of me; Behold, Pharaoh's army, which is come forth to help you, shall return to Egypt into their own land.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the Chaldeans shall come again, and fight against this city, and take it, and burn it with fire.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>耶和华如此说，你们不要自欺说迦勒底人必定离开我们，因为他们必不离开。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thus saith the LORD; Deceive not yourselves, saying, The Chaldeans shall surely depart from us: for they shall not depart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For though ye had smitten the whole army of the Chaldeans that fight against you, and there remained but wounded men among them, yet should they rise up every man in his tent, and burn this city with fire.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
