<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你晓谕以色列人说，无论男女许了特别的愿，就是拿细耳人的愿（拿细耳就是归主的意思下同），要离俗归耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, When either man or woman shall separate themselves to vow a vow of a Nazarite, to separate themselves unto the LORD:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他就要远离清酒浓酒，也不可喝什么清酒浓酒作的醋。不可喝什么葡萄汁，也不可吃鲜葡萄和乾葡萄。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall separate himself from wine and strong drink, and shall drink no vinegar of wine, or vinegar of strong drink, neither shall he drink any liquor of grapes, nor eat moist grapes, or dried.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在一切离俗的日子，凡葡萄树上结的，自核至皮所作的物，都不可吃。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the days of his separation shall he eat nothing that is made of the vine tree, from the kernels even to the husk.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他一切许愿离俗的日子，不可用剃头刀剃头，要由发绺长长了。他要圣洁，直到离俗归耶和华的日子满了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the days of the vow of his separation there shall no razor come upon his head: until the days be fulfilled, in the which he separateth himself unto the LORD, he shall be holy, and shall let the locks of the hair of his head grow.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他离俗归耶和华的一切日子，不可挨近死尸。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the days that he separateth himself unto the LORD he shall come at no dead body.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的父母或是弟兄姊妹死了的时候，他不可因他们使自己不洁净，因为那离俗归神的凭据是在他头上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall not make himself unclean for his father, or for his mother, for his brother, or for his sister, when they die: because the consecration of his God is upon his head.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他一切离俗的日子是归耶和华为圣。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the days of his separation he is holy unto the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>若在他旁边忽然有人死了，以致沾染了他离俗的头，他要在第七日，得洁净的时候，剃头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And if any man die very suddenly by him, and he hath defiled the head of his consecration; then he shall shave his head in the day of his cleansing, on the seventh day shall he shave it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>第八日，他要把两只斑鸠或两只雏鸽带到会幕门口，交给祭司。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And on the eighth day he shall bring two turtles, or two young pigeons, to the priest, to the door of the tabernacle of the congregation:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>祭司要献一只作赎罪祭，一只作燔祭，为他赎那因死尸而有的罪，并要当日使他的头成为圣洁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the priest shall offer the one for a sin offering, and the other for a burnt offering, and make an atonement for him, for that he sinned by the dead, and shall hallow his head that same day.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他要另选离俗归耶和华的日子，又要牵一只一岁的公羊羔来作赎愆祭。但先前的日子要归徒然，因为他在离俗之间被玷污了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he shall consecrate unto the LORD the days of his separation, and shall bring a lamb of the first year for a trespass offering: but the days that were before shall be lost, because his separation was defiled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
