	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And thou shalt lay them up in the tabernacle of the congregation before the testimony, where I will meet with you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>后来我所拣选的那人，他的杖必发芽。这样，我必使以色列人向你们所发的怨言止息，不再达到我耳中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it shall come to pass, that the man's rod, whom I shall choose, shall blossom: and I will make to cease from me the murmurings of the children of Israel, whereby they murmur against you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>于是摩西晓谕以色列人，他们的首领就把杖交给他，按着支派，每首领一根，共有十二根。亚伦的杖也在其中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Moses spake unto the children of Israel, and every one of their princes gave him a rod apiece, for each prince one, according to their fathers' houses, even twelve rods: and the rod of Aaron was among their rods.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

</table>
