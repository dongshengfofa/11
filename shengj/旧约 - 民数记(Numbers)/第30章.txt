<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 民数记(Numbers) 第30章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>摩西晓谕以色列各支派的首领说，耶和华所吩咐的乃是这样，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Moses spake unto the heads of the tribes concerning the children of Israel, saying, This is the thing which the LORD hath commanded.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人若向耶和华许愿或起誓，要约束自己，就不可食言，必要按口中所出的一切话行。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If a man vow a vow unto the LORD, or swear an oath to bind his soul with a bond; he shall not break his word, he shall do according to all that proceedeth out of his mouth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>女子年幼，还在父家的时候，若向耶和华许愿，要约束自己，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If a woman also vow a vow unto the LORD, and bind herself by a bond, being in her father's house in her youth;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>她父亲也听见她所许的愿并约束自己的话，却向她默默不言，她所许的愿并约束自己的话就都要为定。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And her father hear her vow, and her bond wherewith she hath bound her soul, and her father shall hold his peace at her: then all her vows shall stand, and every bond wherewith she hath bound her soul shall stand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但她父亲听见的日子若不应承她所许的愿和约束自己的话，就都不得为定。耶和华也必赦免她，因为她父亲不应承。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if her father disallow her in the day that he heareth; not any of her vows, or of her bonds wherewith she hath bound her soul, shall stand: and the LORD shall forgive her, because her father disallowed her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>她若出了嫁，有愿在身，或是口中出了约束自己的冒失话，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And if she had at all an husband, when she vowed, or uttered ought out of her lips, wherewith she bound her soul;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>她丈夫听见的日子，却向她默默不言，她所许的愿并约束自己的话就都要为定。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And her husband heard it, and held his peace at her in the day that he heard it: then her vows shall stand, and her bonds wherewith she bound her soul shall stand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但她丈夫听见的日子，若不应承，就算废了她所许的愿和她出口约束自己的冒失话。耶和华也必赦免她。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if her husband disallowed her on the day that he heard it; then he shall make her vow which she vowed, and that which she uttered with her lips, wherewith she bound her soul, of none effect: and the LORD shall forgive her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>寡妇或是被休的妇人所许的愿，就是她约束自己的话，都要为定。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But every vow of a widow, and of her that is divorced, wherewith they have bound their souls, shall stand against her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>她若在丈夫家里许了愿或起了誓，约束自己，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And if she vowed in her husband's house, or bound her soul by a bond with an oath;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>丈夫听见，却向她默默不言，也没有不应承，她所许的愿并约束自己的话就都要为定。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And her husband heard it, and held his peace at her, and disallowed her not: then all her vows shall stand, and every bond wherewith she bound her soul shall stand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>丈夫听见的日子，若把这两样全废了，妇人口中所许的愿或是约束自己的话就都不得为定，因她丈夫已经把这两样废了。耶和华也必赦免她。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if her husband hath utterly made them void on the day he heard them; then whatsoever proceeded out of her lips concerning her vows, or concerning the bond of her soul, shall not stand: her husband hath made them void; and the LORD shall forgive her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡她所许的愿和刻苦约束自己所起的誓，她丈夫可以坚定，也可以废去。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Every vow, and every binding oath to afflict the soul, her husband may establish it, or her husband may make it void.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>倘若她丈夫天天向她默默不言，就算是坚定她所许的愿和约束自己的话。因丈夫听见的日子向她默默不言，就使这两样坚定。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if her husband altogether hold his peace at her from day to day; then he establisheth all her vows, or all her bonds, which are upon her: he confirmeth them, because he held his peace at her in the day that he heard them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但她丈夫听见以后，若使这两样全废了，就要担当妇人的罪孽。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But if he shall any ways make them void after that he hath heard them; then he shall bear her iniquity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
