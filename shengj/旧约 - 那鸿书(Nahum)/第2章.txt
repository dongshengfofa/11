<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 那鸿书(Nahum) 第2章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>尼尼微阿，那打碎邦国的上来攻击你。你要看守保障，谨防道路，使腰强壮，大大勉力。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that dasheth in pieces is come up before thy face: keep the munition, watch the way, make thy loins strong, fortify thy power mightily.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华复兴雅各的荣华，好像以色列的荣华一样。因为使地空虚的，已经使雅各和以色列空虚，将他们的葡萄枝毁坏了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the LORD hath turned away the excellency of Jacob, as the excellency of Israel: for the emptiers have emptied them out, and marred their vine branches.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>他勇士的盾牌是红的，精兵都穿朱红衣服。在他预备争战的日子，战车上的钢铁闪烁如火，柏木把的枪也抡起来了。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The shield of his mighty men is made red, the valiant men are in scarlet: the chariots shall be with flaming torches in the day of his preparation, and the fir trees shall be terribly shaken.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>车辆在街上（或作城外）急行，在宽阔处奔来奔去，形状如火把，飞跑如闪电。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The chariots shall rage in the streets, they shall justle one against another in the broad ways: they shall seem like torches, they shall run like the lightnings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>尼尼微王招聚他的贵胄。他们步行绊跌。速上城墙，预备挡牌。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall recount his worthies: they shall stumble in their walk; they shall make haste to the wall thereof, and the defence shall be prepared.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The gates of the rivers shall be opened, and the palace shall be dissolved.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td>王后蒙羞，被人掳去，宫女捶胸，哀呜如鸽。此乃命定之事。<a></a></td>

		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>尼尼微自古以来充满人民。如同聚水的池子，现在居民却都逃跑。虽有人呼喊说，站住，站住，却无人回顾。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But Nineveh is of old like a pool of water: yet they shall flee away. Stand, stand, shall they cry; but none shall look back.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	</tr>
