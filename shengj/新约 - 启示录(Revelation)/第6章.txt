<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>新约 - 启示录(Revelation) 第6章</strong></p>

	<tr>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I heard a voice in the midst of the four beasts say, A measure of wheat for a penny, and three measures of barley for a penny; and see thou hurt not the oil and the wine.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

</table>

</table>

</table>

</table>
