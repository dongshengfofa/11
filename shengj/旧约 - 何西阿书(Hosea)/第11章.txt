<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 何西阿书(Hosea) 第11章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以色列年幼的时候，我爱他，就从埃及少出我的儿子来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When Israel was a child, then I loved him, and called my son out of Egypt.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>先知越发招呼他们，他们越发走开，向诸巴力献祭，给雕刻的偶像烧香。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As they called them, so they went from them: they sacrificed unto Baalim, and burned incense to graven images.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我原教导以法莲行走，用膀臂抱着他们，他们却不知道是我医治他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I taught Ephraim also to go, taking them by their arms; but they knew not that I healed them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我用慈绳（慈原文是人的）爱索牵引他们，我待他们如人放松牛的两腮夹板，把粮食放在他们面前。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I drew them with cords of a man, with bands of love: and I was to them as they that take off the yoke on their jaws, and I laid meat unto them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们必不归回埃及地，亚述人却要作他们的王，因他们不肯归向我。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall not return into the land of Egypt, but the Assyrian shall be his king, because they refused to return.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>刀剑必临到他们的城邑，毁坏门闩，把人吞灭，都因他们随从自己的计谋。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the sword shall abide on his cities, and shall consume his branches, and devour them, because of their own counsels.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的民偏要背道离开我，众先知虽然招呼他们归向至上的主，却无人尊崇主。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And my people are bent to backsliding from me: though they called them to the most High, none at all would exalt him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以法莲哪，我怎能舍弃你，以色列阿，我怎能弃绝你，我怎能使你如押玛，怎能使你如洗扁，我回心转意，我的怜爱大大发动。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How shall I give thee up, Ephraim? how shall I deliver thee, Israel? how shall I make thee as Admah? how shall I set thee as Zeboim? mine heart is turned within me, my repentings are kindled together.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必不发猛烈的怒气，也不再毁灭以法莲，因我是神，并非世人，是你们中间的圣者，我必不在怒中临到你们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will not execute the fierceness of mine anger, I will not return to destroy Ephraim: for I am God, and not man; the Holy One in the midst of thee: and I will not enter into the city.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华必如狮子吼叫，子民必跟随他。他一吼叫，他们就从西方急速而来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They shall walk after the LORD: he shall roar like a lion: when he shall roar, then the children shall tremble from the west.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们必如雀鸟从埃及急速而来，又如鸽子从亚述地来到，我必使他们住自己的房屋。这是耶和华说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They shall tremble as a bird out of Egypt, and as a dove out of the land of Assyria: and I will place them in their houses, saith the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以法莲用谎话，以色列家用诡计围绕我，犹大却靠神掌权，向圣者有忠心。（或作犹大向神，向诚实的圣者犹疑不定）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ephraim compasseth me about with lies, and the house of Israel with deceit: but Judah yet ruleth with God, and is faithful with the saints.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
