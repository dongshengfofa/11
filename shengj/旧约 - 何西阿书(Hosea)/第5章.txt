<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 何西阿书(Hosea) 第5章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>众祭司阿，要听我的话，以色列家阿，要留心听。王家阿，要侧耳而听。审判要临到你们，因你们在米斯巴如网罗，在他泊山如铺张的网。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear ye this, O priests; and hearken, ye house of Israel; and give ye ear, O house of the king; for judgment is toward you, because ye have been a snare on Mizpah, and a net spread upon Tabor.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这些悖逆的人，肆行杀戮，罪孽极深。我却斥责他们众人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the revolters are profound to make slaughter, though I have been a rebuker of them all.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以法莲为我所知，以色列不能向我隐藏。以法莲哪，现在你行淫了，以色列被玷污了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I know Ephraim, and Israel is not hid from me: for now, O Ephraim, thou committest whoredom, and Israel is defiled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们所行的使他们不能归向神，因有淫心在他们里面，他们也不认识耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They will not frame their doings to turn unto their God: for the spirit of whoredoms is in the midst of them, and they have not known the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以色列的骄傲当面见证自己。故此，以色列和以法莲必因自己的罪孽跌倒，犹大也必与他们一同跌倒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the pride of Israel doth testify to his face: therefore shall Israel and Ephraim fall in their iniquity; Judah also shall fall with them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们必牵着牛羊去寻求耶和华，却寻不见，他已经转去离开他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They shall go with their flocks and with their herds to seek the LORD; but they shall not find him; he hath withdrawn himself from them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们向耶和华行事诡诈，生了私子。到了月朔，他们与他们的地业必被吞灭。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They have dealt treacherously against the LORD: for they have begotten strange children: now shall a month devour them with their portions.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们当在基比亚吹角，在拉玛吹号，在伯亚文吹出大声，说，便雅悯哪，有仇敌在你后头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Blow ye the cornet in Gibeah, and the trumpet in Ramah: cry aloud at Bethaven, after thee, O Benjamin.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在责罚的日子，以法莲必变为荒场。我在以色列支派中。指示将来必成的事。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ephraim shall be desolate in the day of rebuke: among the tribes of Israel have I made known that which shall surely be.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>犹大的首领如同挪移地界的人，我必将忿怒倒在他们身上，如水一般。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The princes of Judah were like them that remove the bound: therefore I will pour out my wrath upon them like water.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以法莲因乐从人的命令，就受欺压，被审判压碎。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ephraim is oppressed and broken in judgment, because he willingly walked after the commandment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我使以法莲如虫蛀之物，使犹大家如朽烂之木。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore will I be unto Ephraim as a moth, and to the house of Judah as rottenness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以法莲见自己有病，犹大见自己有伤，他们就打发人往亚述去见耶雷布王，他却不能医治你们，不能治好你们的伤。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When Ephraim saw his sickness, and Judah saw his wound, then went Ephraim to the Assyrian, and sent to king Jareb: yet could he not heal you, nor cure you of your wound.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必向以法莲如狮子，向犹大家如少壮狮子。我必撕裂而去，我要夺去，无人搭救。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For I will be unto Ephraim as a lion, and as a young lion to the house of Judah: I, even I, will tear and go away; I will take away, and none shall rescue him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我要回到原处，等他们自觉有罪（或作承认己罪），寻求我面，他们在急难的时候，必切切寻求我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will go and return to my place, till they acknowledge their offence, and seek my face: in their affliction they will seek me early.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
