<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 何西阿书(Hosea) 第14章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以色列阿，你要归向耶和华你的神。你是因自己的罪孽跌倒了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O Israel, return unto the LORD thy God; for thou hast fallen by thine iniquity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>当归向耶和华，用言语祷告他说，求你除净罪孽，悦纳善行，这样。我们就把嘴唇的祭，代替牛犊献上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Take with you words, and turn to the LORD: say unto him, Take away all iniquity, and receive us graciously: so will we render the calves of our lips.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们不向亚述求救。不骑埃及的马。也不再对我们手所造的说，你是我们的神。因为孤儿在你耶和华那里得蒙怜悯。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Asshur shall not save us; we will not ride upon horses: neither will we say any more to the work of our hands, Ye are our gods: for in thee the fatherless findeth mercy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必医治他们背道的病，甘心爱他们，因为我的怒气向他们转消。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will heal their backsliding, I will love them freely: for mine anger is turned away from him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必向以色列如甘露，他必如百合花开放，如黎巴嫩的树木扎根。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I will be as the dew unto Israel: he shall grow as the lily, and cast forth his roots as Lebanon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的枝条必延长，他的荣华如橄榄树，他的香气如黎巴嫩的香柏树。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His branches shall spread, and his beauty shall be as the olive tree, and his smell as Lebanon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>曾住在他荫下的必归回，发旺如五谷。开花如葡萄树。他的香气如黎巴嫩的酒。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They that dwell under his shadow shall return; they shall revive as the corn, and grow as the vine: the scent thereof shall be as the wine of Lebanon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以法莲必说，我与偶像还有什么关涉呢。我耶和华回答他，也必顾念他。我如青翠的松树，你的果子从我而得。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ephraim shall say, What have I to do any more with idols? I have heard him, and observed him: I am like a green fir tree. From me is thy fruit found.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谁是智慧人，可以明白这些事，谁是通达人，可以知道这一切。因为，耶和华的道是正直的，义人必在其中行走，罪人却在其上跌倒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who is wise, and he shall understand these things? prudent, and he shall know them? for the ways of the LORD are right, and the just shall walk in them: but the transgressors shall fall therein.
</td>
	</tr>
</table>
