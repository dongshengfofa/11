<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 何西阿书(Hosea) 第6章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>来吧，我们归向耶和华。他撕裂我们，也必医治。他打伤我们，也必缠裹。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Come, and let us return unto the LORD: for he hath torn, and he will heal us; he hath smitten, and he will bind us up.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>过两天他必使我们苏醒，第三天他必使我们兴起，我们就在他面前得以存活。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>After two days will he revive us: in the third day he will raise us up, and we shall live in his sight.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们务要认识耶和华，竭力追求认识他。他出现确如晨光，他必临到我们像甘雨，像滋润田地的春雨。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then shall we know, if we follow on to know the LORD: his going forth is prepared as the morning; and he shall come unto us as the rain, as the latter and former rain unto the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主说，以法莲哪，我可向你怎样行呢。犹大阿，我可向你怎样做呢。因为你们的良善，如同早晨的云雾，又如速散的甘露。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O Ephraim, what shall I do unto thee? O Judah, what shall I do unto thee? for your goodness is as a morning cloud, and as the early dew it goeth away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因此，我藉先知砍伐他们，以我口中的话杀戮他们，我施行的审判如光发出。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore have I hewed them by the prophets; I have slain them by the words of my mouth: and thy judgments are as the light that goeth forth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我喜爱良善（或作怜恤），不喜爱祭祀，喜爱认识神，胜于燔祭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For I desired mercy, and not sacrifice; and the knowledge of God more than burnt offerings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们却如亚当背约，在境内向我行事诡诈。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But they like men have transgressed the covenant: there have they dealt treacherously against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>基列是作孽之人的城。被血沾染。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Gilead is a city of them that work iniquity, and is polluted with blood.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>强盗成群，怎样埋伏杀人。祭司结党，也照样在示剑的路上杀戮，行了邪恶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And as troops of robbers wait for a man, so the company of priests murder in the way by consent: for they commit lewdness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在以色列家，我见了可憎的事。在以法莲那里有淫行，以色列被玷污。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have seen an horrible thing in the house of Israel: there is the whoredom of Ephraim, Israel is defiled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>犹大阿，我使被掳之民归回的时候，必有为你所命定的收场。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Also, O Judah, he hath set an harvest for thee, when I returned the captivity of my people.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
