<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 何西阿书(Hosea) 第7章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我想医治以色列的时候，以法莲的罪孽，和撒玛利亚的罪恶，就显露出来。他们行事虚谎，内有贼人入室偷窃，外有强盗成群骚扰。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When I would have healed Israel, then the iniquity of Ephraim was discovered, and the wickedness of Samaria: for they commit falsehood; and the thief cometh in, and the troop of robbers spoileth without.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们心里并不思想我记念他们的一切恶，他们所行的现在缠绕他们，都在我面前。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they consider not in their hearts that I remember all their wickedness: now their own doings have beset them about; they are before my face.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们行恶使君王欢喜，说谎使首领喜乐。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They make the king glad with their wickedness, and the princes with their lies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们都是行淫的，像火炉被烤饼的烧热，从抟面到发面的时候，暂不使火着旺。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are all adulterers, as an oven heated by the baker, who ceaseth from raising after he hath kneaded the dough, until it be leavened.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在我们王宴乐的日子，首领因酒的烈性成病。王与亵慢人拉手。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the day of our king the princes have made him sick with bottles of wine; he stretched out his hand with scorners.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>首领埋伏的时候，心中热如火炉，就如烤饼的整夜睡卧，到了早晨火气炎炎。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For they have made ready their heart like an oven, whiles they lie in wait: their baker sleepeth all the night; in the morning it burneth as a flaming fire.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>众民也热如火炉，烧灭他们的官长。他们的君王都仆倒而死。他们中间无一人求告我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are all hot as an oven, and have devoured their judges; all their kings are fallen: there is none among them that calleth unto me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以法莲与列邦人搀杂。以法莲是没有翻过的饼。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ephraim, he hath mixed himself among the people; Ephraim is a cake not turned.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>外邦人吞吃他劳力得来的，他却不知道，头发斑白，他也不觉得。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Strangers have devoured his strength, and he knoweth it not: yea, gray hairs are here and there upon him, yet he knoweth not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以色列的骄傲当面见证自己，虽遭遇这一切，他们仍不归向耶和华他们的神，也不寻求他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the pride of Israel testifieth to his face: and they do not return to the LORD their God, nor seek him for all this.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以法莲好像鸽子愚蠢无知。他们求告埃及，投奔亚述。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ephraim also is like a silly dove without heart: they call to Egypt, they go to Assyria.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们去的时候，我必将我的网撒在他们身上，我要打下他们，如同空中的鸟。我必按他们会众所听见的惩罚他们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When they shall go, I will spread my net upon them; I will bring them down as the fowls of the heaven; I will chastise them, as their congregation hath heard.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们因离弃我，必定有祸，因违背我，必被毁灭。我虽要救赎他们，他们却向我说谎。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Woe unto them! for they have fled from me: destruction unto them! because they have transgressed against me: though I have redeemed them, yet they have spoken lies against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们并不诚心哀求我，乃在床上呼号。他们为求五谷新酒聚集，仍然悖逆我。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they have not cried unto me with their heart, when they howled upon their beds: they assemble themselves for corn and wine, and they rebel against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我虽教导他们，坚固他们的膀臂，他们竟图谋抗拒我。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Though I have bound and strengthened their arms, yet do they imagine mischief against me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们归向，却不归向至上者。他们如同翻背的弓。他们的首领必因舌头的狂傲倒在刀下，这在埃及地必作人的讥笑。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They return, but not to the most High: they are like a deceitful bow: their princes shall fall by the sword for the rage of their tongue: this shall be their derision in the land of Egypt.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
