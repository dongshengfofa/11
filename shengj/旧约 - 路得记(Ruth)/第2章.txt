<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 路得记(Ruth) 第2章</strong></p>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then said Boaz unto Ruth, Hearest thou not, my daughter? Go not to glean in another field, neither go from hence, but abide here fast by my maidens:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let thine eyes be on the field that they do reap, and go thou after them: have I not charged the young men that they shall not touch thee? and when thou art athirst, go unto the vessels, and drink of that which the young men have drawn.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
