<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 约书亚记(Joshua) 第2章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>当下，嫩的儿子约书亚从什亭暗暗打发两个人作探子，吩咐说，你们去窥探那地和耶利哥。于是二人去了，来到一个妓女名叫喇合的家里，就在那里躺卧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Joshua the son of Nun sent out of Shittim two men to spy secretly, saying, Go view the land, even Jericho. And they went, and came into an harlot's house, named Rahab, and lodged there.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有人告诉耶利哥王说，今夜有以色列人来到这里窥探此地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it was told the king of Jericho, saying, Behold, there came men in hither to night of the children of Israel to search out the country.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶利哥王打发人去见喇合说，那来到你这里，进了你家的人要交出来，因为他们来窥探全地。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the king of Jericho sent unto Rahab, saying, Bring forth the men that are come to thee, which are entered into thine house: for they be come to search out all the country.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>女人将二人隐藏，就回答说，那人果然到我这里来。他们是哪里来的我却不知道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the woman took the two men, and hid them, and said thus, There came men unto me, but I wist not whence they were:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>天黑，要关城门的时候，他们出去了，往哪里去我却不知道。你们快快地去追赶，就必追上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it came to pass about the time of shutting of the gate, when it was dark, that the men went out: whither the men went I wot not: pursue after them quickly; for ye shall overtake them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>（先是女人领二人上了房顶，将他们藏在那里所摆的麻秸中。）<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But she had brought them up to the roof of the house, and hid them with the stalks of flax, which she had laid in order upon the roof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那些人就往约旦河的渡口追赶他们去了。追赶他们的人一出去，城门就关了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the men pursued after them the way to Jordan unto the fords: and as soon as they which pursued after them were gone out, they shut the gate.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>二人还没有躺卧，女人就上房顶，到他们那里，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And before they were laid down, she came up unto them upon the roof;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>对他们说，我知道耶和华已经把这地赐给你们，并且因你们的缘故我们都惊慌了。这地的一切居民在你们面前心都消化了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And she said unto the men, I know that the LORD hath given you the land, and that your terror is fallen upon us, and that all the inhabitants of the land faint because of you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为我们听见你们出埃及的时候，耶和华怎样在你们前面使红海的水乾了，并且你们怎样待约旦河东的两个亚摩利王西宏和噩，将他们尽行毁灭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For we have heard how the LORD dried up the water of the Red sea for you, when ye came out of Egypt; and what ye did unto the two kings of the Amorites, that were on the other side Jordan, Sihon and Og, whom ye utterly destroyed.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们一听见这些事，心就消化了。因你们的缘故，并无一人有胆气。耶和华你们的神本是上天下地的神。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And as soon as we had heard these things, our hearts did melt, neither did there remain any more courage in any man, because of you: for the LORD your God, he is God in heaven above, and in earth beneath.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>现在我既是恩待你们，求你们指着耶和华向我起誓，也要恩待我父家，并给我一个实在的证据，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Now therefore, I pray you, swear unto me by the LORD, since I have shewed you kindness, that ye will also shew kindness unto my father's house, and give me a true token:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要救活我的父母，弟兄，姊妹，和一切属他们的，拯救我们性命不死。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And that ye will save alive my father, and my mother, and my brethren, and my sisters, and all that they have, and deliver our lives from death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the men answered her, Our life for yours, if ye utter not this our business. And it shall be, when the LORD hath given us the land, that we will deal kindly and truly with thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then she let them down by a cord through the window: for her house was upon the town wall, and she dwelt upon the wall.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
