		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And there remained among the children of Israel seven tribes, which had not yet received their inheritance.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>约书亚对以色列人说，耶和华你们列祖的神所赐给你们的地，你们耽延不去得，要到几时呢。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Joshua said unto the children of Israel, How long are ye slack to go to possess the land, which the LORD God of your fathers hath given you?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们每支派当选举三个人，我要打发他们去，他们就要起身走遍那地，按着各支派应得的地业写明（或作画图），就回到我这里来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Give out from among you three men for each tribe: and I will send them, and they shall rise, and go through the land, and describe it according to the inheritance of them; and they shall come again to me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们要将地分作七分。犹大仍在南方，住在他的境内。约瑟家仍在北方，住在他的境内。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they shall divide it into seven parts: Judah shall abide in their coast on the south, and the house of Joseph shall abide in their coasts on the north.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们要将地分作七分，写明了拿到我这里来。我要在耶和华我们神面前，为你们拈阄。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ye shall therefore describe the land into seven parts, and bring the description hither to me, that I may cast lots for you here before the LORD our God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>利未人在你们中间没有分，因为供耶和华祭司的职任就是他们的产业。迦得支派，流便支派，和玛拿西半支派已经在约旦河东得了地业，就是耶和华仆人摩西所给他们的。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But the Levites have no part among you; for the priesthood of the LORD is their inheritance: and Gad, and Reuben, and half the tribe of Manasseh, have received their inheritance beyond Jordan on the east, which Moses the servant of the LORD gave them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the men arose, and went away: and Joshua charged them that went to describe the land, saying, Go and walk through the land, and describe it, and come again to me, that I may here cast lots for you before the LORD in Shiloh.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
