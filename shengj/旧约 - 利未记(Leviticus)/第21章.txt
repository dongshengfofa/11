<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 利未记(Leviticus) 第21章</strong></p>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>除非为他骨肉之亲的父母，儿女，弟兄，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But for his kin, that is near unto him, that is, for his mother, and for his father, and for his son, and for his daughter, and for his brother,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>和未曾出嫁，作处女的姊妹，才可以沾染自己。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And for his sister a virgin, that is nigh unto him, which hath had no husband; for her may he be defiled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>祭司既在民中为首，就不可从俗沾染自己。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But he shall not defile himself, being a chief man among his people, to profane himself.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不可使头光秃，不可剃除胡须的周围，也不可用刀划身。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They shall not make baldness upon their head, neither shall they shave off the corner of their beard, nor make any cuttings in their flesh.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要归神为圣，不可亵渎神的名，因为耶和华的火祭，就是神的食物，是他们献的，所以他们要成为圣。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They shall be holy unto their God, and not profane the name of their God: for the offerings of the LORD made by fire, and the bread of their God, they do offer: therefore they shall be holy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不可娶妓女或被污的女人为妻，也不可娶被休的妇人为妻，因为祭司是归神为圣。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They shall not take a wife that is a whore, or profane; neither shall they take a woman put away from her husband: for he is holy unto his God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以你要使他成圣，因为他奉献你神的食物，你要以他为圣，因为我使你们成圣的耶和华是圣的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou shalt sanctify him therefore; for he offereth the bread of thy God: he shall be holy unto thee: for I the LORD, which sanctify you, am holy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>祭司的女儿若行淫辱没自己，就辱没了父亲，必用火将她焚烧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the daughter of any priest, if she profane herself by playing the whore, she profaneth her father: she shall be burnt with fire.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And he that is the high priest among his brethren, upon whose head the anointing oil was poured, and that is consecrated to put on the garments, shall not uncover his head, nor rend his clothes;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Neither shall he go in to any dead body, nor defile himself for his father, or for his mother;</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Neither shall he go out of the sanctuary, nor profane the sanctuary of his God; for the crown of the anointing oil of his God is upon him: I am the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

</table>

		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	<tr>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
