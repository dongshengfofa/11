<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第8章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧岂不呼叫，聪明岂不发声。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Doth not wisdom cry? and understanding put forth her voice?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他在道旁高处的顶上，在十字路口站立。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>She standeth in the top of high places, by the way in the places of the paths.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在城门旁，在城门口，在城门洞，大声说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>She crieth at the gates, at the entry of the city, at the coming in at the doors.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>众人哪，我呼叫你们。我向世人发声，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Unto you, O men, I call; and my voice is to the sons of man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说，愚昧人哪，你们要会悟灵明。愚昧人哪，你们当心里明白。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O ye simple, understand wisdom: and, ye fools, be ye of an understanding heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们当听，因我要说极美的话。我张嘴要论正直的事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear; for I will speak of excellent things; and the opening of my lips shall be right things.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的口要发出真理。我的嘴憎恶邪恶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For my mouth shall speak truth; and wickedness is an abomination to my lips.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我口中的言语，都是公义，并无弯曲乖僻。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the words of my mouth are in righteousness; there is nothing froward or perverse in them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有聪明的以为明显，得知识的以为正直。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They are all plain to him that understandeth, and right to them that find knowledge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们当受我的教训，不受白银。宁得知识，胜过黄金。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Receive my instruction, and not silver; and knowledge rather than choice gold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为智慧比珍珠（或作红宝石）更美。一切可喜爱的，都不足与比较。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For wisdom is better than rubies; and all the things that may be desired are not to be compared to it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我智慧以灵明为居所，又寻得知识和谋略。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I wisdom dwell with prudence, and find out knowledge of witty inventions.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>敬畏耶和华，在乎恨恶邪恶。那骄傲，狂妄，并恶道，以及乖谬的口，都为我所恨恶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fear of the LORD is to hate evil: pride, and arrogancy, and the evil way, and the froward mouth, do I hate.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我有谋略，和真知识。我乃聪明。我有能力。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Counsel is mine, and sound wisdom: I am understanding; I have strength.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Riches and honour are with me; yea, durable riches and righteousness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My fruit is better than gold, yea, than fine gold; and my revenue than choice silver.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
