<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第4章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>众子阿，要听父亲的教训，留心得知聪明。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear, ye children, the instruction of a father, and attend to know understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因我所给你们的，是好教训。不可离弃我的法则。（或作指教）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For I give you good doctrine, forsake ye not my law.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我在父亲面前为孝子，在母亲眼中为独一的娇儿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For I was my father's son, tender and only beloved in the sight of my mother.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>父亲教训我说，你心要存记我的言语，遵守我的命令，便得存活。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He taught me also, and said unto me, Let thine heart retain my words: keep my commandments, and live.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要得智慧，要得聪明。不可忘记，也不可偏离我口中的言语。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Get wisdom, get understanding: forget it not; neither decline from the words of my mouth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不可离弃智慧，智慧就护卫你。要爱他，他就保守你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Forsake her not, and she shall preserve thee: love her, and she shall keep thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧为首。所以要得智慧。在你一切所得之内，必得聪明。（或作用你一切所得的去换聪明）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wisdom is the principal thing; therefore get wisdom: and with all thy getting get understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>高举智慧，他就使你高升。怀抱智慧，他就使你尊荣。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Exalt her, and she shall promote thee: she shall bring thee to honour, when thou dost embrace her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他必将华冠加在你头上，把荣冕交给你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>She shall give to thine head an ornament of grace: a crown of glory shall she deliver to thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，你要听受我的言语，就必延年益寿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear, O my son, and receive my sayings; and the years of thy life shall be many.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我已指教你走智慧的道，引导你行正直的路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have taught thee in the way of wisdom; I have led thee in right paths.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你行走，脚步必不致狭窄。你奔跑，也不致跌倒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When thou goest, thy steps shall not be straitened; and when thou runnest, thou shalt not stumble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要持定训诲，不可放松。必当谨守，因为他是你的生命。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Take fast hold of instruction; let her not go: keep her; for she is thy life.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不可行恶人的路。不可走坏人的道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Enter not into the path of the wicked, and go not in the way of evil men.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要躲避，不可轻过。要转身而去。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Avoid it, pass not by it, turn from it, and pass away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这等人若不行恶，不得睡觉。不使人跌倒，睡卧不安。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For they sleep not, except they have done mischief; and their sleep is taken away, unless they cause some to fall.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为他们以奸恶吃饼，以强暴喝酒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For they eat the bread of wickedness, and drink the wine of violence.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>但义人的路，好像黎明的光，越照越明，直到日午。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But the path of the just is as the shining light, that shineth more and more unto the perfect day.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人的道好像幽暗。自己不知因什吗跌倒。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The way of the wicked is as darkness: they know not at what they stumble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，要留心听我的言词，侧耳听我的话语。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, attend to my words; incline thine ear unto my sayings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>都不可离你的眼目。要存记在你心中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let them not depart from thine eyes; keep them in the midst of thine heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为得着他的，就得了生命，又得了医全体的良药。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For they are life unto those that find them, and health to all their flesh.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要保守你心，胜过保守一切。（或作你要切切保守你心）因为一生的果效，是由心发出。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Keep thy heart with all diligence; for out of it are the issues of life.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要除掉邪僻的口，弃绝乖谬的嘴。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Put away from thee a froward mouth, and perverse lips put far from thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的眼目，要向前升看，你的眼睛，（原文作皮）当向前直观。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let thine eyes look right on, and let thine eyelids look straight before thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第26段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要修平你脚下的路，坚定你一切的道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ponder the path of thy feet, and let all thy ways be established.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第27段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不可偏向左右。要使你的脚离开邪恶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Turn not to the right hand nor to the left: remove thy foot from evil.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
