<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第25章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以下也是所罗门的箴言。是犹大王希西家的人所誊录的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>These are also proverbs of Solomon, which the men of Hezekiah king of Judah copied out.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>将事隐秘，乃神的荣耀。将事察清，乃君王的荣耀。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is the glory of God to conceal a thing: but the honour of kings is to search out a matter.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>天之高，地之厚，君王之心也测不透。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The heaven for height, and the earth for depth, and the heart of kings is unsearchable.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>除去银子的渣滓，就有银子出来，银匠能以作器皿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Take away the dross from the silver, and there shall come forth a vessel for the finer.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>除去王面前的恶人，国位就靠公义坚立。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Take away the wicked from before the king, and his throne shall be established in righteousness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要在王面前妄自尊大。不要在大人的位上站立。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Put not forth thyself in the presence of the king, and stand not in the place of great men:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>宁可有人说，请你上来，强如在你觐见的王子面前，叫你退下。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For better it is that it be said unto thee, Come up hither; than that thou shouldest be put lower in the presence of the prince whom thine eyes have seen.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要冒失出去与人争竞，免得至终被他羞辱，你就不知道怎样行了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Go not forth hastily to strive, lest thou know not what to do in the end thereof, when thy neighbour hath put thee to shame.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你与邻舍争讼，要与他ㄧ人辩论。不可泄漏人的密事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Debate thy cause with thy neighbour himself; and discover not a secret to another:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恐怕听见的人骂你，你的臭名就难以脱离。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lest he that heareth it put thee to shame, and thine infamy turn not away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>一句话说得合宜，就如金苹果在银网子里。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A word fitly spoken is like apples of gold in pictures of silver.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人的劝戒，在顺从的人耳中，好像金耳环，和精金的妆饰。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As an earring of gold, and an ornament of fine gold, so is a wise reprover upon an obedient ear.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>忠信的使者，叫差他的人心里舒畅，就如在收割时，有冰雪的凉气。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As the cold of snow in the time of harvest, so is a faithful messenger to them that send him: for he refresheth the soul of his masters.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>空夸赠送礼物的，好像无雨的风云。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso boasteth himself of a false gift is like clouds and wind without rain.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恒常忍耐，可以劝动君王。柔和的舌头，能折断骨头。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>By long forbearing is a prince persuaded, and a soft tongue breaketh the bone.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你得了蜜吗，只可吃够而已。恐怕你过饱就呕吐出来。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hast thou found honey? eat so much as is sufficient for thee, lest thou be filled therewith, and vomit it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的脚要少进邻舍的家，恐怕他厌烦你，恨恶你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Withdraw thy foot from thy neighbour's house; lest he be weary of thee, and so hate thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>作假见证陷害邻舍的，就是大槌，是利刀，是快箭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man that beareth false witness against his neighbour is a maul, and a sword, and a sharp arrow.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>患难时倚靠不忠诚的人，好像破坏的牙，错骨缝的脚。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Confidence in an unfaithful man in time of trouble is like a broken tooth, and a foot out of joint.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>对伤心的人唱歌，就如冷天脱衣服，又如硷上倒醋。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As he that taketh away a garment in cold weather, and as vinegar upon nitre, so is he that singeth songs to an heavy heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的仇敌，若饿了就给他饭吃。若渴了就给他水喝。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thine enemy be hungry, give him bread to eat; and if he be thirsty, give him water to drink:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thou shalt heap coals of fire upon his head, and the LORD shall reward thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>北风生雨，谗谤人的舌头也生怒容。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The north wind driveth away rain: so doth an angry countenance a backbiting tongue.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>宁可住在房顶的角上，不在宽阔的房屋与争吵的妇人同住。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is better to dwell in the corner of the housetop, than with a brawling woman and in a wide house.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As cold waters to a thirsty soul, so is good news from a far country.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
