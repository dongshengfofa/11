<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第27章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要为明日自夸，因为一日要生何事，你尚且不能知道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Boast not thyself of to morrow; for thou knowest not what a day may bring forth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要别人夸奖你，不可用口自夸。等外人称赞你，不可用嘴自称。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let another man praise thee, and not thine own mouth; a stranger, and not thine own lips.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>石头重，沙土沉，愚妄人的恼怒，比这两样更重。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A stone is heavy, and the sand weighty; but a fool's wrath is heavier than them both.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>忿怒为残忍，怒气为狂澜，惟有嫉妒，谁能敌得住呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wrath is cruel, and anger is outrageous; but who is able to stand before envy?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>当面的责备，强如背地的爱情。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Open rebuke is better than secret love.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>朋友加的伤痕，出于忠诚，仇敌连连亲嘴，却是多馀。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Faithful are the wounds of a friend; but the kisses of an enemy are deceitful.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人吃饱了，厌恶蜂房的蜜。人饥饿了，一切苦物都觉甘甜。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The full soul loatheth an honeycomb; but to the hungry soul every bitter thing is sweet.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人离本处飘流，好像雀鸟离窝游飞。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As a bird that wandereth from her nest, so is a man that wandereth from his place.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>膏油与香料，使人心喜悦。朋友诚实的劝教，也是如此甘美。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ointment and perfume rejoice the heart: so doth the sweetness of a man's friend by hearty counsel.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的朋友，和父亲的朋友，你都不可离弃。你遭难的日子，不要上弟兄的家去。向近的邻舍，强如远方的弟兄。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thine own friend, and thy father's friend, forsake not; neither go into thy brother's house in the day of thy calamity: for better is a neighbour that is near than a brother far off.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，你要作智慧人，好叫我的心喜欢，使我可以回答那讥笑我的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, be wise, and make my heart glad, that I may answer him that reproacheth me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

</table>

</table>
