<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第31章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>利慕伊勒王的言语，是他母亲教训他的真言。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The words of king Lemuel, the prophecy that his mother taught him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的儿阿，我腹中生的儿阿，我许愿得的儿阿，我当怎样教训你呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>What, my son? and what, the son of my womb? and what, the son of my vows?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要将你的精力给妇女，也不要有败坏君王的行为。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Give not thy strength unto women, nor thy ways to that which destroyeth kings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>利慕伊勒阿，君王喝酒，君王喝酒不相宜。王子说，浓酒在那里也不相宜。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is not for kings, O Lemuel, it is not for kings to drink wine; nor for princes strong drink:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恐怕喝了就忘记律例，颠倒一切困苦人的是非。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lest they drink, and forget the law, and pervert the judgment of any of the afflicted.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>可以把浓酒给将亡的人喝。把清酒给苦心的人喝。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Give strong drink unto him that is ready to perish, and wine unto those that be of heavy hearts.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>让他们喝了，就忘记他的贫穷，不在记念他的苦楚。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let him drink, and forget his poverty, and remember his misery no more.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Open thy mouth for the dumb in the cause of all such as are appointed to destruction.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
