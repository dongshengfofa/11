<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第26章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>夏天落雪，收割时下雨，都不相宜。愚昧人得尊荣，也是如此。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As snow in summer, and as rain in harvest, so honour is not seemly for a fool.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>麻雀往来，燕子翻飞，这样，无故的咒诅，也必不临到。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As the bird by wandering, as the swallow by flying, so the curse causeless shall not come.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>鞭子是为打马。辔头是为勒驴。刑杖是为打愚昧人的背。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A whip for the horse, a bridle for the ass, and a rod for the fool's back.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要照愚昧人的愚妄话回答他，恐怕你与他一样。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Answer not a fool according to his folly, lest thou also be like unto him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要照愚昧人的愚妄话回答他，免得他自以为有智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Answer a fool according to his folly, lest he be wise in his own conceit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>藉愚昧人手寄的，是砍断自己的脚，自受损害。（自受原作喝）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that sendeth a message by the hand of a fool cutteth off the feet, and drinketh damage.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>瘸子的脚，空存无用。箴言在愚昧人的口中，也是如此。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The legs of the lame are not equal: so is a parable in the mouth of fools.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>将尊荣给愚昧人的，好像人把石子包在机弦里。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As he that bindeth a stone in a sling, so is he that giveth honour to a fool.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>箴言在愚昧人的口中，好像荆棘刺入醉汉的手。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As a thorn goeth up into the hand of a drunkard, so is a parable in the mouth of fools.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>雇愚昧人的，与雇过路人的，就像射伤众人的弓箭手。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The great God that formed all things both rewardeth the fool, and rewardeth transgressors.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚昧人行愚妄事，行了又行，就如狗转过来吃他所吐的。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As a dog returneth to his vomit, so a fool returneth to his folly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你见自以为有智慧的人吗，愚昧人比他更有指望。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Seest thou a man wise in his own conceit? there is more hope of a fool than of him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>懒惰人说，道上有猛狮，街上有壮狮。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The slothful man saith, There is a lion in the way; a lion is in the streets.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

</table>

</table>
