<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第11章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>诡诈的天平，为耶和华所憎恶。公平的法码，为他所喜悦。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A false balance is abomination to the LORD: but a just weight is his delight.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>骄傲来，羞耻也来。谦逊人却有智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When pride cometh, then cometh shame: but with the lowly is wisdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>正直人的纯正，必引导自己。奸诈人的乖僻，必毁灭自己。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The integrity of the upright shall guide them: but the perverseness of transgressors shall destroy them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>发怒的日子，赀财无益。惟有公义能救人脱离死亡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Riches profit not in the day of wrath: but righteousness delivereth from death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>完全人的义，必指引他的路。但恶人必因自己的恶跌倒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The righteousness of the perfect shall direct his way: but the wicked shall fall by his own wickedness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>正直人的义，必拯救自己。奸诈人必陷在自己的罪孽中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The righteousness of the upright shall deliver them: but transgressors shall be taken in their own naughtiness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人一死，他的指望必灭绝。罪人的盼望，也必灭没。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When a wicked man dieth, his expectation shall perish: and the hope of unjust men perisheth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人得脱离患难，有恶人来代替他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The righteous is delivered out of trouble, and the wicked cometh in his stead.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不虔敬的人用口败坏邻舍。义人却因知识得救。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>An hypocrite with his mouth destroyeth his neighbour: but through knowledge shall the just be delivered.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人享福合城喜乐。恶人灭亡，人都欢呼。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When it goeth well with the righteous, the city rejoiceth: and when the wicked perish, there is shouting.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>By the blessing of the upright the city is exalted: but it is overthrown by the mouth of the wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>
