<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第14章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧妇人，建立家室。愚妄妇人，亲手拆毁。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Every wise woman buildeth her house: but the foolish plucketh it down with her hands.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>行动正直的，敬畏耶和华。乖僻的，却藐视他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that walketh in his uprightness feareth the LORD: but he that is perverse in his ways despiseth him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚妄人口中骄傲，如杖责打己身。智慧人的嘴，必保守自己。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the mouth of the foolish is a rod of pride: but the lips of the wise shall preserve them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>家里无牛，槽头乾净。土产加多，乃凭牛力。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Where no oxen are, the crib is clean: but much increase is by the strength of the ox.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>诚实见证人，不说谎话。假见证人，吐出谎言。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A faithful witness will not lie: but a false witness will utter lies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>亵慢人寻智慧，却寻不着。聪明人易得知识。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A scorner seeketh wisdom, and findeth it not: but knowledge is easy unto him that understandeth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>到愚昧人面前，不见他嘴中有知识。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Go from the presence of a foolish man, when thou perceivest not in him the lips of knowledge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>通达人的智慧，在乎明白己道。愚昧人的愚妄，乃是诡诈。（或作自欺）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wisdom of the prudent is to understand his way: but the folly of fools is deceit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚妄人犯罪，以为戏耍。（或作赎愆祭愚弄愚妄人）正直人互相喜悦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Fools make a mock at sin: but among the righteous there is favour.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>心中的苦楚，自己知道。心里的喜乐，外人无干。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The heart knoweth his own bitterness; and a stranger doth not intermeddle with his joy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>奸恶人的房屋必倾倒。正直人的帐棚必兴盛。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The house of the wicked shall be overthrown: but the tabernacle of the upright shall flourish.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有一条路人以为正。至终成为死亡之路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There is a way which seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof are the ways of death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人在喜笑中，心也忧愁。快乐至极，就生愁苦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Even in laughter the heart is sorrowful; and the end of that mirth is heaviness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>心中背道的，必满得自己的结果。善人必从自己（的行为，得以知足）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The backslider in heart shall be filled with his own ways: and a good man shall be satisfied from himself.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚蒙人是话都信。通达人处处谨慎。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The simple believeth every word: but the prudent man looketh well to his going.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人惧怕，就远离恶事。愚妄人却狂傲自恃。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wise man feareth, and departeth from evil: but the fool rageth, and is confident.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that is soon angry dealeth foolishly: and a man of wicked devices is hated.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The poor is hated even of his own neighbour: but the rich hath many friends.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谋恶的岂非走入迷途吗。谋善的必得慈爱和诚实。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Do they not err that devise evil? but mercy and truth shall be to them that devise good.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In all labour there is profit: but the talk of the lips tendeth only to penury.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
