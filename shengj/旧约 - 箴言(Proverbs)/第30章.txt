	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I neither learned wisdom, nor have the knowledge of the holy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谁升天又降下来。谁聚风在掌中。谁包水在衣服里。谁立定地的四极。他名叫什么。他儿子叫名叫什么。你知道吗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who hath ascended up into heaven, or descended? who hath gathered the wind in his fists? who hath bound the waters in a garment? who hath established all the ends of the earth? what is his name, and what is his son's name, if thou canst tell?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

</table>
