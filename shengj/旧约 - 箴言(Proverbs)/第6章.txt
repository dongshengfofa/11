<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第6章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，你若为朋友作保，替外人击掌，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, if thou be surety for thy friend, if thou hast stricken thy hand with a stranger,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你就被口中的话语缠住，被嘴里的言语捉住。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou art snared with the words of thy mouth, thou art taken with the words of thy mouth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，你既落在朋友手中，就当这样行，才可救自己。你要自卑，去恳求你的朋友。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Do this now, my son, and deliver thyself, when thou art come into the hand of thy friend; go, humble thyself, and make sure thy friend.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要容你的眼睛睡觉，不要容你的眼皮打盹。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Give not sleep to thine eyes, nor slumber to thine eyelids.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要救自己，如鹿脱离猎户的手，如鸟脱离捕鸟人的手。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Deliver thyself as a roe from the hand of the hunter, and as a bird from the hand of the fowler.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>懒惰人哪，你去察看蚂蚁的动作，就可得智慧。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Go to the ant, thou sluggard; consider her ways, and be wise:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>蚂蚁没有元帅，没有官长，没有君王，<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which having no guide, overseer, or ruler,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Provideth her meat in the summer, and gathereth her food in the harvest.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>懒惰人哪，你要睡到几时呢。你何时睡醒呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How long wilt thou sleep, O sluggard? when wilt thou arise out of thy sleep?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yet a little sleep, a little slumber, a little folding of the hands to sleep:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的贪穷就必如强盗速来，你的缺乏仿佛拿兵器的人来到。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So shall thy poverty come as one that travelleth, and thy want as an armed man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A naughty person, a wicked man, walketh with a froward mouth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He winketh with his eyes, he speaketh with his feet, he teacheth with his fingers;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

</table>
