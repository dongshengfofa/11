<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第7章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，你要遵守我的言语，将我的命令存记在心。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, keep my words, and lay up my commandments with thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>遵守我的命令，就得存活。保守我的法则，（或作指教）好像保守眼中的瞳人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Keep my commandments, and live; and my law as the apple of thine eye.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>系在你指头上，刻在你心版上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Bind them upon thy fingers, write them upon the table of thine heart.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Say unto wisdom, Thou art my sister; and call understanding thy kinswoman:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That they may keep thee from the strange woman, from the stranger which flattereth with her words.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
