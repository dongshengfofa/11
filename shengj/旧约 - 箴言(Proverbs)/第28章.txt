<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第28章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人虽无人追赶也逃跑，义人却胆壮像狮子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wicked flee when no man pursueth: but the righteous are bold as a lion.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>邦国因有罪过，君王就多更换。因有聪明知识的人，国必长存。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the transgression of a land many are the princes thereof: but by a man of understanding and knowledge the state thereof shall be prolonged.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>穷人欺压贫民，好像暴雨冲没粮食。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A poor man that oppresseth the poor is like a sweeping rain which leaveth no food.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>违弃律法的，夸奖恶人。遵守律法的，却与恶人相争。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They that forsake the law praise the wicked: but such as keep the law contend with them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>坏人不明白公义。惟有寻求耶和华的，无不明白。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Evil men understand not judgment: but they that seek the LORD understand all things.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>行为纯正的穷乏人，胜过行事乖僻的富足人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Better is the poor that walketh in his uprightness, than he that is perverse in his ways, though he be rich.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谨守律法的是智慧之子。与贪食人作伴的，却羞辱其父。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso keepeth the law is a wise son: but he that is a companion of riotous men shameth his father.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人以厚利加增财物，是给那怜悯穷人者积蓄的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that by usury and unjust gain increaseth his substance, he shall gather it for him that will pity the poor.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>转耳不听律法的，他的祈祷也为可憎。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that turneth away his ear from hearing the law, even his prayer shall be abomination.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>诱惑正直人行恶道的，必掉在自己的坑里。惟有完全人，必承受福分。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso causeth the righteous to go astray in an evil way, he shall fall himself into his own pit: but the upright shall have good things in possession.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>富足人自以为有智慧。但聪明的贫穷人，能将他查透。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The rich man is wise in his own conceit; but the poor that hath understanding searcheth him out.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人得志有大荣耀。恶人兴起人就躲藏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When righteous men do rejoice, there is great glory: but when the wicked rise, a man is hidden.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>遮掩自己罪过的，必不享通。承认离弃罪过的，必蒙怜恤。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that covereth his sins shall not prosper: but whoso confesseth and forsaketh them shall have mercy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>常存敬畏的，便为有福。心存刚硬的，必陷在祸患里。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Happy is the man that feareth alway: but he that hardeneth his heart shall fall into mischief.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>暴虐的君王辖制贫民。好像吼叫的狮子，觅食的熊。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As a roaring lion, and a ranging bear; so is a wicked ruler over the poor people.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>无知的君多行暴虐。以贪财为可恨的，必年长日久。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The prince that wanteth understanding is also a great oppressor: but he that hateth covetousness shall prolong his days.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>背负流人血之罪的，必往坑里奔跑，谁也不可拦阻他。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man that doeth violence to the blood of any person shall flee to the pit; let no man stay him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>行动正直的，必蒙拯救。行事弯曲的，立时跌倒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso walketh uprightly shall be saved: but he that is perverse in his ways shall fall at once.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耕种自己田地的，必得饱食。追随虚浮的，足受穷乏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that tilleth his land shall have plenty of bread: but he that followeth after vain persons shall have poverty enough.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>诚实人必多得福，想要急速发财的，不免受罚。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A faithful man shall abound with blessings: but he that maketh haste to be rich shall not be innocent.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看人的情面，乃为不好。人因一块饼枉法，也为不好。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To have respect of persons is not good: for for a piece of bread that man will transgress.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人有恶眼想要急速发财，却不知穷乏必临到他身。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that hasteth to be rich hath an evil eye, and considereth not that poverty shall come upon him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>责备人的，后来蒙人喜悦，多于那用舌头谄媚人的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that rebuketh a man afterwards shall find more favour than he that flattereth with the tongue.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>偷窃父母的，说，这不是罪。此人就是与强盗同类。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso robbeth his father or his mother, and saith, It is no transgression; the same is the companion of a destroyer.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>心中贪婪的，挑起争端。倚靠耶和华的，必得丰裕。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that is of a proud heart stirreth up strife: but he that putteth his trust in the LORD shall be made fat.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第26段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>心中自是的，便是愚昧人。凭智慧行事的，必蒙拯救。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that trusteth in his own heart is a fool: but whoso walketh wisely, he shall be delivered.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第27段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>周济贫穷的，不致缺乏。佯为不见的，必多受咒诅。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that giveth unto the poor shall not lack: but he that hideth his eyes shall have many a curse.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
