<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第29章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人屡次受责罚，仍然硬着颈项，他必顷刻败坏，无法可治。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He, that being often reproved hardeneth his neck, shall suddenly be destroyed, and that without remedy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人增多，民就喜乐。恶人掌权，民就叹息。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice: but when the wicked beareth rule, the people mourn.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>爱慕智慧的，使父亲喜乐。与妓女结交的，却浪费钱财。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father: but he that keepeth company with harlots spendeth his substance.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王藉公平，使国坚定。索要贿赂，使国倾败。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The king by judgment establisheth the land: but he that receiveth gifts overthroweth it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谄媚邻舍的，就是设网罗绊他的脚。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man that flattereth his neighbour spreadeth a net for his feet.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人犯罪，自陷网罗。惟独义人，欢呼喜乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the transgression of an evil man there is a snare: but the righteous doth sing and rejoice.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人知道查明穷人的案。恶人没有聪明，就不得而知。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The righteous considereth the cause of the poor: but the wicked regardeth not to know it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>亵慢人煽惑通城。智慧人止息众怒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Scornful men bring a city into a snare: but wise men turn away wrath.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人与愚妄人相争，或怒，或笑，总不能使他止息。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If a wise man contendeth with a foolish man, whether he rage or laugh, there is no rest.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>好流人血的，恨恶完全人。索取正直人的性命。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The bloodthirsty hate the upright: but the just seek his soul.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚妄人怒气全发。智慧人忍气含怒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A fool uttereth all his mind: but a wise man keepeth it in till afterwards.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>君王若听谎言，他一切臣仆都是奸恶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If a ruler hearken to lies, all his servants are wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>贫穷人，强暴人，在世相遇，他们的眼目，都蒙耶和华光照。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The poor and the deceitful man meet together: the LORD lighteneth both their eyes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>君王凭诚实判断穷人，他的国位，必永远坚立。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The king that faithfully judgeth the poor, his throne shall be established for ever.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>杖打和责备，能加增智慧。放纵的儿子，使母亲羞愧。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The rod and reproof give wisdom: but a child left to himself bringeth his mother to shame.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人加多，过犯也加多。义人必看见他们跌倒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When the wicked are multiplied, transgression increaseth: but the righteous shall see their fall.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>管教你的儿子，他就使你得安息，也必使你心里喜乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Correct thy son, and he shall give thee rest; yea, he shall give delight unto thy soul.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>没有异象，（或作默示）民就放肆。惟遵守律法的，便为有福。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Where there is no vision, the people perish: but he that keepeth the law, happy is he.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>只用言语，仆人不肯受管教，他虽然明白，也不留意。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A servant will not be corrected by words: for though he understand he will not answer.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你见言语急躁的人吗。愚昧人比他更有指望。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Seest thou a man that is hasty in his words? there is more hope of a fool than of him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人将仆人从小娇养，这仆人终久必成了他的儿子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that delicately bringeth up his servant from a child shall have him become his son at the length.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>好气的人，挑启争端。暴怒的人，多多犯罪。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>An angry man stirreth up strife, and a furious man aboundeth in transgression.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人的高傲，必使他卑下。心里谦逊的，必得尊荣。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man's pride shall bring him low: but honour shall uphold the humble in spirit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

		<td>人与盗贼分赃，是恨恶自己的性命。他听见叫人发誓的声音，却不言语。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso is partner with a thief hateth his own soul: he heareth cursing, and bewrayeth it not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惧怕人的陷入网罗。惟有倚靠耶和华的，必得安稳。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fear of man bringeth a snare: but whoso putteth his trust in the LORD shall be safe.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td>求王恩的人多。定人事乃在耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Many seek the ruler's favour; but every man's judgment cometh from the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
