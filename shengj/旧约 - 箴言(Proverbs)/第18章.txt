<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第18章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>与众寡合的，独自寻求心愿，并恼恨一切真智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Through desire a man, having separated himself, seeketh and intermeddleth with all wisdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚昧人不喜爱明哲，只喜爱显露心意。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A fool hath no delight in understanding, but that his heart may discover itself.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人来，藐视随来。羞耻到，辱骂同到。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When the wicked cometh, then cometh also contempt, and with ignominy reproach.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人口中的言语，如同深水。智慧的泉源，好像涌流的河水。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The words of a man's mouth are as deep waters, and the wellspring of wisdom as a flowing brook.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>瞻徇恶人的情面，偏断义人的案件，都为不善。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is not good to accept the person of the wicked, to overthrow the righteous in judgment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚昧人张嘴启争端，开口招鞭打。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A fool's lips enter into contention, and his mouth calleth for strokes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚昧人的口，自取败坏。他的嘴，是他生命的网罗。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A fool's mouth is his destruction, and his lips are the snare of his soul.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>传舌人的言语，如同美食，深入人的心腹。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The words of a talebearer are as wounds, and they go down into the innermost parts of the belly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>作工懈怠的，与浪费人为弟兄。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He also that is slothful in his work is brother to him that is a great waster.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的名，是坚固台。义人奔入，便得安稳。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The name of the LORD is a strong tower: the righteous runneth into it, and is safe.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>富足人的财物，是他的坚城，在他心想，犹如高墙。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The rich man's wealth is his strong city, and as an high wall in his own conceit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>败坏之先，人心骄傲。尊荣以前，必有谦卑。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Before destruction the heart of man is haughty, and before honour is humility.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>未曾听完先回答的，便是他的愚昧，和羞辱。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that answereth a matter before he heareth it, it is folly and shame unto him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人有疾病，心能忍耐。心灵忧伤，谁能承当呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The spirit of a man will sustain his infirmity; but a wounded spirit who can bear?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>聪明人的心得知识。智慧人的耳求知识。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The heart of the prudent getteth knowledge; and the ear of the wise seeketh knowledge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人的礼物，为他开路，引他到高位的人面前。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man's gift maketh room for him, and bringeth him before great men.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>先诉情由的，似乎有理。但邻舍来到，就察出实情。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that is first in his own cause seemeth just; but his neighbour cometh and searcheth him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>掣签能止息争竞，也能解散强胜的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The lot causeth contentions to cease, and parteth between the mighty.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>弟兄结怨，劝他和好，比取坚固城还难。这样的争竞，如同坚寨的门闩。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A brother offended is harder to be won than a strong city: and their contentions are like the bars of a castle.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人口中所结的果子，必充满肚腹。他嘴所出的，必使他饱足。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man's belly shall be satisfied with the fruit of his mouth; and with the increase of his lips shall he be filled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>生死在舌头的权下。喜爱他的，必吃他所结的果子。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Death and life are in the power of the tongue: and they that love it shall eat the fruit thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>得着贤妻的，是得着好处，也是蒙了耶和华的恩惠。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso findeth a wife findeth a good thing, and obtaineth favour of the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>贫穷人说哀求的话。富足人用威吓的话回答。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The poor useth intreaties; but the rich answereth roughly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
