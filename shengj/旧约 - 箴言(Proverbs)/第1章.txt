<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第1章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以色列王大卫儿子所罗门的箴言。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The proverbs of Solomon the son of David, king of Israel;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要使人晓得智慧和训诲。分辨通达的言语。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To know wisdom and instruction; to perceive the words of understanding;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>使人处世，领受智慧，仁义，公平，正直的训诲。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To receive the instruction of wisdom, justice, and judgment, and equity;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>使愚人灵明，使少年人有知识和谋略。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To give subtilty to the simple, to the young man knowledge and discretion.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>使智慧人听见，增长学问。使聪明人得着智谋。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wise man will hear, and will increase learning; and a man of understanding shall attain unto wise counsels:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>使人明白箴言和譬喻，懂得智慧人的言词和谜语。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To understand a proverb, and the interpretation; the words of the wise, and their dark sayings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>敬畏耶和华是知识的开端。愚妄人藐视智慧和训诲。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge: but fools despise wisdom and instruction.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，要听你父亲的训诲，不可离弃你母亲的法则。（或作指教）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, hear the instruction of thy father, and forsake not the law of thy mother:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为这要作为你头上的华冠，项上的金链。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For they shall be an ornament of grace unto thy head, and chains about thy neck.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，恶人若引诱你，你不可随从。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, if sinners entice thee, consent thou not.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他们若说，你与我们同去，我们要埋伏流人之血，要蹲伏害无罪之人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If they say, Come with us, let us lay wait for blood, let us lurk privily for the innocent without cause:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们好像阴间，把他们活活吞下。他们如同下坑的人，被我们囫囵吞了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let us swallow them up alive as the grave; and whole, as those that go down into the pit:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们必得各样宝物，将所掳来的装满房屋。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>We shall find all precious substance, we shall fill our houses with spoil:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你与我们大家同分。我们共用一各囊袋。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Cast in thy lot among us; let us all have one purse:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，不要与他们同行一道。禁止你脚走他们的路。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, walk not thou in the way with them; refrain thy foot from their path:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为他们的脚奔跑行恶，他们急速流人的血。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For their feet run to evil, and make haste to shed blood.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>好像飞鸟，网罗设在眼前仍不躲避。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Surely in vain the net is spread in the sight of any bird.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这些人埋伏，是为自流己血。蹲伏是为自害己命。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And they lay wait for their own blood; they lurk privily for their own lives.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡贪恋财利的，所行之路，都是如此。这贪恋之心，乃夺去得财者之命。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So are the ways of every one that is greedy of gain; which taketh away the life of the owners thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧在街市上呼喊，在宽阔处发声。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wisdom crieth without; she uttereth her voice in the streets:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在热闹街头喊叫，在城门口，在城中发出言语。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>She crieth in the chief place of concourse, in the openings of the gates: in the city she uttereth her words, saying,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说你们愚昧人喜爱愚昧，亵慢人喜欢亵慢，愚顽人恨恶知识，要到几时呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How long, ye simple ones, will ye love simplicity? and the scorners delight in their scorning, and fools hate knowledge?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们当因我的责备回转。我要将我的灵浇灌你们，将我的话指示你们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Turn you at my reproof: behold, I will pour out my spirit unto you, I will make known my words unto you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我呼唤你们不肯听从。我伸手，无人理会。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Because I have called, and ye refused; I have stretched out my hand, and no man regarded;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>反轻弃我一切的劝戒，不肯受我的责备。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But ye have set at nought all my counsel, and would none of my reproof:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第26段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们遭灾难，我就发笑惊恐临到你们，我必嗤笑。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I also will laugh at your calamity; I will mock when your fear cometh;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第27段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惊恐临到你们，好像狂风，灾难来到，如同暴风。急难痛苦临到你们身上。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When your fear cometh as desolation, and your destruction cometh as a whirlwind; when distress and anguish cometh upon you.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第28段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那时，你们必呼求我，我却不答应，恳切的寻梢我，却寻不见。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then shall they call upon me, but I will not answer; they shall seek me early, but they shall not find me:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第29段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为你们恨恶知识，不喜爱敬畏耶和华，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For that they hated knowledge, and did not choose the fear of the LORD:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第30段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不听我的劝戒，藐视我一切的责备，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>They would none of my counsel: they despised all my reproof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第31段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所以必吃自结的果子，充满自设的计谋。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Therefore shall they eat of the fruit of their own way, and be filled with their own devices.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第32段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚昧人背道，必杀己身，愚顽人安逸，必害己命。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the turning away of the simple shall slay them, and the prosperity of fools shall destroy them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第33段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟有听从我的，必安然居住，得享安静，不怕灾祸。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But whoso hearkeneth unto me shall dwell safely, and shall be quiet from fear of evil.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
