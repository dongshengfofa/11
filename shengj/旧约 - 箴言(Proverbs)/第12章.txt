<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第12章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>喜爱管教的，就是喜爱知识。恨恶责备的，却是畜类。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso loveth instruction loveth knowledge: but he that hateth reproof is brutish.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>善人必蒙耶和华的嗯惠。设诡计的人，耶和华必定他得罪。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A good man obtaineth favour of the LORD: but a man of wicked devices will he condemn.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人靠恶行不能坚立。义人的根，必不动摇。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man shall not be established by wickedness: but the root of the righteous shall not be moved.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>才德的妇人，是丈夫的冠冕。贻羞的妇人，如同朽烂在他丈夫的骨中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A virtuous woman is a crown to her husband: but she that maketh ashamed is as rottenness in his bones.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人的思念是公平。恶人的计谋是诡诈。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The thoughts of the righteous are right: but the counsels of the wicked are deceit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人的言论，是埋伏流人的血。正直人的口，必拯救人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The words of the wicked are to lie in wait for blood: but the mouth of the upright shall deliver them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人倾覆，归于无有。义人的家，必站得住。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wicked are overthrown, and are not: but the house of the righteous shall stand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人必按自己的智慧被称赞。心中乖谬的，必被藐视。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man shall be commended according to his wisdom: but he that is of a perverse heart shall be despised.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>被人轻贱，却有仆人，强如自尊，缺少食物。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that is despised, and hath a servant, is better than he that honoureth himself, and lacketh bread.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人顾惜他牲畜的命。恶人的怜悯，也是残忍。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A righteous man regardeth the life of his beast: but the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耕种自己田地的，必得饱食。追随虚浮的，却是无知。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that tilleth his land shall be satisfied with bread: but he that followeth vain persons is void of understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人想得坏人的网罗。义人的根，得以结实。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wicked desireth the net of evil men: but the root of the righteous yieldeth fruit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人嘴中的过错，是自己的网罗。但义人必脱离患难。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wicked is snared by the transgression of his lips: but the just shall come out of trouble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人因口所结的果子，必饱得美福。人手所作的，必为自己的报应。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth: and the recompence of a man's hands shall be rendered unto him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚妄人所行的，在自己的眼中看为正直。惟智慧人，肯听人的劝教。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The way of a fool is right in his own eyes: but he that hearkeneth unto counsel is wise.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚妄人的恼怒，立时显露。通达人能忍辱藏羞。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A fool's wrath is presently known: but a prudent man covereth shame.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说出真话的，显明公义。作假见证的，显出诡诈。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that speaketh truth sheweth forth righteousness: but a false witness deceit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说话浮躁的，如刀刺人。智慧人的舌头，却为医人的良药。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There is that speaketh like the piercings of a sword: but the tongue of the wise is health.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>口吐真言，永远坚立。舌说谎话，只存片时。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The lip of truth shall be established for ever: but a lying tongue is but for a moment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>图谋恶事的，心存诡诈。劝人和睦的，便得喜乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Deceit is in the heart of them that imagine evil: but to the counsellors of peace is joy.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人不遭灾害恶人满受祸患。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There shall no evil happen to the just: but the wicked shall be filled with mischief.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说谎言的嘴，为耶和华所憎恶。行事诚实的，为他所喜悦。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lying lips are abomination to the LORD: but they that deal truly are his delight.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>通达人隐藏知识。愚昧人的心，彰显愚昧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A prudent man concealeth knowledge: but the heart of fools proclaimeth foolishness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>殷勤人的手必掌权。懒惰的人必服苦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The hand of the diligent shall bear rule: but the slothful shall be under tribute.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第25段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人心忧虑，屈而不伸。一句良言，使心欢乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Heaviness in the heart of man maketh it stoop: but a good word maketh it glad.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第26段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人引导他的邻舍。恶人的道，叫人失迷。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The righteous is more excellent than his neighbour: but the way of the wicked seduceth them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

		<td>懒惰的人，不烤打猎所得的。殷勤的人，却得宝贵的财物。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The slothful man roasteth not that which he took in hunting: but the substance of a diligent man is precious.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
