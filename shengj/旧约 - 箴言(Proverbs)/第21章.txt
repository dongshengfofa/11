<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第21章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王的心在耶和华手中，好像陇沟的水，随意流转。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The king's heart is in the hand of the LORD, as the rivers of water: he turneth it whithersoever he will.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人所行的，在自己的眼中都看为正，惟有耶和华衡量人心。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Every way of a man is right in his own eyes: but the LORD pondereth the hearts.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>行仁义公平，比献祭更蒙耶和华悦纳。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To do justice and judgment is more acceptable to the LORD than sacrifice.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人发达，眼高心傲，这乃是罪。（发达原文是灯）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>An high look, and a proud heart, and the plowing of the wicked, is sin.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>殷勤筹画的，足致丰裕。行事急躁的，都必缺乏。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The thoughts of the diligent tend only to plenteousness; but of every one that is hasty only to want.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>用诡诈之舌求财的，就是自己取死。所得之财，乃是吹来吹去的浮云。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The getting of treasures by a lying tongue is a vanity tossed to and fro of them that seek death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人的强暴，必将自己扫除。因他们不肯按公平行事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The robbery of the wicked shall destroy them; because they refuse to do judgment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>负罪之人的路，甚是弯曲。至于清洁的人，他所行的乃是正直。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The way of man is froward and strange: but as for the pure, his work is right.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>宁可住在房顶的角上，不在宽阔的房屋，与争吵的妇人同住。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is better to dwell in a corner of the housetop, than with a brawling woman in a wide house.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人的心，乐人受祸。他眼并不怜恤邻舍。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The soul of the wicked desireth evil: his neighbour findeth no favour in his eyes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>亵慢的人受刑罚，愚蒙的人就得智慧。智慧人受训诲，便得知识。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When the scorner is punished, the simple is made wise: and when the wise is instructed, he receiveth knowledge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人思想恶人的家，知道恶人倾倒，必至灭亡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The righteous man wisely considereth the house of the wicked: but God overthroweth the wicked for their wickedness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>塞耳不听穷人哀求的，他将来呼吁也不蒙应允。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso stoppeth his ears at the cry of the poor, he also shall cry himself, but shall not be heard.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>暗中送的礼物，挽回怒气。怀中搋的贿赂，止息暴怒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A gift in secret pacifieth anger: and a reward in the bosom strong wrath.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>秉公行义，使义人喜乐，使作孽的人败坏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is joy to the just to do judgment: but destruction shall be to the workers of iniquity.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>迷离通达道路的，必住在阴魂的会中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The man that wandereth out of the way of understanding shall remain in the congregation of the dead.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>爱宴乐的，必致穷乏。好酒爱膏油的，必不富足。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that loveth pleasure shall be a poor man: he that loveth wine and oil shall not be rich.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is better to dwell in the wilderness, than with a contentious and an angry woman.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
