<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第15章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>回答柔和，使怒消退。言语暴戾，触动怒气。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A soft answer turneth away wrath: but grievous words stir up anger.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人的舌，善发知识。愚昧人的口，吐出愚昧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The tongue of the wise useth knowledge aright: but the mouth of fools poureth out foolishness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的眼目，无处不在。恶人善人，他都鉴察。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The eyes of the LORD are in every place, beholding the evil and the good.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>温良的舌，是生命树。乖谬的嘴，使人心碎。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wholesome tongue is a tree of life: but perverseness therein is a breach in the spirit.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚妄人藐视父亲的管教。领受责备的，得着见识。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A fool despiseth his father's instruction: but he that regardeth reproof is prudent.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人家中，多有财宝。恶人得利，反受扰害。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the house of the righteous is much treasure: but in the revenues of the wicked is trouble.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人的嘴，播扬知识。愚昧人的心，并不如此。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The lips of the wise disperse knowledge: but the heart of the foolish doeth not so.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人献祭，为耶和华所憎恶。正直人祈祷，为他所喜悦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The sacrifice of the wicked is an abomination to the LORD: but the prayer of the upright is his delight.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人的道路，为耶和华所憎恶。追求公义的，为他所喜爱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The way of the wicked is an abomination unto the LORD: but he loveth him that followeth after righteousness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>舍弃正路的，必受严刑。恨恶责备的，必致死亡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Correction is grievous unto him that forsaketh the way: and he that hateth reproof shall die.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>阴间和死亡，尚在耶和华眼前，何况世人的心呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hell and destruction are before the LORD: how much more then the hearts of the children of men?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>亵慢人不爱受责备。他也不就近智慧人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A scorner loveth not one that reproveth him: neither will he go unto the wise.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>心中喜乐，面带笑容。心里忧愁，灵被损伤。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A merry heart maketh a cheerful countenance: but by sorrow of the heart the spirit is broken.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>聪明人心求知识。愚昧人口吃愚昧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The heart of him that hath understanding seeketh knowledge: but the mouth of fools feedeth on foolishness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>困苦人的日子，都是愁苦。心中欢畅的，常享丰筵。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the days of the afflicted are evil: but he that is of a merry heart hath a continual feast.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>少有财宝，敬畏耶和华，强如多有财宝，烦乱不安。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Better is little with the fear of the LORD than great treasure and trouble therewith.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>吃素菜，彼此相爱，强如吃肥牛，彼此相恨。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Better is a dinner of herbs where love is, than a stalled ox and hatred therewith.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>暴怒的人，挑启争端。忍怒的人，止息分争。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wrathful man stirreth up strife: but he that is slow to anger appeaseth strife.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The way of the slothful man is as an hedge of thorns: but the way of the righteous is made plain.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	<tr>

	<tr>
