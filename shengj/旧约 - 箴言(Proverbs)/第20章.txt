<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第20章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>酒能使人亵慢，浓酒使人喧囔。凡因酒错误的，就无智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wine is a mocker, strong drink is raging: and whosoever is deceived thereby is not wise.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王的威吓，如同狮子吼叫。惹动他怒的，是自害己命。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fear of a king is as the roaring of a lion: whoso provoketh him to anger sinneth against his own soul.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>远离分争，是人的尊荣。愚妄人都爱争闹。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is an honour for a man to cease from strife: but every fool will be meddling.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>懒惰人因冬寒不肯耕种。到收割的时候，他必讨饭，而无所得。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The sluggard will not plow by reason of the cold; therefore shall he beg in harvest, and have nothing.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人心怀藏谋略，好像深水。惟明哲人，才能汲引出来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Counsel in the heart of man is like deep water; but a man of understanding will draw it out.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人多述说自己的仁慈。但忠信人谁能遇着呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Most men will proclaim every one his own goodness: but a faithful man who can find?</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

</table>

	<tr>

</table>
