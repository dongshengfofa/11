<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第10章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所罗门的箴言。智慧之子，使父亲欢乐。愚昧之子，叫母亲担忧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The proverbs of Solomon. A wise son maketh a glad father: but a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不义之财，毫无益处。惟有公义，能救人脱离死亡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Treasures of wickedness profit nothing: but righteousness delivereth from death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华不使义人受饥饿。恶人所欲的他必推开。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish: but he casteth away the substance of the wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>手懒的要受贫穷。手勤的却要富足。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He becometh poor that dealeth with a slack hand: but the hand of the diligent maketh rich.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>夏天聚敛的，是智慧之子。收割时沉睡的，是贻羞之子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that gathereth in summer is a wise son: but he that sleepeth in harvest is a son that causeth shame.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>福祉临到义人的头。强暴蒙蔽恶人的口。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Blessings are upon the head of the just: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人的纪念被称赞。恶人的名字必朽烂。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The memory of the just is blessed: but the name of the wicked shall rot.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>心中智慧的，必受命令。口里愚妄的，必致倾倒。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wise in heart will receive commandments: but a prating fool shall fall.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>行正直路的，步步安稳。走弯曲道的，必致败露。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that walketh uprightly walketh surely: but he that perverteth his ways shall be known.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以眼传神的，使人忧患。口里愚妄的，必致倾倒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that winketh with the eye causeth sorrow: but a prating fool shall fall.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人的口是生命的泉源。强暴蒙蔽恶人的口。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The mouth of a righteous man is a well of life: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恨能挑启争端。爱能遮掩一切过错。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hatred stirreth up strifes: but love covereth all sins.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>明哲人嘴里有智慧。无知人背上受刑杖。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the lips of him that hath understanding wisdom is found: but a rod is for the back of him that is void of understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人积存知识。愚妄人的口速致败坏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wise men lay up knowledge: but the mouth of the foolish is near destruction.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>富户的财物，是他的坚城。穷人的贫乏，是他的败坏。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The rich man's wealth is his strong city: the destruction of the poor is their poverty.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人的勤劳致生。恶人的进项致死。（死原文作罪）</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The labour of the righteous tendeth to life: the fruit of the wicked to sin.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谨守训诲的，乃在生命的道上。违弃责备的，便失迷了路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He is in the way of life that keepeth instruction: but he that refuseth reproof erreth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>隐藏怨恨的，有说谎的嘴。口出谗谤的，是愚妄的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that hideth hatred with lying lips, and he that uttereth a slander, is a fool.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>多言多语难免有过。禁止嘴唇是有智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the multitude of words there wanteth not sin: but he that refraineth his lips is wise.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人的舌，乃似高银。恶人的心，所值无几。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The tongue of the just is as choice silver: the heart of the wicked is little worth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The lips of the righteous feed many: but fools die for want of wisdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is as sport to a fool to do mischief: but a man of understanding hath wisdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As the whirlwind passeth, so is the wicked no more: but the righteous is an everlasting foundation.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fear of the LORD prolongeth days: but the years of the wicked shall be shortened.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
