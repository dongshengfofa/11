<p>第1段</p>

		<td>Wisdom hath builded her house, she hath hewn out her seven pillars:</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>宰杀牲畜，调和旨酒，设摆筵席。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>She hath killed her beasts; she hath mingled her wine; she hath also furnished her table.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>打发使女出去，自己在城中至高处呼叫，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>She hath sent forth her maidens: she crieth upon the highest places of the city,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说，谁是愚蒙人，可以转到这里来。又对那无知的人说，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso is simple, let him turn in hither: as for him that wanteth understanding, she saith to him,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们来，吃我的饼，喝我调和的酒。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Come, eat of my bread, and drink of the wine which I have mingled.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们愚蒙人，要舍弃愚蒙，就得存活。并要走光明的道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Forsake the foolish, and live; and go in the way of understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>指斥亵慢人的，必受辱骂。责备恶人的，必被玷污。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that reproveth a scorner getteth to himself shame: and he that rebuketh a wicked man getteth himself a blot.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要责备亵慢人，恐怕他恨你。要责备智慧人，他必爱你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Reprove not a scorner, lest he hate thee: rebuke a wise man, and he will love thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>教导智慧人，他就越发有智慧。指示义人，他就增长学问。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Give instruction to a wise man, and he will be yet wiser: teach a just man, and he will increase in learning.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
