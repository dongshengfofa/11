<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第13章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧子听父亲的教训。亵慢人不听责备。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wise son heareth his father's instruction: but a scorner heareth not rebuke.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人因口所结的果子，必享美福。奸诈人必遭强暴。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man shall eat good by the fruit of his mouth: but the soul of the transgressors shall eat violence.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谨守口的，得保生命。大张嘴的，必致败亡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that keepeth his mouth keepeth his life: but he that openeth wide his lips shall have destruction.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>懒惰人羡慕，却无所得。殷勤人必得丰裕。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The soul of the sluggard desireth, and hath nothing: but the soul of the diligent shall be made fat.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人恨恶谎言。恶人有臭名，且致惭愧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A righteous man hateth lying: but a wicked man is loathsome, and cometh to shame.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>行为正直的，有公义保守。犯罪的被邪恶倾覆。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Righteousness keepeth him that is upright in the way: but wickedness overthroweth the sinner.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>假作富足的，却一无所有。装作穷乏的，却广有财物。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There is that maketh himself rich, yet hath nothing: there is that maketh himself poor, yet hath great riches.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人的赀财，是他生命的赎价。穷乏人却听不见威吓的话。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The ransom of a man's life are his riches: but the poor heareth not rebuke.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人的光明亮。（明亮原文作喜欢）恶人的灯要熄灭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The light of the righteous rejoiceth: but the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>骄傲只启争竞。听劝言的，却有智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Only by pride cometh contention: but with the well advised is wisdom.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不劳而得之财，必然消耗。勤劳积蓄的，必见加增。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wealth gotten by vanity shall be diminished: but he that gathereth by labour shall increase.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所盼望的迟延未得，令人心忧。所愿意的临到，却是生命树。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hope deferred maketh the heart sick: but when the desire cometh, it is a tree of life.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>藐视训言的，自取灭亡。敬畏诫命的，必得善报。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso despiseth the word shall be destroyed: but he that feareth the commandment shall be rewarded.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧人的法则，（或作指教）是生命的泉源，可以使人离开死亡的网罗。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The law of the wise is a fountain of life, to depart from the snares of death.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>美好的聪明，使人蒙恩。奸诈人的道路，崎岖难行。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Good understanding giveth favour: but the way of transgressors is hard.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡通达人都凭知识行事。愚昧人张扬自己的愚昧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Every prudent man dealeth with knowledge: but a fool layeth open his folly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>奸恶的使者，必陷在祸患里。忠信的使臣，乃医人的良药。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wicked messenger falleth into mischief: but a faithful ambassador is health.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>弃绝教管的，必致贫受辱。领受责备的，必得尊荣。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Poverty and shame shall be to him that refuseth instruction: but he that regardeth reproof shall be honoured.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所欲的成就，心觉甘甜。远离恶事，为愚昧人所憎恶。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The desire accomplished is sweet to the soul: but it is abomination to fools to depart from evil.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>与智慧人同行的，必得智慧。和愚昧人作伴的，必受亏损。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that walketh with wise men shall be wise: but a companion of fools shall be destroyed.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>祸患追赶罪人。义人必得善报。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Evil pursueth sinners: but to the righteous good shall be repayed.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>善人给子孙遗留产业。罪人为义人积存赀财。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A good man leaveth an inheritance to his children's children: and the wealth of the sinner is laid up for the just.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>穷人耕种多得粮食，但因不义有消灭的。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Much food is in the tillage of the poor: but there is that is destroyed for want of judgment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第24段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不忍用杖打儿子的，是恨恶他。疼爱儿子的，随时管教。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that spareth his rod hateth his son: but he that loveth him chasteneth him betimes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>义人吃得饱足。恶人肚腹缺粮。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The righteous eateth to the satisfying of his soul: but the belly of the wicked shall want.</td>
