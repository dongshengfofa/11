<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第19章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>行为纯正的贫穷人，胜过乖谬愚妄的富足人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Better is the poor that walketh in his integrity, than he that is perverse in his lips, and is a fool.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>心无知识的，乃为不善。脚步急快的难免犯罪。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Also, that the soul be without knowledge, it is not good; and he that hasteth with his feet sinneth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人的愚昧，倾败他的道。他的心也抱怨耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The foolishness of man perverteth his way: and his heart fretteth against the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>财物使朋友增多。但穷人朋友远离。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Wealth maketh many friends; but the poor is separated from his neighbour.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>作假见证的，必不免受罚。吐出谎言的，终不能逃脱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A false witness shall not be unpunished, and he that speaketh lies shall not escape.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>好施散的，有多人求他的恩情。爱送礼的，人都为他的朋友。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Many will intreat the favour of the prince: and every man is a friend to him that giveth gifts.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>贫穷人弟兄都恨他，何况他的朋友，更远离他。他用言语追随，他们却走了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the brethren of the poor do hate him: how much more do his friends go far from him? he pursueth them with words, yet they are wanting to him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>得着智慧的，爱惜生命，保守聪明的，必得好处。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He that getteth wisdom loveth his own soul: he that keepeth understanding shall find good.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>

		<td>作假见证的，不免受罚。吐出谎言的，也必灭亡。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A false witness shall not be unpunished, and he that speaketh lies shall perish.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愚昧人宴乐度日，是不合宜的，何况仆人管辖王子呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Delight is not seemly for a fool; much less for a servant to have rule over princes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人有见识，就不轻易发怒。宽恕人的过失，便是自己的荣耀。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The discretion of a man deferreth his anger; and it is his glory to pass over a transgression.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王的忿怒，好像狮子吼叫。他的恩典，却如草上的甘露。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The king's wrath is as the roaring of a lion; but his favour is as dew upon the grass.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A foolish son is the calamity of his father: and the contentions of a wife are a continual dropping.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
