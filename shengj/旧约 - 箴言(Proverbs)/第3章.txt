<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第3章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，不要忘记我的法则。（或作指教）你心要谨守我的诫命。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, forget not my law; but let thine heart keep my commandments:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为他必将长久的日子，生命的年数，与平安，加给你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For length of days, and long life, and peace, shall they add to thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不可使慈爱诚实离开你。要系在你颈项上，刻在你心版上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let not mercy and truth forsake thee: bind them about thy neck; write them upon the table of thine heart:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这样，你必在神和世人眼前蒙恩宠，有聪明。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So shalt thou find favour and good understanding in the sight of God and man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要专心仰赖耶和华，不可倚靠自己的聪明。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Trust in the LORD with all thine heart; and lean not unto thine own understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在你一切所行的事上，都要认定他，他必指引你的路。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In all thy ways acknowledge him, and he shall direct thy paths.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要自以为有智慧。要敬畏耶和华，远离恶事。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Be not wise in thine own eyes: fear the LORD, and depart from evil.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这便医治你的肚脐，滋润你的百骨。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It shall be health to thy navel, and marrow to thy bones.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要以财物，和一切初熟的土产，尊荣耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Honour the LORD with thy substance, and with the firstfruits of all thine increase:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>这样，你的仓房，必充满有馀，你的酒榨，有新酒盈溢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So shall thy barns be filled with plenty, and thy presses shall burst out with new wine.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，你不可轻看耶和华的管教，（或作惩治）也不可厌烦他的责备。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, despise not the chastening of the LORD; neither be weary of his correction:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为耶和华所爱的，他必责备。正如父亲责备所喜爱的儿子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For whom the LORD loveth he correcteth; even as a father the son in whom he delighteth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>得智慧，得聪明的，这人便为有福。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Happy is the man that findeth wisdom, and the man that getteth understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为得智慧胜过得银子，其利益强如精金。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the merchandise of it is better than the merchandise of silver, and the gain thereof than fine gold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>She is more precious than rubies: and all the things thou canst desire are not to be compared unto her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

		<td>他右手有长寿。左手有富贵。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Length of days is in her right hand; and in her left hand riches and honour.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

</table>

	<tr>
