<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第5章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，要留心我智慧的话语，侧耳听我聪明的言词。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, attend unto my wisdom, and bow thine ear to my understanding:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>为要使你谨守谋略，嘴唇保存知识。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That thou mayest regard discretion, and that thy lips may keep knowledge.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为淫妇的嘴滴下蜂蜜，他的口比油更滑。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the lips of a strange woman drop as an honeycomb, and her mouth is smoother than oil:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>至终却苦似茵？？，快如两刃的刀。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But her end is bitter as wormwood, sharp as a two-edged sword.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的脚，下入死地。他脚步，踏住阴间。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Her feet go down to death; her steps take hold on hell.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>以致他找不着生命平坦的道。他的路变迁不定，自己还不知道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lest thou shouldest ponder the path of life, her ways are moveable, that thou canst not know them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>众子阿，现在要听从我，不可离弃我口中的话。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Hear me now therefore, O ye children, and depart not from the words of my mouth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你所行的道要离他远，不可就近他的房门。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Remove thy way far from her, and come not nigh the door of her house:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恐怕将你的尊荣给别人，将你的岁月给残忍的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lest thou give thine honour unto others, and thy years unto the cruel:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恐怕外人满得你的力量，你劳碌得来的，归入外人的家。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Lest strangers be filled with thy wealth; and thy labours be in the house of a stranger;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>终久你皮肉和身体消毁，你就悲叹，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And thou mourn at the last, when thy flesh and thy body are consumed,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>说，我怎吗恨恶训诲，心中藐视责备，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And say, How have I hated instruction, and my heart despised reproof;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>也不听从我师父的话，又不侧耳听那教训我的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And have not obeyed the voice of my teachers, nor inclined mine ear to them that instructed me!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我在圣会里，几乎落在诸般恶中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I was almost in all evil in the midst of the congregation and assembly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你要喝自己池中的水，饮自己井里的活水。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Drink waters out of thine own cistern, and running waters out of thine own well.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的泉源岂可涨溢在外。你的河水岂可流在街上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let thy fountains be dispersed abroad, and rivers of waters in the streets.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟独归你一人，不可与外人同用。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let them be only thine own, and not strangers' with thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要使你的泉源蒙福。要喜悦你幼年所娶的妻。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let thy fountain be blessed: and rejoice with the wife of thy youth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他如可爱的？？鹿，可喜的母鹿。愿他的胸怀，使你时时知足。他的爱情，使你常常恋慕。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let her be as the loving hind and pleasant roe; let her breasts satisfy thee at all times; and be thou ravished always with her love.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，你为何恋慕淫妇，为何抱外女的胸怀。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And why wilt thou, my son, be ravished with a strange woman, and embrace the bosom of a stranger?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为人所行的道，都在耶和华眼前。他也修平人一切的路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the ways of man are before the eyes of the LORD, and he pondereth all his goings.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>恶人必被自己的罪孽捉住。他必被自己的罪恶如绳索缠绕。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His own iniquities shall take the wicked himself, and he shall be holden with the cords of his sins.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第23段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他因不受训诲，就必死亡。又因愚昧过甚，必走差了路。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He shall die without instruction; and in the greatness of his folly he shall go astray.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
