<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第16章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>心中的谋算在乎人。舌头的应对，由于耶和华。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The preparations of the heart in man, and the answer of the tongue, is from the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人一切所行的，在自己眼中看为清洁。惟有耶和华衡量人心。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>All the ways of a man are clean in his own eyes; but the LORD weigheth the spirits.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你所作的，要交托耶和华，你所谋的，就必成立。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Commit thy works unto the LORD, and thy thoughts shall be established.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华所造的，各适其用。就是恶人，也为祸患的日子所造。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD hath made all things for himself: yea, even the wicked for the day of evil.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡心里骄傲的，为耶和华所憎恶。虽然连手，他必不免受罚。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Every one that is proud in heart is an abomination to the LORD: though hand join in hand, he shall not be unpunished.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因怜悯诚实，罪孽得赎。敬畏耶和华的，远离恶事。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>By mercy and truth iniquity is purged: and by the fear of the LORD men depart from evil.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人所行的若蒙耶和华喜悦，耶和华也使他的仇敌与他和好。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When a man's ways please the LORD, he maketh even his enemies to be at peace with him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>多有财利，行事不义，不如少有财利，行事公义。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Better is a little with righteousness than great revenues without right.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>人心筹算自己的道路。惟耶和华指引他的脚步。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A man's heart deviseth his way: but the LORD directeth his steps.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王的嘴中有神语。审判之时，他的口，必不差错。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A divine sentence is in the lips of the king: his mouth transgresseth not in judgment.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>公道的天平和秤，都属耶和华。囊中一切法码，都为他所定。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A just weight and balance are the LORD's: all the weights of the bag are his work.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>作恶为王所憎恶。因国位是靠公义坚立。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>It is an abomination to kings to commit wickedness: for the throne is established by righteousness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>公义的嘴，为王所喜悦。说正直话的，为王所喜爱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Righteous lips are the delight of kings; and they love him that speaketh right.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王的震怒，如杀人的使者。但智慧人能止息王怒。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The wrath of a king is as messengers of death: but a wise man will pacify it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王的脸光，使人有生命。王的恩典，好像春云时雨。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In the light of the king's countenance is life; and his favour is as a cloud of the latter rain.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How much better is it to get wisdom than gold! and to get understanding rather to be chosen than silver!</td>
	</tr>
</table>

</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>
