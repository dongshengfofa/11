<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第17章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>设筵满屋，大家相争，不如有块乾饼，大家相安。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Better is a dry morsel, and quietness therewith, than an house full of sacrifices with strife.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wise servant shall have rule over a son that causeth shame, and shall have part of the inheritance among the brethren.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>鼎为炼银，炉为炼金。惟有耶和华熬炼人心。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fining pot is for silver, and the furnace for gold: but the LORD trieth the hearts.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A wicked doer giveth heed to false lips; and a liar giveth ear to a naughty tongue.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whoso mocketh the poor reproacheth his Maker: and he that is glad at calamities shall not be unpunished.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Children's children are the crown of old men; and the glory of children are their fathers.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

</table>

	<tr>

</table>
