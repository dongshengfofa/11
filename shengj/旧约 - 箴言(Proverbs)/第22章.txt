<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第22章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A GOOD name is rather to be chosen than great riches, and loving favour rather than silver and gold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The rich and poor meet together: the LORD is the maker of them all.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>通达人见祸藏躲。愚蒙人前往受害。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A prudent man foreseeth the evil, and hideth himself: but the simple pass on, and are punished.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>By humility and the fear of the LORD are riches, and honour, and life.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thorns and snares are in the way of the froward: he that doth keep his soul shall be far from them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>
