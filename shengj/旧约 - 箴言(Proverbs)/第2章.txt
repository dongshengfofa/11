<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 箴言(Proverbs) 第2章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我儿，你若领受我的言语，存记我得命令，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My son, if thou wilt receive my words, and hide my commandments with thee;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>侧耳听智慧，专心求聪明。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>So that thou incline thine ear unto wisdom, and apply thine heart to understanding;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>呼求明哲，扬声求聪明，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Yea, if thou criest after knowledge, and liftest up thy voice for understanding;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>寻梢他如寻梢银子，搜求他如搜求隐藏的珍宝，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thou seekest her as silver, and searchest for her as for hid treasures;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你就明白敬畏耶和华，得以认识神。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then shalt thou understand the fear of the LORD, and find the knowledge of God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为耶和华赐人智慧。知识和聪明，都由他口而出。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the LORD giveth wisdom: out of his mouth cometh knowledge and understanding.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他给正直人存留真智慧，给行为纯正的人作盾牌。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He layeth up sound wisdom for the righteous: he is a buckler to them that walk uprightly.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>为要保守公平人的路，护庇虔敬人的道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He keepeth the paths of judgment, and preserveth the way of his saints.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你也必明白仁义，公平，正直，一切的善道。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then shalt thou understand righteousness, and judgment, and equity; yea, every good path.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧必入你心。你的灵要以知识为美。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When wisdom entereth into thine heart, and knowledge is pleasant unto thy soul;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>谋略必护卫你。聪明必保守你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Discretion shall preserve thee, understanding shall keep thee:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要救你脱离恶道，（恶道或作恶人的道）脱离说乖谬话的人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To deliver thee from the way of the evil man, from the man that speaketh froward things;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那等人舍弃正直的路，行走黑暗的道，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who leave the paths of uprightness, to walk in the ways of darkness;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>喜欢作恶，喜爱恶人的乖僻。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who rejoice to do evil, and delight in the frowardness of the wicked;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>在他们的道中弯曲，在他们的路上偏僻。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whose ways are crooked, and they froward in their paths:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧要救你拖离淫妇，就是那油嘴滑舌的外女。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>To deliver thee from the strange woman, even from the stranger which flattereth with her words;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他离弃幼年的配偶，忘了神的盟约。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Which forsaketh the guide of her youth, and forgetteth the covenant of her God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第18段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的家陷入死地，他的路偏向阴间。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For her house inclineth unto death, and her paths unto the dead.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第19段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡到他那里去的不得转回，也得不着生命的路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>None that go unto her return again, neither take they hold of the paths of life.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第20段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>智慧必使你行善人的道，守义人的路。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>That thou mayest walk in the way of good men, and keep the paths of the righteous.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第21段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>正直人必在世上居住。完全人必在地上存留。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For the upright shall dwell in the land, and the perfect shall remain in it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第22段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>惟有恶人必然剪除。奸诈的必然拔出。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>But the wicked shall be cut off from the earth, and the transgressors shall be rooted out of it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
