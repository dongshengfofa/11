<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 撒迦利亚书(Zechariah) 第12章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华论以色列的默示。铺张诸天，建立地基，造人里面之灵的耶和华说。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The burden of the word of the LORD for Israel, saith the LORD, which stretcheth forth the heavens, and layeth the foundation of the earth, and formeth the spirit of man within him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必使耶路撒冷被围困的时候，向四围列国的民成为令人昏醉的杯，这默示也论到犹大（或作犹大也是如此）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, I will make Jerusalem a cup of trembling unto all the people round about, when they shall be in the siege both against Judah and against Jerusalem.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那日，我必使耶路撒冷向聚集攻击他的万民，当作一块重石头。凡举起的必受重伤。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And in that day will I make Jerusalem a burdensome stone for all people: all that burden themselves with it shall be cut in pieces, though all the people of the earth be gathered together against it.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华说，到那日，我必使一切马匹惊惶，使骑马的颠狂。我必看顾犹大家，使列国的一切马匹瞎眼。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In that day, saith the LORD, I will smite every horse with astonishment, and his rider with madness: and I will open mine eyes upon the house of Judah, and will smite every horse of the people with blindness.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>犹大的族长，必心里说，耶路撒冷的居民，倚靠万军之耶和华他们的神，就作我们的能力。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the governors of Judah shall say in their heart, The inhabitants of Jerusalem shall be my strength in the LORD of hosts their God.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那日，我必使犹大的族长，如火盆在木柴中，又如火把在禾捆里。他们必左右烧灭四围列国的民。耶路撒冷人必仍住本处，就是耶路撒冷。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In that day will I make the governors of Judah like an hearth of fire among the wood, and like a torch of fire in a sheaf; and they shall devour all the people round about, on the right hand and on the left: and Jerusalem shall be inhabited again in her own place, even in Jerusalem.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华必先拯救犹大的帐棚，免得大卫家的荣耀，和耶路撒冷居民的荣耀，胜过犹大。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD also shall save the tents of Judah first, that the glory of the house of David and the glory of the inhabitants of Jerusalem do not magnify themselves against Judah.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那日，耶和华必保护耶路撒冷的居民。他们中间软弱的，必如大卫。大卫的家，必如神，如行在他们前面之耶和华的使者。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>In that day shall the LORD defend the inhabitants of Jerusalem; and he that is feeble among them at that day shall be as David; and the house of David shall be as God, as the angel of the LORD before them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那日，我必定意灭绝来攻击耶路撒冷各国的民。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And it shall come to pass in that day, that I will seek to destroy all the nations that come against Jerusalem.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必将那施恩叫人恳求的灵，浇灌大卫家，和耶路撒冷的居民。他们必仰望我，或作他本节同就是他们所扎的。必为我悲哀，如丧独生子，又为我愁苦，如丧长子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will pour upon the house of David, and upon the inhabitants of Jerusalem, the spirit of grace and of supplications: and they shall look upon me whom they have pierced, and they shall mourn for him, as one mourneth for his only son, and shall be in bitterness for him, as one that is in bitterness for his firstborn.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>

</table>
