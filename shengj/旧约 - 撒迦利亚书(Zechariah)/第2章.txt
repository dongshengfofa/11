<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 撒迦利亚书(Zechariah) 第2章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我又举目观看，见一人手拿准绳。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I lifted up mine eyes again, and looked, and behold a man with a measuring line in his hand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我说，你往那里去。他对我说，要去量耶路撒冷，看有多宽多长。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Then said I, Whither goest thou? And he said unto me, To measure Jerusalem, to see what is the breadth thereof, and what is the length thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>与我说话的天使去的时候，又有一位天使迎着他来，</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And, behold, the angel that talked with me went forth, and another angel went out to meet him,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>对他说，你跑去告诉那少年人说，耶路撒冷必有人居住，好像无城墙的乡村，因为人民和牲畜甚多。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And said unto him, Run, speak to this young man, saying, Jerusalem shall be inhabited as towns without walls for the multitude of men and cattle therein:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华说，我要作耶路撒冷四围的火城，并要作其中的荣耀。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For I, saith the LORD, will be unto her a wall of fire round about, and will be the glory in the midst of her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华说，我从前分散你们在天的四方。（原文作犹如天的四风），现在你们要从北方之地逃回。这是耶和华说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ho, ho, come forth, and flee from the land of the north, saith the LORD: for I have spread you abroad as the four winds of the heaven, saith the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>与巴比伦人同住的锡安民哪，应当逃脱。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Deliver thyself, O Zion, that dwellest with the daughter of Babylon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>万军之耶和华说，在显出荣耀之后，差遣我去惩罚那掳掠你们的列国。摸你们的，就是摸他眼中的瞳人。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For thus saith the LORD of hosts; After the glory hath he sent me unto the nations which spoiled you: for he that toucheth you toucheth the apple of his eye.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>看哪，我（或作他）要向他们抡手，他们就必作服事他们之人的掳物，你们便知道万军之耶和华差遣我了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For, behold, I will shake mine hand upon them, and they shall be a spoil to their servants: and ye shall know that the LORD of hosts hath sent me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>锡安城阿，应当欢乐歌唱，因为我来要住在你中间。这是耶和华说的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Sing and rejoice, O daughter of Zion: for, lo, I come, and I will dwell in the midst of thee, saith the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那时，必有许多国归附耶和华，作他（原文作我）的子民。他（原文是我）要住在你中间，你就知道万军之耶和华差遣我到你那里去了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And many nations shall be joined to the LORD in that day, and shall be my people: and I will dwell in the midst of thee, and thou shalt know that the LORD of hosts hath sent me unto thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华必收回犹大作他圣地的分，也必再拣选耶路撒冷。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the LORD shall inherit Judah his portion in the holy land, and shall choose Jerusalem again.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>凡有血气的，都当在耶和华面前静默无声。因为他兴起，从圣所出来了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Be silent, O all flesh, before the LORD: for he is raised up out of his holy habitation.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
