<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 撒迦利亚书(Zechariah) 第9章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华的默示应验在哈得拉地，大马士革（世人和以色列各支派的眼目都仰望耶和华）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The burden of the word of the LORD in the land of Hadrach, and Damascus shall be the rest thereof: when the eyes of man, as of all the tribes of Israel, shall be toward the LORD.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>和靠近的哈马，并推罗，西顿，因为这二城的人大有智慧。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Hamath also shall border thereby; Tyrus, and Zidon, though it be very wise.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>推罗为自己修筑保障，积蓄银子如尘沙，堆起精金如街上的泥土。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And Tyrus did build herself a strong hold, and heaped up silver as the dust, and fine gold as the mire of the streets.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>主必赶出她，打败她海上的权利。她必被火烧灭。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, the LORD will cast her out, and he will smite her power in the sea; and she shall be devoured with fire.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>亚实基伦看见必惧怕。迦萨看见甚痛苦。以革伦因失了盼望蒙羞。迦萨必不再有君王。亚实基伦也不再有居民。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Ashkelon shall see it, and fear; Gaza also shall see it, and be very sorrowful, and Ekron; for her expectation shall be ashamed; and the king shall perish from Gaza, and Ashkelon shall not be inhabited.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>私生子（或作外族人）必住在亚实突。我必除灭非利士人的骄傲。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And a bastard shall dwell in Ashdod, and I will cut off the pride of the Philistines.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必除去他口中带血之肉，和牙齿内可憎之物。他必作为馀剩的人归与我们的神，必在犹大像族长，以革伦人必如耶布斯人。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will take away his blood out of his mouth, and his abominations from between his teeth: but he that remaineth, even he, shall be for our God, and he shall be as a governor in Judah, and Ekron as a Jebusite.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必在我家的四围安营，使敌军不得任意往来。暴虐的人也不再经过，因为我亲眼看顾我的家。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will encamp about mine house because of the army, because of him that passeth by, and because of him that returneth: and no oppressor shall pass through them any more: for now have I seen with mine eyes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>锡安的民哪，应当大大喜乐。耶路撒冷的民哪，应当欢呼。看哪，你的王来到你这里。他是公义的，并且施行拯救，谦谦和和地骑着驴，就是骑着驴的驹子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Rejoice greatly, O daughter of Zion; shout, O daughter of Jerusalem: behold, thy King cometh unto thee: he is just, and having salvation; lowly, and riding upon an ass, and upon a colt the foal of an ass.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我必除灭以法莲的战车，和耶路撒冷的战马。争战的弓也必除灭。他必向列国讲和平。他权柄必从这海管到那海，从大河管到地极。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And I will cut off the chariot from Ephraim, and the horse from Jerusalem, and the battle bow shall be cut off: and he shall speak peace unto the heathen: and his dominion shall be from sea even to sea, and from the river even to the ends of the earth.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>锡安哪，我因与你立约的血，将你中间被掳而囚的人，从无水的坑中释放出来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As for thee also, by the blood of thy covenant I have sent forth thy prisoners out of the pit wherein is no water.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你们被囚而有指望的人，都要转回保障。我今日说明，我必加倍赐福给你们。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Turn you to the strong hold, ye prisoners of hope: even to day do I declare that I will render double unto thee;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我拿犹大作上弦的弓，我拿以法莲为张弓的箭。锡安哪，我要激发你的众子，攻击希腊（原文作雅完）的众子，使你如勇士的刀。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>When I have bent Judah for me, filled the bow with Ephraim, and raised up thy sons, O Zion, against thy sons, O Greece, and made thee as the sword of a mighty man.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶和华必显现在他们以上。他的箭必射出像闪电。主耶和华必吹角，乘南方的旋风而行。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the LORD shall be seen over them, and his arrow shall go forth as the lightning: and the LORD God shall blow the trumpet, and shall go with whirlwinds of the south.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>万军之耶和华必保护他们。他们必吞灭仇敌，践踏弹石。他们必喝血呐喊，犹如饮酒。他们必像盛满血的碗，又像坛的四角满了血。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The LORD of hosts shall defend them; and they shall devour, and subdue with sling stones; and they shall drink, and make a noise as through wine; and they shall be filled like bowls, and as the corners of the altar.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>当那日，耶和华他们的神必看他的民，如群羊拯救他们。因为他们必像冠冕上的宝石，高举在他的地以上（高举云云或作在他的地上发光辉）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the LORD their God shall save them in that day as the flock of his people: for they shall be as the stones of a crown, lifted up as an ensign upon his land.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
