<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 雅歌(Song of Songs) 第7章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王女阿，你的脚在鞋中何其美好。你的大腿圆润，好像美玉，是巧匠的手做成的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How beautiful are thy feet with shoes, O prince's daughter! the joints of thy thighs are like jewels, the work of the hands of a cunning workman.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的肚脐如圆杯，不缺调和的酒。你的腰如一堆麦子，周围有百合花。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy navel is like a round goblet, which wanteth not liquor: thy belly is like an heap of wheat set about with lilies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的两乳好像一对小鹿，就是母鹿双生的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy two breasts are like two young roes that are twins.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的颈项如象牙台。你的眼目像希实本，巴特拉并门旁的水池。你的鼻子彷佛朝大马士革的黎巴嫩塔。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy neck is as a tower of ivory; thine eyes like the fishpools in Heshbon, by the gate of Bathrabbim: thy nose is as the tower of Lebanon which looketh toward Damascus.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的头在你身上好像迦密山。你头上的发是紫黑色。王的心因这下垂的发绺系住了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thine head upon thee is like Carmel, and the hair of thine head like purple; the king is held in the galleries.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我所爱的，你何其美好。何其可悦，使人欢畅喜乐。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How fair and how pleasant art thou, O love, for delights!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的身量好像棕树。你的两乳如同其上的果子，累累下垂。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>This thy stature is like to a palm tree, and thy breasts to clusters of grapes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我说，我要上这棕树，抓住枝子。愿你的两乳好像葡萄累累下垂，你鼻子的气味香如苹果。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I said, I will go up to the palm tree, I will take hold of the boughs thereof: now also thy breasts shall be as clusters of the vine, and the smell of thy nose like apples;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的口如上好的酒，女子说，为我的良人下咽舒畅，流入睡觉人的嘴中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>And the roof of thy mouth like the best wine for my beloved, that goeth down sweetly, causing the lips of those that are asleep to speak.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我属我的良人，他也恋慕我。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I am my beloved's, and his desire is toward me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的良人，来吧，你我可以往田间去。你我可以在村庄住宿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Come, my beloved, let us go forth into the field; let us lodge in the villages.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们早晨起来往葡萄园去，看看葡萄发芽开花没有，石榴放蕊没有。我在那里要将我的爱情给你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let us get up early to the vineyards; let us see if the vine flourish, whether the tender grape appear, and the pomegranates bud forth: there will I give thee my loves.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>风茄放香，在我们的门内有各样新陈佳美的果子。我的良人，这都是我为你存留的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The mandrakes give a smell, and at our gates are all manner of pleasant fruits, new and old, which I have laid up for thee, O my beloved.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
