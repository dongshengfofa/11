<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 雅歌(Song of Songs) 第4章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的佳偶，你甚美丽，你甚美丽。你的眼在帕子内好像鸽子眼。你的头发如同山羊群卧在基列山旁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Behold, thou art fair, my love; behold, thou art fair; thou hast doves' eyes within thy locks: thy hair is as a flock of goats, that appear from mount Gilead.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的牙齿如新剪毛的一群母羊，洗净上来，个个都有双生，没有一只丧掉子的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy teeth are like a flock of sheep that are even shorn, which came up from the washing; whereof every one bear twins, and none is barren among them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的唇好像一条朱红线，你的嘴也秀美。你的两太阳在帕子内，如同一块石榴。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy lips are like a thread of scarlet, and thy speech is comely: thy temples are like a piece of a pomegranate within thy locks.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的颈项好像大卫建造收藏军器的高台，其上悬挂一千盾牌，都是勇士的藤牌。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy neck is like the tower of David builded for an armoury, whereon there hang a thousand bucklers, all shields of mighty men.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的两乳好像百合花中吃草的一对小鹿，就是母鹿双生的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy two breasts are like two young roes that are twins, which feed among the lilies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我要往没药山和乳香冈去，直等到天起凉风，日影飞去的时候回来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Until the day break, and the shadows flee away, I will get me to the mountain of myrrh, and to the hill of frankincense.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的佳偶，你全然美丽，毫无瑕疵。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou art all fair, my love; there is no spot in thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的新妇，求你与我一同离开黎巴嫩，与我一同离开黎巴嫩。从亚玛拿顶，从示尼珥与黑门顶，从有狮子的洞，从有豹子的山往下观看。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Come with me from Lebanon, my spouse, with me from Lebanon: look from the top of Amana, from the top of Shenir and Hermon, from the lions' dens, from the mountains of the leopards.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我妹子，我新妇，你夺了我的心。你用眼一看，用你项上的一条金链，夺了我的心。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou hast ravished my heart, my sister, my spouse; thou hast ravished my heart with one of thine eyes, with one chain of thy neck.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我妹子，我新妇，你的爱情何其美。你的爱情比酒更美。你膏油的香气胜过一切香品。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>How fair is thy love, my sister, my spouse! how much better is thy love than wine! and the smell of thine ointments than all spices!</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我新妇，你的嘴唇滴蜜。好像蜂房滴蜜。你的舌下有蜜，有奶。你衣服的香气如黎巴嫩的香气。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy lips, O my spouse, drop as the honeycomb: honey and milk are under thy tongue; and the smell of thy garments is like the smell of Lebanon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我妹子，我新妇，乃是关锁的园，禁闭的井，封闭的泉源。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A garden inclosed is my sister, my spouse; a spring shut up, a fountain sealed.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你园内所种的结了石榴，有佳美的果子，并凤仙花与哪哒树。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy plants are an orchard of pomegranates, with pleasant fruits; camphire, with spikenard,</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有哪哒和番红花，菖蒲和桂树，并各样乳香木，没药，沉香，与一切上等的果品。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Spikenard and saffron; calamus and cinnamon, with all trees of frankincense; myrrh and aloes, with all the chief spices:</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你是园中的泉，活水的井，从黎巴嫩流下来的溪水。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A fountain of gardens, a well of living waters, and streams from Lebanon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>北风阿，兴起。南风阿，吹来。吹在我的园内，使其中的香气发出来。愿我的良人进入自己园里，吃他佳美的果子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Awake, O north wind; and come, thou south; blow upon my garden, that the spices thereof may flow out. Let my beloved come into his garden, and eat his pleasant fruits.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
