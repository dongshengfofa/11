<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 雅歌(Song of Songs) 第5章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我妹子，我新妇，我进了我的园中，采了我的没药和香料，吃了我的蜜房和蜂蜜。喝了我的酒和奶。我的朋友们，请吃，我所亲爱的，请喝，且多多的喝。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I am come into my garden, my sister, my spouse: I have gathered my myrrh with my spice; I have eaten my honeycomb with my honey; I have drunk my wine with my milk: eat, O friends; drink, yea, drink abundantly, O beloved.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我身睡卧，我心却醒。这是我良人的声音。他敲门，说，我的妹子，我的佳偶，我的鸽子，我的完全人，求你给我开门，因我的头满了露水，我的头发被夜露滴湿。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I sleep, but my heart waketh: it is the voice of my beloved that knocketh, saying, Open to me, my sister, my love, my dove, my undefiled: for my head is filled with dew, and my locks with the drops of the night.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我回答说，我脱了衣裳，怎能再穿上呢。我洗了脚，怎能再玷污呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have put off my coat; how shall I put it on? I have washed my feet; how shall I defile them?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的良人从门孔里伸进手来，我便因他动了心。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My beloved put in his hand by the hole of the door, and my bowels were moved for him.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我起来，要给我良人开门。我的两手滴下没药，我的指头有没药汁滴在门闩上。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I rose up to open to my beloved; and my hands dropped with myrrh, and my fingers with sweet smelling myrrh, upon the handles of the lock.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我给我的良人开了门。我的良人却已转身走了。他说话的时候，我神不守舍。我寻梢他，竟寻不见。我呼叫他，他却不回答。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I opened to my beloved; but my beloved had withdrawn himself, and was gone: my soul failed when he spake: I sought him, but I could not find him; I called him, but he gave me no answer.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>城中巡逻看守的人遇见我，打了我，伤了我。看守城墙的人夺去我的披肩。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The watchmen that went about the city found me, they smote me, they wounded me; the keepers of the walls took away my veil from me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶路撒冷的众女子阿，我嘱咐你们。若遇见我的良人，要告诉他，我因思爱成病。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I charge you, O daughters of Jerusalem, if ye find my beloved, that ye tell him, that I am sick of love.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你这女子中极美丽的，你的良人，比别人的良人有何强处。你的良人，比别人的良人有何强处，你就这样嘱咐我们。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>What is thy beloved more than another beloved, O thou fairest among women? what is thy beloved more than another beloved, that thou dost so charge us?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的良人，白而且红，超乎万人之上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My beloved is white and ruddy, the chiefest among ten thousand.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的头像至精的金子。他的头发厚密累垂，黑如乌鸦。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His head is as the most fine gold, his locks are bushy, and black as a raven.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的眼如溪水旁的鸽子眼，用奶洗净，安得合式。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His eyes are as the eyes of doves by the rivers of waters, washed with milk, and fitly set.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的两腮如香花畦，如香草台。他的嘴唇像百合花，且滴下没药汁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His cheeks are as a bed of spices, as sweet flowers: his lips like lilies, dropping sweet smelling myrrh.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His legs are as pillars of marble, set upon sockets of fine gold: his countenance is as Lebanon, excellent as the cedars.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
