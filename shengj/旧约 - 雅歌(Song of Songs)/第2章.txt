<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 雅歌(Song of Songs) 第2章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我是沙仑的玫瑰花（或作水仙花），是谷中的百合花。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I am the rose of Sharon, and the lily of the valleys.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的佳偶在女子中，好像百合花在荆棘内。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As the lily among thorns, so is my love among the daughters.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的良人在男子中，如同苹果树在树林中。我欢欢喜喜坐在他的荫下，尝他果子的滋味，觉得甘甜。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As the apple tree among the trees of the wood, so is my beloved among the sons. I sat down under his shadow with great delight, and his fruit was sweet to my taste.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他带我入筵宴所，以爱为旗在我以上。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>He brought me to the banqueting house, and his banner over me was love.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你们给我葡萄乾增补我力，给我苹果畅快我心，因我思爱成病。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Stay me with flagons, comfort me with apples: for I am sick of love.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>他的左手在我头下，他的右手将我抱住。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>His left hand is under my head, and his right hand doth embrace me.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶路撒冷的众女子阿，我指着羚羊或田野的母鹿，嘱咐你们，不要惊动，不要叫醒我所亲爱的，等他自己情愿（不要叫醒云云或作不要激动爱情等它自发）。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I charge you, O ye daughters of Jerusalem, by the roes, and by the hinds of the field, that ye stir not up, nor awake my love, till he please.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>听阿，是我良人的声音。看哪，他蹿山越岭而来。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The voice of my beloved! behold, he cometh leaping upon the mountains, skipping upon the hills.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的良人好像羚羊，或像小鹿。他站在我们墙壁后，从窗户往里观看，从窗棂往里窥探。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My beloved is like a roe or a young hart: behold, he standeth behind our wall, he looketh forth at the windows, shewing himself through the lattice.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我良人对我说，我的佳偶，我的美人，起来，与我同去。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My beloved spake, and said unto me, Rise up, my love, my fair one, and come away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>因为冬天已往。雨水止住过去了。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>For, lo, the winter is past, the rain is over and gone;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>地上百花开放。百鸟呜叫的时候（或作修理葡萄树的时候）已经来到，斑鸠的声音在我们境内也听见了。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The flowers appear on the earth; the time of the singing of birds is come, and the voice of the turtle is heard in our land;</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>无花果树的果子渐渐成熟，葡萄树开花放香。我的佳偶，我的美人，起来，与我同去。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The fig tree putteth forth her green figs, and the vines with the tender grape give a good smell. Arise, my love, my fair one, and come away.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的鸽子阿，你在磐石穴中，在陡岩的隐密处。求你容我得见你的面貌，得听你的声音。因为你的声音柔和，你的面貌秀美。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>O my dove, that art in the clefts of the rock, in the secret places of the stairs, let me see thy countenance, let me hear thy voice; for sweet is thy voice, and thy countenance is comely.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第15段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>要给我们擒拿狐狸，就是毁坏葡萄园的小狐狸。因为我们的葡萄正在开花。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Take us the foxes, the little foxes, that spoil the vines: for our vines have tender grapes.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第16段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>良人属我，我也属他。他在百合花中牧放群羊<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My beloved is mine, and I am his: he feedeth among the lilies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第17段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的良人哪，求你等到天起凉风，日影飞去的时候，你要转回，好像羚羊，或像小鹿在比特山上。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Until the day break, and the shadows flee away, turn, my beloved, and be thou like a roe or a young hart upon the mountains of Bether.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
