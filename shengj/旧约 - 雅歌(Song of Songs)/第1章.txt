<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 雅歌(Song of Songs) 第1章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>所罗门的歌，是歌中的雅歌。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>The song of songs, which is Solomon's.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿他用口与我亲嘴。因你的爱情比酒更美。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Let him kiss me with the kisses of his mouth: for thy love is better than wine.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的膏油馨香。你的名如同倒出来的香膏，所以众童女都爱你。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Because of the savour of thy good ointments thy name is as ointment poured forth, therefore do the virgins love thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>愿你吸引我，我们就快跑跟随你。王带我进了内室，我们必因你欢喜快乐。我们要称赞你的爱情，胜似称赞美酒。他们爱你是理所当然的。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Draw me, we will run after thee: the king hath brought me into his chambers: we will be glad and rejoice in thee, we will remember thy love more than wine: the upright love thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>耶路撒冷的众女子阿，我虽然黑，却是秀美，如同基达的帐棚，好像所罗门的幔子。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I am black, but comely, O ye daughters of Jerusalem, as the tents of Kedar, as the curtains of Solomon.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不要因日头把我晒黑了，就轻看我。我同母的弟兄向我发怒，他们使我看守葡萄园，我自己的葡萄园却没有看守。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Look not upon me, because I am black, because the sun hath looked upon me: my mother's children were angry with me; they made me the keeper of the vineyards; but mine own vineyard have I not kept.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我心所爱的阿，求你告诉我，你在何处牧羊，晌午在何处使羊歇卧。我何必在你同伴的羊群旁边，好像蒙着脸的人呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Tell me, O thou whom my soul loveth, where thou feedest, where thou makest thy flock to rest at noon: for why should I be as one that turneth aside by the flocks of thy companions?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你这女子中极美丽的，你若不知道，只管跟随羊群的脚踪去，把你的山羊羔牧放在牧人帐棚的旁边。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>If thou know not, O thou fairest among women, go thy way forth by the footsteps of the flock, and feed thy kids beside the shepherds' tents.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的佳偶，我将你比法老车上套的骏马。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I have compared thee, O my love, to a company of horses in Pharaoh's chariots.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的两腮因发辫而秀美，你的颈项因珠串而华丽。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy cheeks are comely with rows of jewels, thy neck with chains of gold.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我们要为你编上金辫，镶上银钉。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>We will make thee borders of gold with studs of silver.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>王正坐席的时候，我的哪哒香膏发出香味。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>While the king sitteth at his table, my spikenard sendeth forth the smell thereof.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我以我的良人为一袋没药，常在我怀中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>A bundle of myrrh is my well-beloved unto me; he shall lie all night betwixt my breasts.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第14段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我以我的良人为一棵凤仙花，在隐基底葡萄园中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My beloved is unto me as a cluster of camphire in the vineyards of Engedi.</td>
	</tr>
</table>

<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的佳偶，你甚美丽，你甚美丽，你的眼好像鸽子眼。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

	</tr>
</table>

	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>

</table>
