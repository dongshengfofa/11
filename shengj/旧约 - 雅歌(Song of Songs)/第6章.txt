<p style="color:#F00;"><strong>旧约 - 雅歌(Song of Songs) 第6章</strong></p>


<p>第1段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你这女子中极美丽的，你的良人往何处去了。你的良人转向何处去了，我们好与你同去寻梢他。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Whither is thy beloved gone, O thou fairest among women? whither is thy beloved turned aside? that we may seek him with thee.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第2段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的良人下入自己园中，到香花畦，在园内牧放群羊，采百合花。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My beloved is gone down into his garden, to the beds of spices, to feed in the gardens, and to gather lilies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第3段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我属我的良人，我的良人也属我。他在百合花中牧放群羊。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I am my beloved's, and my beloved is mine: he feedeth among the lilies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第4段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的佳偶阿，你美丽如得撒，秀美如耶路撒冷，威武如展开旌旗的军队。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thou art beautiful, O my love, as Tirzah, comely as Jerusalem, terrible as an army with banners.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第5段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>求你掉转眼目不看我，因你的眼目使我惊乱。你的头发如同山羊群卧在基列山旁。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Turn away thine eyes from me, for they have overcome me: thy hair is as a flock of goats that appear from Gilead.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第6段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的牙齿如一群母羊，洗净上来。个个都有双生，没有一只丧掉子的。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Thy teeth are as a flock of sheep which go up from the washing, whereof every one beareth twins, and there is not one barren among them.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第7段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>你的两太阳在帕子内，如同一块石榴。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>As a piece of a pomegranate are thy temples within thy locks.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第8段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>有六十王后八十妃嫔，并有无数的童女。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>There are threescore queens, and fourscore concubines, and virgins without number.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第9段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我的鸽子，我的完全人，只有这一个是她母亲独生的。是生养她者所宝爱的。众女子见了，就称她有福。王后妃嫔见了，也赞美她。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>My dove, my undefiled is but one; she is the only one of her mother, she is the choice one of her that bare her. The daughters saw her, and blessed her; yea, the queens and the concubines, and they praised her.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第10段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>那向外观看，如晨光发现，美丽如月亮，皎洁如日头，威武如展开旌旗军队的是谁呢。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Who is she that looketh forth as the morning, fair as the moon, clear as the sun, and terrible as an army with banners?</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第11段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>我下入核桃园，要看谷中青绿的植物，要看葡萄发芽没有，石榴开花没有。<a></a></td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>I went down into the garden of nuts to see the fruits of the valley, and to see whether the vine flourished, and the pomegranates budded.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第12段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>不知不觉，我的心将我安置在我尊长的车中。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Or ever I was aware, my soul made me like the chariots of Amminadib.</td>
	</tr>
</table>


<p>第13段</p>
<table border="1" width="98%" id="table1" style="border-collapse: collapse">
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">中文：</td>
		<td>回来，回来，书拉密女。你回来，你回来，使我们得观看你。你们为何要观看书拉密女，像观看玛哈念跳舞的呢。</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td width="73" align="center">英文：</td>
		<td>Return, return, O Shulamite; return, return, that we may look upon thee. What will ye see in the Shulamite? As it were the company of two armies.</td>
	</tr>
</table>
